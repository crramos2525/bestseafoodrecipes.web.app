##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Shrimp Tempura:

1. Take 5 shrimps
1. Get 4 tablespoon flour
1. Get 1 tablespoon starch
1. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1. Prepare 70 ml water
1. Prepare 1 cube ice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Japanese Shrimp Tempura:

1. Remove outside skin. Cut inside and make shrimp straight.
1. Make Tempura mixture putting one cube ice.
1. Put shrimp in Tempura mixture and immediately fry in 2cm deep oil. Sprinkle Tempura mixture over shrimp.
1. Fry both side
1. Garnish with green onion.
