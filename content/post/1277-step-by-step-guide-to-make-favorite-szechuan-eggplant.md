##### Instructions to make Szechuan Eggplant:

1. Roast eggplants in fire until skin can be peeled off. Let it cool before peeling off the skin. Then, start chopping and mincing the chili and the garlic.
1. Sautee garlic and chili until golden brown. Add in pepper.
1. Add oyster sauce, light soy and sugar. Let it boil.
1. Either add the eggplant to the sauce and cook it a bit more OR pour the sauce into the eggplant, then garnish and serve!
