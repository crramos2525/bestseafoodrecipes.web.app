##### Steps to make Goan prawns curry:

1. Grind all the ingredients given to make fine masala paste
1. Remove the shell and Devain the prawns. Wash and keep aside.
1. In a pan heat oil, add grated ginger then add onion.fry till they turn brown.
1. Add tomatoes, fry until they get soft and mushy.
1. Add masala paste and salt.
1. Fry masala for 5-7 minutes.
1. Add prawns. Cover and cook for few minutes.
1. Add water if required.Cook till prawns get cooked. It will take less than 10 minutes.
1. Add curry leaves, switch off the flame. Let it rest for atleast 5 minutes.
1. Serve with steamed rice.
