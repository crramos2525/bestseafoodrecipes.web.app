##### The ingredients needed to make Kejibeling Salad (RUJAK BELING):

1. Take 8-10 leaves kecibeling / kejibeling
1. Make ready 2 blocks tofu, diced
1. Get 1 cucumber, seeded and diced
1. Get 100 gr bean sprouts, remove the roots and boiled
1. Get  SAMBAL SAUCE
1. Take 10 cayenne peppers
1. Get 3 red chillis
1. Get 1 garlic
1. Take 1 tsp shrimp paste
1. Prepare 200 gr brown sugar
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp tamarind
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Prepare 2 Tbsp roasted peanuts, roughly mash for sprinkling




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Kejibeling Salad (RUJAK BELING):

1. Clean the kecibeling leaves. They can be boiled first, or left fresh, thinly sliced.
1. Fry tofu along with the garlic until browned and cooked well. Remove and drain.
1. SAMBAL SAUCE: Mash cayenne chilis, red chilis, garlic, shrimp paste, salt, sugar and tamarind until smooth. If too thick, you can add a little water.
1. Put kecibeling leaves and sprouts at the base of the plate. Pile the tofu and cucumber on top.
1. Top with the sauce and sprinkle roasted peanuts. Ready to eat!
