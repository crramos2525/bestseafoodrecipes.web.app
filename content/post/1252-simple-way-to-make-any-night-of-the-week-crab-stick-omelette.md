##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stick Omelette:

1. Prepare  salt and pepper
1. Take 1/2 tbs butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Crab Stick Omelette:

1. Heat the butter on a pan. Pour the eggs mixture.
