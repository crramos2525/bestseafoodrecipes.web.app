##### The ingredients needed to make Bonito Flake Rice Ball Bento:

1. Take 360 ml Freshly cooked white rice
1. Get 1 tsp Salted kombu &#34;tea&#34; (not kombucha)
1. Take 2 tsp Soy sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp Toasted white sesame seeds
1. Take 3 grams Bonito flakes
1. Make ready 4 sheets Nori seaweed




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Bonito Flake Rice Ball Bento:

1. Quickly mix the freshly cooked rice with kombu tea, toasted white sesame seeds, and bonito flakes.
1. Divide into 8 portions, wrap with plastic wrap, then make triangle-shaped rice balls. If you squeeze too much, you will mash the rice, so it&#39;s ok if the rice doesn&#39;t stick together.
1. Cut the nori seaweed sheets into 2 pieces to make triangles.
1. Put a desiccant and the nori seaweed in a ziplock bag, and pack the rice balls separately. The bento is done!
1. Wrap the rice balls with nori seaweed right before eating. You can enjoy the crunchy nori seaweed.
