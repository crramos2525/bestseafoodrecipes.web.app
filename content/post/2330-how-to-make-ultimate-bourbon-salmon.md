##### The ingredients needed to make Bourbon Salmon:

1. Make ready 4 fresh salmon fillets
1. Get 3 tbsp brown sugar
1. Prepare 3 tbsp bourbon
1. Take 2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 tbsp grated peeled fresh ginger
1. Make ready 1 tbsp fresh lime juice
1. Prepare 3 garlic cloves, minced
1. Get 1/4 tsp black ground pepper
1. Make ready 1 cooking spray




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Bourbon Salmon:

1. Combine all ingredients in a large zip-top plastic bag. Add fish to bag; seal.
1. Marinate in refrigerator 1 1/2 hours.
1. Preheat oven 350°
1. grease baking pan ( I usually use olive oil)
1. bake fish until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork or until desired degree of doneness.
1. I usually serve over Jasmine Rice with a side of asparagus.
1. Enjoy ❤
