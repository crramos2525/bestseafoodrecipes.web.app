##### The ingredients needed to make Asian Flavored Ribs:

1. Prepare 1 pound beef short ribs about 500g
1. Get 1/3 cup brown sugar

1. Take 3 cloves garlic minced
1. Take 1 tbsp fresh grated ginger
1. Take 1 tbsp scallions

##### Steps to make Asian Flavored Ribs:

1. Put the short ribs in a large Ziploc bag.
1. In a large bowl, mix all other ingredients together, except scallions. Pour the mixture into the Ziploc bag and mix it with the ribs. Marinate the ribs for about 2 hours.
1. Line the fryer basket with a sheet of lightly greased aluminum foil.
1. Place the ribs inside the fryer basket, without stacking. Air fry at 380F (190C) for 8-10 minutes, flip once in the middle until the surface is slightly caramelized.
1. Sprinkle some scallions to garnish.
