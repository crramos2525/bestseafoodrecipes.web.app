##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Atsuage with Bonito Flakes &amp; Mayo:

1. Prepare 3 grams Bonito flakes
1. Get 2 blocks Atsuage
1. Make ready 100 grams Spinach
1. Get 5 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Prepare 2 tsp Soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 Shichimi spice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fried Atsuage with Bonito Flakes &amp; Mayo:

1. Remove the root from the spinach and cut into 1 cm lengths. Parboil in salted water and then thoroughly wring out the excess moisture. Put the spinach, bonito flakes, mayonnaise, and soy sauce into a bowl and mix well.
1. Toast the atsuage in the toaster until crunchy and then cut into bite-sized pieces. You could also toast them in a frying pan with no oil.
1. Add the toasted atsuage to the bowl from Step 1 and roughly combine. Serve on a dish and season with shichimi spice to finish.
