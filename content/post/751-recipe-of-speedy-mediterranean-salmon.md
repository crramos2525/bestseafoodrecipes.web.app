##### Steps to make Mediterranean Salmon:

1. Preheat oven to broil.
1. Pat salmon fillets dry.
1. Add salt and pepper.
1. In a large skillet, on medium high heat, pour in a little olive oil, coating the pans bottom. Then add in the salmon fillets and cook until the bottom of the salmon has turned light pink (about 1/4 inch).
1. Top salmon fillets with tomatoes, onions, and black olives. Season with salt and dry basil. Then sprinkle on olive oil, lemon juice, &amp; balsamic vinegar.
1. Place salmon in the oven and broil for 12-13 minutes.
