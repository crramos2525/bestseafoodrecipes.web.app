##### Steps to make Jambalaya:

1. Prepare all your ingredients before starting, it will help a lot!
1. Heat up butter in a deep pan and fry the chicken until it’s slightly browned. Put aside when done.
1. Repeat with chorizo. Put aside when done.
1. Next add flour to the fat in the pan and mix well on low heat. Add cold water if needed.
1. Add meat, onions, pepper, garlic, chopped tomatoes and thyme to the mixture. Fry for 10 minutes.
1. Add rice and fry for 1 minute. Then add stock. Leave simmering on medium heat for 20-30 minutes until most of the liquid is gone.
1. Add prawns and Tabasco sauce. Heat for 5 minutes.
1. Take off the heat and cover for 10 minutes. Serve with sprinkle of parsley on top.
