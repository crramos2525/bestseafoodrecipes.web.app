##### The ingredients needed to make Very Easy Crabstick Salad:

1. Prepare 1 packet Imitation crab sticks
1. Get 2 Cucumber
1. Make ready 5 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Get 1 Salt and pepper
1. Make ready 1 Salt (to rub the cucumbers)

##### Instructions to make Very Easy Crabstick Salad:

1. Prepare the ingredients.
1. Slice the cucumbers thinly. Sprinkle with salt and massage. When they start to release moisture, put into a bowl of water, drain and squeeze out tightly. Shred the crabsticks.
1. Add all the other ingredients and mix.
