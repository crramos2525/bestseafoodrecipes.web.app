##### The ingredients needed to make Squid in Garlic and Olive Oil:

1. Make ready 250 g Squid
1. Take 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
1. Prepare 8 cloves Garlic
1. Prepare 2 pcs. Bay leaf
1. Make ready 1/4 Tsp pepper
1. Make ready 1 pc. Chili finger, sliced
1. Take 1 Tbsp Liquid Seasoning
1. Take 1 Tbsp Calamansi

1. Clean squid, remove transparent backbone, squid ink, eyes and mouth
1. Heat oil in a pan, saute garlic
1. Add chili
1. Put squid, saute
