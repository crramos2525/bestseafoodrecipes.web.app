##### Steps to make Fried Rice with Salmon Flakes:

1. Finely chop the green onion. Add some salt (not listed) to the egg and beat it.
1. Heat up the sesame oil in a frying pan and pour in the beaten egg from Step 1. Add the rice immediately and cook over high heat.
1. Mix and stir-fry the egg and rice, then add the salmon flakes and the green onion. Continue cooking over medium heat.
1. Once Step 3 has been lightly cooked, add the white sesame seeds and the soy sauce. Once the flavors have blended, add salt and pepper to taste. Cook over low heat and serve.
