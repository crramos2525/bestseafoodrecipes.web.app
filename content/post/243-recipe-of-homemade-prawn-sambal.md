##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Sambal:

1. Make ready  Prawn (skinned &amp; cleaned) 500grm
1. Make ready  Potato 2 - diced &amp; fried
1. Make ready  Garlic Ginger paste
1. Prepare  Onion 2 - sliced
1. Prepare  Tomato 1 - sliced
1. Get  Dry chilli - blended
1. Prepare 3 tbspn Oil
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Make ready to taste Sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Prawn Sambal:

1. Add oil in a wok/pan. Add onion. Fry until it turn glassy. Add garlic ginger paste. Cook until oil breaks. Add dry chilli paste. Cook until oil breaks.
1. Add prawns. Mix well. Cook for 2 minutes. Add salt, sugar tomato and fried potato. Stir and mix well. Cook until the gravy thickens (5 to 15min)
1. This dish can be served with rice.
