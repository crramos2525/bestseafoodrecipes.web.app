##### Instructions to make Shrimp Ceviche:

1. Place chopped onion in a colander, and rinse with cold water. Drain
1. Bring 6 cups water and 1/2 cup juice to a boil in a Dutch oven. Add shrimp; cook 3 minutes or until done. Drain and rinse with cold water; peel shrimp.
1. Combine shrimp and 1/2 cup juice in a large bowl; cover and chill 1 hour. Stir in onion, cucumber, and remaining ingredients.
1. Dice avocado into squares using the skin as a cup to dice
1. Add diced Avocado and  Serve immediately or chilled.
