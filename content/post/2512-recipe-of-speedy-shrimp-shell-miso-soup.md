##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp-shell Miso Soup:

1. Prepare 6 to 8 heads Shrimp heads (botan shrimp, amaebi, etc.)
1. Make ready 1 Green onions
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Miso
1. Get 1 tbsp Sake
1. Make ready 400 ml Dashi stock




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Shrimp-shell Miso Soup:

1. Sashimi-ready shrimp is fine. In our house we usually eat the bodies as sashimi, and use the heads for this miso soup.
1. Put the dashi stock in a pan and heat. Put in the shrimp heads and sake.
1. Skip off the scum carefully. Dissolve in the miso. Sprinkle with chopped green onions just before serving. Done!
