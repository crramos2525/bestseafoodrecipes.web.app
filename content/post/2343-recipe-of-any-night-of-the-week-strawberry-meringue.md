##### The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Meringue:

1. Make ready 150 g strawberries
1. Take 200 ml double cream
1. Make ready As needed powdered sugar (optional)
1. Make ready 4 ready made meringue shells or make your own
1. Get 1 chocolate flake




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Strawberry Meringue:

1. Prepare the strawberries and cut them in half or quarters depending on how big the strawberries are.
1. In a bowl whip the double cream until it becomes stiff.
1. Add the whipped cream to the top of the shells.
1. Using your fingers break the chocolate into flakes. And sprinkle over the cream.
1. Then layer them with the strawberries.
1. Sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired.
