##### The ingredients needed to make Squid in Garlic and Olive Oil:

1. Prepare 250 g Squid
1. Get 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
1. Prepare 8 cloves Garlic
1. Take 2 pcs. Bay leaf
1. Get 1/4 Tsp pepper
1. Prepare 1 pc. Chili finger, sliced
1. Make ready 1 Tbsp Liquid Seasoning
1. Make ready 1 Tbsp Calamansi




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Squid in Garlic and Olive Oil:

1. Clean squid, remove transparent backbone, squid ink, eyes and mouth
1. Heat oil in a pan, saute garlic
1. Add chili
1. Put squid, saute
1. Add bay leaf and pepper
1. Add liquid seasoning
1. Put Calamansi and turn off the heat. Do not overcook the squid
