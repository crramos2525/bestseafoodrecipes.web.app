##### Instructions to make Hennessy Honey Salmon:

1. Combine your dry ingredients.
1. Heat honey to a boil in a small sauce pan. Once honey bubbles add Hennessy, minced garlic and dry ingredients and simmer for 5 minutes
1. Cool sauce for 10 to 15 minutes or until it thickens
1. Brush olive oil on salmon and season portions with black pepper and pink salt
1. Turn oven on broil and cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes depending on thickness of portions
