##### Instructions to make Blackened Salmon with Greek Yogurt:

1. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium- high heat, for about 3 minutes. Brush with the oil.
1. Place the fish, skin side up, in the skillet. Cook until blackened and crisp, 3 minutes per side.
