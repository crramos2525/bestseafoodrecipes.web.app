##### Steps to make Bamboo beef:

1. Slice the meat into thin strips, put in a bowl and mix well with baking soda. Let marinate for 30 minutes and rinse well in cold water.
1. Put the beef back in the bowl with the rest of the ingredients for the marinade and leave for at least 20 minutes - over night is the best!
1. On medium-high heat, fry the meat until it gets a bit of colour and then add the hoisin sauce and onion. Fry for a bit longer and then add the rest of the ingredients. Turn the heat down and let simmer for 8 minutes.
1. To thicken the sauce, mix 1 tbsp corn starch with 50 ml water and add to the sauce while stiring.
1. Serve with rice and enjoy!
