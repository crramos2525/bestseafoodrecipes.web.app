##### The ingredients needed to make Addictive Cajun Boiled Peanuts:

1. Prepare 1 bag of raw peanuts
1. Get 1 small crockpot
1. Make ready 1 water (as needed)
1. Take 3 oz sea salt
1. Make ready 1 4 oz. bottle of liquid crab boil
1. Get 2 handful Tony Chachares creole seasoning
1. Take 2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
1. Take 1 handful crushed red pepper
1. Make ready 3 bay leaf
1. Prepare 1 clove garlic (chopped)
1. Make ready  onion powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Addictive Cajun Boiled Peanuts:

1. Pour bag of peanuts into the crock pot.
1. Fill crock pot up with water, leaving about 2 inches to the top for stirring purposes.
1. Set crock pot on high. Once water is boiling,  add spices.
1. Let peanuts cook in crockpot for 24 hours, stirring occasionally.
1. Once 24 hours is up, check doneness and spiciness of peanut.  If not tender enough, cook an additional 3 hours at a time until done to your liking.  If too spicy, dump half the liquid and refill with water. Stir and cook until boiling.
