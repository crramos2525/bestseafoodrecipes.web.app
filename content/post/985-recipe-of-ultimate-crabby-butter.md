##### The ingredients needed to make Crabby Butter:

1. Get 1 stick butter, at room temperature
1. Take 16 oz cream cheese
1. Take 6 oz canned crab meat (do NOT drain)
1. Prepare 8 oz artificial crab meat, lump style
1. Make ready 3 green onions, chopped
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1. Take 1 tsp garlic powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Crabby Butter:

1. In your food processor with chopping blade, blend the butter and cream cheese until smooth.
1. Add the remaining ingredients and pulse until blended. If necessary, scrape down the sides of the food processor bowl with a rubber spatula so that all ingredients are well blended.
1. Chill. Serve as a spread on crackers or toasted mini bread rounds.
