##### Steps to make Japanese Nikumaki Onigiri(Meat wrapped rice balls):

1. Wrap the rice balls in sliced meat. Coat with flour.
1. Place a little bit of oil in a frying pan. Start frying from sealed side down until they becomes crispy brown. If the bottoms are cooked, turn them over. Keep rolling and cook all sides.
