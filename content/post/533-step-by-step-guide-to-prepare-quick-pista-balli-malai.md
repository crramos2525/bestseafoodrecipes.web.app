##### The ingredients needed to make Pista Balli malai:

1. Make ready 1/2 litre milk
1. Make ready 1 cup sugar
1. Take 3 tbsp agar agar powder
1. Make ready 1 small tin evaporated milk
1. Get 1/4 tsp Mawa essence
1. Take Few Pista
1. Take Few green colour
1. Get 1/4 cup water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pista Balli malai:

1. Heat milk in a pan,add sugar,essence,colour and evaporated milk,and stir well till sugar dissolves.
1. Now mix agar agar powder with water and add into milk.and keep mixing.when all things get well mixed. Strain the mixture from strainer.
1. Add the mixture in a square shape in or any flat hollow dish. Put cut pistas on top and let to set in the fridge for two hours. Cut into pieces and serve.drizzel some rabdi on it and yummy pista Balli malai is ready.
