##### The ingredients needed to make Prawns, Pineapple and Spring Green Veg:

1. Take 400 Grams Frozen Large Prawns
1. Take 200 Grams Spring Greens
1. Prepare 100 Grams Spring Onions
1. Take 1 large Green Sweet Pepper
1. Get 400 Grams Tin Pineapple in Juice
1. Make ready 100 Grams Garlic Butter
1. Take 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1. Take 1 Large Onion
1. Take Handful Fresh Chives




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Prawns, Pineapple and Spring Green Veg:

1. Put Butter and Oil in a large Wok or saucepan. Add the chopped Onion, chopped Chives and Frozen Prawns. Cook gently until the Prawns start to go pink.
1. Add the Pineapple and Juice, cook for about 5 minutes, then add the chopped Spring Onions, chopped Spring Greens, and chopped Green Pepper. Continue on a slightly higher heat for about 5-10 minutes until everything is cooked and soft. Serve and enjoy!
