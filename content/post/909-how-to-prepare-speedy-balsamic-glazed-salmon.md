##### The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Glazed Salmon:

1. Take 4 salmon fillets
1. Take 2 clove garlic crushed
1. Take 1 tbsp white wine
1. Get 1 tbsp honey
1. Get 85 ml balsamic vinegar
1. Prepare 2 tbsp wholegrain mustard
1. Take 1 tsp oregano




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Balsamic Glazed Salmon:

1. fry garlic and add wine, honey, balsamic vinegar and mustard, season well
1. simmer for 3 mins until thickens then add oregano
1. brush salmon with glaze and grill 10-15 mins adding more glaze occasionally
