##### Steps to make Oyster Minced Chicken Congee:

1. Boil a pot of hot water, add oysters and cook for 30 seconds，Set it aside
1. Add olive oil into a soup pot
1. Add garlic, ginger and onion into the pot and cook for 5 minutes
1. Add minced chicken and stir until it‘s cooked
1. Add cooked rice and water to a boil and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
1. Once the rice has smoothed out and the congee is the texture you&#39;the like, add oysters and celery, bring it to boil again and cook for 5 minutes
