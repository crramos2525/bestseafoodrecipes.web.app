##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Sriracha Tuna Melt:

1. Prepare 2 slice Multigrain Bread
1. Prepare 1 can tuna
1. Get 1 tsp mayonnaise
1. Make ready 1 butter
1. Prepare 1 Handfull shredded cheese
