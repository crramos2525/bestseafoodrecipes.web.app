##### Instructions to make Chipirones a la Romana:

1. Keep the tentacles to one side and slice the heads into small ringlets. You will need to remove the beak from the tentacles as this is hard and not really edible.
1. In a ziplock bag, put in the flour, salt and smoked paprika and shake well
