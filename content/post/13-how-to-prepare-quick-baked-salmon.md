##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon:

1. Make ready 2 clove garlic
1. Prepare 6 tbsp light olive oil
1. Take 1 tsp dried basil
1. Get 1 tsp salt/pepper
1. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
1. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1. Get 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Baked Salmon:

1. In medium glass bowl, prepare marinade by mixing: garlic,olive oil, basil, salt/pepper, lemon juice, and parsley.
1. Place salmon fillets in medium baking dish, and cover with marinade. Marinated in the fridge for 1 hour, turning occasionally.
1. Preheat oven to 375°F
1. Place fillets in aluminum foil, cover with marinade, and seal. Place salmon in glass dish, and bake 35-45 minutes, until easily flakes with fork.
