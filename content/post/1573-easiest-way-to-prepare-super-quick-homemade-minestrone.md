##### Instructions to make Minestrone:

1. Rinse kidney beans with cold water.
1. Chop celery, carrots, and onion.
1. Combine chicken broth, kidney beans, carrots, celery, onion, boullion, basil, oregano, and pepper into a large sauce pan. Bring to a boil.
1. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 15 minutes.
1. Add in tomatoes, pasta, and garlic. Personally, I like adding in the juices with the tomatoes for extra flavor.
1. Return to boil, then reduce heat to low again. Cover, let simmer for 5-10 more minutes. Remove from heat and add in parmesan, then serve.
1. Enjoy!
