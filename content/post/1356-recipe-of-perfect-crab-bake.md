1. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese
1. Take 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1. Prepare 1 tbsp milk
1. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
1. Get 1 1/2 cup cheddar cheese

##### Steps to make Crab Bake:
