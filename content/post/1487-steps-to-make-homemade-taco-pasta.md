##### The ingredients needed to make Taco Pasta:

1. Make ready 1 lb ground beef
1. Get 1 medium onion, chopped
1. Take 1/2 packages taco seasoning
1. Take 1/2 tsp garlic salt
1. Take 1 can diced tomatoes drained
1. Get  salt
1. Get 1 cup sour cream
1. Take 8 oz medium shell pasta uncooked




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Taco Pasta:

1. Dice onions and cook fully with ground beef in a skillet.
1. In a separate pot bring water to a boil and fully cook pasta drain and set aside.
1. Add tomatoes, garlic salt, taco seasoning and sour cream to ground beef and onions.
1. Mix with pasta stir until combined serve with cheese and green onions if desired.
