##### The ingredients needed to make Bok choy in garlic &amp; oyster sauce:

1. Get 3 tbsp oyster sauce

1. Make ready Pinch pepper

##### Instructions to make Bok choy in garlic &amp; oyster sauce:

1. Combine water, sugar, oyster sauce in a bowl. Stir well and set aside.
1. Put oil and garlic on a pan. Stir. Turn the heat on until garlic turns brown.
1. Pour mixture of sauce and water to the pan.
1. Bring to a boil.
1. Add bokchoy and cook for about 5 minutes
1. Add pepper and sesame oil
1. Turn off fire when sauce is reduced to half.
1. Transfer to a different container to stop cooking.
1. Serve and enjoy!
