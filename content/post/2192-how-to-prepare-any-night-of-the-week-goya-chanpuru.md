##### The ingredients needed to make Goya Chanpuru:

1. Prepare 1 goya
1. Get 2 eggs
1. Make ready 300 g sliced porks
1. Prepare 1 handful bonito flakes
1. Make ready 200 g tofu
1. Prepare  sesame oil
1. Get  salt and pepper
1. Prepare  soy sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Goya Chanpuru:

1. Slice the pork into 4cm, goya into 1cm, tofu into a bite size
1. Stir-fry the meat with one tsp of sesame oil
1. When the meat is well cooked, add the tofu and goya into the pan and fry together for 3 minutes
1. When the tofu start to get a little charcoal, season with salt and pepper.
1. Put the beaten egg and the bonito flakes into the pan, and pour soy sauce evenly from the edge of the pan.
