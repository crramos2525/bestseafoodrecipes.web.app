##### The ingredients needed to make Cold Crab Salad:

1. Prepare 2 dash onion powder
1. Make ready 3 tbsp fresh cracked pepper
1. Prepare 1 box Keebler Club Crackers

##### Steps to make Cold Crab Salad:
