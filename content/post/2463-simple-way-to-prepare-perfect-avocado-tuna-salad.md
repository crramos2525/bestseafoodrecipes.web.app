##### The ingredients needed to make Avocado Tuna Salad:

1. Take 1 Ripe avocado
1. Take 2 can Tuna, drained and squeezed
1. Get 1 Jalapeño
1. Prepare 1 as needed Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Avocado Tuna Salad:

1. Drain the cans of tuna, squeeze out as much moisture as possible.  Place in a mixing bowl.
1. Half and pit the avocado scoop it out of the skin and add it to the bowl.
1. Using a cheese grater shred the jalapeño into the bowl. For a less spicy version take out the inner membrane and seeds.
1. Salt to taste
1. Combine in the bowl, I used a fork to mash it all together until well combined
1. Use it anywhere you would normally use tuna salad
