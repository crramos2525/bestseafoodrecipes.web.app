##### Steps to make Salmon Chowder:

1. Remove the prawns from the shells.
1. In a large saucepan over low heat, add the oil and allow it to heat for 2 - 3 minutes.
1. Add the prawn shells, onion, thyme, bay leaves, peppercorns, lemon, and tomato, and sweat for 2 - 3 minutes.
1. Add the white wine and water. Simmer for 60 minutes. Strain and reserve. Set aside the seafood stock.
1. Trim fat from the bacon and fry until crispy. Remove and set aside.
1. In a large saucepan over low heat, add the oil and allow it to heat for 2 - 3 minutes.
1. Sweat the onion for up to 10 minutes. Add the celery and sweat for 10 minutes.
1. Add the potatoes and sweat for 10 minutes. Add the bacon.
1. Add the shell stock and cream. Simmer for 20 minutes on medium-low heat.
1. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
1. Add the salmon right before serving. For medium rare salmon, let it cook for 2 minutes. For medium salmon, let it cook for 5 minutes.
