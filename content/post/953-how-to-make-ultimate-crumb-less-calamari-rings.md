##### The ingredients needed to make Crumb-less Calamari Rings:

1. Get 2 Squid Tubes *400 to 500g
1. Make ready  Salt &amp; Pepper
1. Make ready 3-4 tablespoons Plain Flour
1. Get 1 Egg *lightly whisked
1. Make ready  Oil for frying
1. Make ready  Lemon Wedges to serve *optional

##### Instructions to make Crumb-less Calamari Rings:

1. Heat Oil in a heavy-based saucepan to 170째C to 180째C.
1. Clean Squid Tubes, pat dry with paper towel, cut into 1cm-thick rings. Season with Salt and Pepper. Sprinkle with Plain Flour and lightly coat.
1. Dip into whisked Egg, allow excess to run off, then carefully fry for 1 to 2 minutes or until lightly golden and crispy. Take them out and lay them on a rack to drain the excess oil. Serve hot or warm, with Lemon Wedges.
