##### Steps to make Salmon Pie:

1. Fry the red onion and after 3 mins add the garlic. After another 3 mins dice and add the courgettes
1. Boil the carrots for 3 mins. Feel free to skip this step. Once ready add to the mix
1. Add the salmon with a sprinkling of fish sauce and two teaspoons if the Za’atar.
1. When the salmon has changed colour add the double cream. I like to add 200ml so there’s a little left to be dropped into tomorrow’s meal, whatever that may be.
1. Add the pepper and leave for 5 mins.
1. Transfer to the pie dish, cover with a layer of pastry and use the leftovers to decorate in a silly fashion. Bake for 25 mins at 170.
