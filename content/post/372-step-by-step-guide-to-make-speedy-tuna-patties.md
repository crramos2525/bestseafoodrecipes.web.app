##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Patties:

1. Get 200 grams of canned tuna
1. Prepare 2 piece spring onions, chopped
1. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, minced
1. Get 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
1. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1. Take 2 tbsp cornmeal
1. Prepare 6 tbsp ap flour
1. Take 1 egg
1. Get 1 tbsp mayonnaise
1. Get 1 salt and pepper to taste, i use 1 teaspoon for each
1. Get  frying oil, i used corn oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tuna Patties:

1. Before using the tuna, leave it for 5 mins in a strainer. Then in a bowl mix the tuna with the onion, garlic, lemon juice, parsley, salt and pepper.
1. Next add the cornmeal, flour, egg and mayo and mix well.
1. Heat the oil and drop spoonfuls of the tuna mix. Let the patties cook until golden brown. Enjoy!
