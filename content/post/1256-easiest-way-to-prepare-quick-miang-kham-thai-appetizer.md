##### Instructions to make Miang Kham /Thai Appetizer:

1. Finely chop ginger,lemongrass,galangal  ,clean leaves ,set aside
1. Roast coconut until light brown,add shrimp paste,dry shrimps,stir until little more brown,add palm sugar,water ,half of salt ,then let it simmer on low heat,stir frequently protect from burning until sauce get to dark brown color approximately 1 to 2 hours
1. During this time in  seperate pan ,roast peanut until crispy ,do the same with shreded coconut until light brown ..then remove to serving plate ,chop chili, dice lime,ginger,clean leaves ,pat dry ,prepare to serving plate
1. To eat : fill one leaf with fillings ,top with half teaspoon of  sauce ,this can get a big bowl of sauce ,good for party or daily appetizer as it can save in refrigerator for later use
