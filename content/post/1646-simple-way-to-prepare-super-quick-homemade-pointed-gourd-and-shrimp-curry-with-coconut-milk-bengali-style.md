##### The ingredients needed to make Pointed-gourd and Shrimp Curry with Coconut Milk (Bengali style):-:

1. Take 1/2 cup Gobindobhog rice
1. Take 2 cups Pointed gourd pieces
1. Prepare 1 cup Shrimp
1. Take 3 cups Coconut milk
1. Take 1 Chopped onion
1. Make ready 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
1. Make ready 1 tsp Chopped green chilli
1. Take 1 tsp Green chilli paste
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Cumin powder
1. Take 1 tbsp Coriander powder
1. Take 1 tsp Garam masala powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1. Take 1 Bay leaf
1. Get 1 clove
1. Make ready 1 inch Cinnamon
1. Take 2 Cardamom
1. Prepare  Salt As per taste
1. Take 1 tbsp Ghee
1. Prepare  Oil As required




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pointed-gourd and Shrimp Curry with Coconut Milk (Bengali style):-:

1. Wash gobindovog rice and soak in water.
1. Keep aside for about 1 hour.
1. Add salt and turmeric powder with shrimps and saute till brown.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add bay leaf, clove, cardamom, cinnamon for seasoning.
1. Add onion and fry till it becomes golden brown.
1. Add ginger-garlic paste.
1. Add the rest of the ingredients and 2 cups coconut milk till the oil separates.
1. Add gobindovog rice, pointed gourd, shrimp and cook for sometime.
1. After the rice is ready, add 1 cup coconut milk and ghee.
1. Serve with steamed rice.
