##### Instructions to make Beloved shrimp pasta:

1. Defrost the shrimps if they are frozen or peel and devein them if they are fresh.
1. Prepare the pasta.
1. Cut the onion into round slices and sauté. As soon as it starts to get translucent, add the shrimps and sauté everything together.
1. Season with salt, pepper and rosemary.
1. As soon as the shrimps are well sautéed and they are left with just a little of their juices, add the wine and the tomato. Allow to simmer.
1. Ready to serve!
