##### The ingredients needed to make Whosayna’s Prawn Tempura:

1. Prepare  Marinade:
1. Make ready 1 kg Prawns (with tails)
1. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
1. Get 1 tsp Paprika
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Garlic paste
1. Get 1/2 Lemon juice
1. Take  Coating:
1. Make ready 1 cup All Purpose flour
1. Get 1/4 cup Cornflour
1. Get 1 Egg
1. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Blackpepper powder
1. Take  Salt (little)
1. Take  Cold Water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Whosayna’s Prawn Tempura:

1. Marinate the prawns and keep in fridge for three hours
1. Keep wok filled with oil on flame
1. In a bowl beat egg add the rest add little cold water at a time and mix till you get pouring consistency
1. Dip each prawn in the batter and put in hot oil, put atleast six at a time
1. Deep fry till crispy golden color remove on strainer then lay on platter with kitchen towel on it
