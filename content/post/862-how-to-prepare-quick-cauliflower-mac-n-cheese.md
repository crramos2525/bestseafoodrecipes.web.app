##### The ingredients needed to make Cauliflower Mac n Cheese:

1. Take 2 cup cauliflower florets
1. Prepare 1 cup elbow pasta shells
1. Take 2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1. Prepare 1/2 cup low fat sour cream
1. Get 1/4 cup 2% milk
1. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Prepare 2 tbsp bread crumbs
1. Make ready 1 tbsp dried parsley




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cauliflower Mac n Cheese:

1. Preheat oven to 375 ° Spray a 9&#39; dish with cooking spray.
1. Cook pasta to direction. In last 3 minutes, add cauliflower florets. Drain well.
1. Return to pot. Add milk, sour cream and cheese by handful. Stirring until well mixed.
1. Scoop into sprayed dish. Bake in oven 15-20 minutes.
1. In a small pan, heat olive oil and brown bread crumbs with parsley.
1. Top with bread crumbs. Bon Appetit!
