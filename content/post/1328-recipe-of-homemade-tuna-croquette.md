##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Croquette:

1. Take 2 cans tuna in water, drained
1. Make ready 1 shallot, minced
1. Get 2 garlic cloves, minced
1. Prepare 1 egg
1. Get 1 tbsp spicy mustard
1. Take 1 tbsp mayo
1. Make ready  juice of one lemon
1. Get  squirt of sriarcha sauce
1. Prepare 1 cup breadcrumbs
1. Take dash basil and seasoning salt
1. Take  oil for frying




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tuna Croquette:

1. Mix all ingredients except oil in a bowl.
1. In a large skillet heat oil. About 1/2 inch of oil. Medium high heat
1. Form 4 patties. Pat each side with a bit more breadcrumbs to help hold together
1. Fry in skillet, about 5 mins per side.
1. Drain on paper towel before serving.
