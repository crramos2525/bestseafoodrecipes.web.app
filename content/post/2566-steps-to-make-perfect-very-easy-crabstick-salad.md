##### The ingredients needed to make Very Easy Crabstick Salad:

1. Take 1 packet Imitation crab sticks
1. Take 2 Cucumber
1. Prepare 5 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Take 1 Salt and pepper
1. Prepare 1 Salt (to rub the cucumbers)

##### Instructions to make Very Easy Crabstick Salad:

1. Prepare the ingredients.
