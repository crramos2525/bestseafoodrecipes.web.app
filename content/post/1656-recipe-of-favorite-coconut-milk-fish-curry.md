##### The ingredients needed to make Coconut Milk Fish Curry:

1. Get 6-7 pieces Rohu fish ( you can use prawn in the place of Rohu
1. Take 1 cup fresh coconut milk
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
1. Get To taste Salt
1. Prepare To tsste Garam masala
1. Take 1 chopped tomato
1. Get 1 chopped onion
1. Get 1 Tbsp chopped ginger and garlic
1. Take as needed curry leaves
1. Get 2-3 green chilli slices
1. Get as needed Chopped coriander
1. Get 1tsp Coriander &amp; Cumin Powder
1. Make ready As needed Refined oil
1. Take As needed Water

##### Instructions to make Coconut Milk Fish Curry:

1. Fry the fishes first after marinating with turmeric &amp; salt
1. Again hit a pan. Fry onion &amp; chopped ginger-garlic till golden brown.
1. After that add tomatoes &amp; coriander, cumin powder. Add 2tbsp water. Cook till the tomatoes became soft. Add curry leaves.
1. Add coconut milk, salt &amp; turmeric. Bring it a boil &amp; add fish fry.
1. Add garam masala &amp; reduce the gravy. Add chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
