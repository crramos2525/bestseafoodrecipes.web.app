##### The ingredients needed to make Meat-Wrapped Temari Pinchos:

1. Prepare 180 grams Hot cooked rice
1. Make ready 150 grams Thinly sliced beef
1. Get 1 dash Salt and pepper
1. Make ready 1 dash Plain flour
1. Take 1 tbsp ■Sake
1. Prepare 1 tbsp ■Mirin
1. Take 1 tbsp ■Vinegar
1. Prepare 1 tbsp ■Soy sauce
1. Prepare 2 tsp ■Sugar
1. Take 1 tsp ■Bonito dashi stock, granulated
1. Take 1 tsp ■Sesame oil
1. Take 1 White sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Meat-Wrapped Temari Pinchos:

1. Divide the rice into 15-18 g portions and use cling film to shape into balls. Combine all of the ingredients marked with ■.
1. This time, I used store-bought microwaveable rice.
1. Wrap the rice balls with meat and use the palms of your hands to shape them into balls. Season with salt and pepper.
1. Sprinkle some flour over the top.
1. Add some vegetable oil and a little sesame oil to a frying pan and cook the meat-wrapped riceballs until browned. Then add the sauce from. Heat it up and coat the meatballs with it.
1. Top with white sesame seeds and skewer them through!
1. Ready!
