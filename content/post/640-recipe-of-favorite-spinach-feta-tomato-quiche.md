##### The ingredients needed to make Spinach, Feta, Tomato Quiche:

1. Prepare 1 frozen pie shell (thawed)
1. Make ready 4 eggs
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup fresh baby spinach
1. Make ready 1/2 cup roughly chopped fresh mushrooms
1. Make ready 2 clove minced garlic
1. Get 3 tbsp chopped purple onion
1. Make ready 15 grape tomatoes halved
1. Take 1/2 tbsp salt (I use Celtic gray salt)
1. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Get 3/4 cup feta crumbles
1. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spinach, Feta, Tomato Quiche:

1. Take pie shell out to thaw and preheat your oven to 350°.
1. Beat 4 eggs in a bowl then add the salt, pepper, and baking powder.
1. Add all the other ingredients and mix until the spinach has wilted a little.
1. Pour egg mixture into your pie crust and bake for about 50 minutes. Let set for at least 30-45 minutes. Enjoy!
