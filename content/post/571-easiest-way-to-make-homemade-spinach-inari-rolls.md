##### The ingredients needed to make Spinach Inari Rolls:

1. Make ready 1 sheet Unflavored Inari
1. Prepare 1 bundle Spinach
1. Take 1 dash Salt
1. Take 1 Nori seaweed




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spinach Inari Rolls:

1. Boil the Inari pouches in hot water, and tightly wring out the water by hand. Open both sides by slitting it with a knife.
1. Thoroughly wring out the spinach after boiling it in salt water. Place the spinach towards the front, and roll it up.
1. Placed the overlap towards the bottom, and cut it in half.
1. Tightly wrap with seaweed, and it is done.
