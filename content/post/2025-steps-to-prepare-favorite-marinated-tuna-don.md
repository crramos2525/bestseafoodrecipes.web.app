##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Tuna Don:

1. Prepare 150 grams Tuna (sashimi grade)
1. Take 1 Green onions
1. Take 1 Toasted sesame seeds
1. Make ready 1 Sesame oil
1. Take 1 Shredded nori seaweed
1. Make ready 2 bowlfuls Plain steamed rice
1. Take  Seasonings
1. Take 50 ml Soy sauce
1. Get 25 ml Sake
1. Take 25 ml Mirin
1. Take 1 tsp Sugar
1. Make ready 1 Wasabi




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Marinated Tuna Don:

1. Warm all the seasoning ingredients in a pot. Let cool, then dissolve the wasabi.
1. Marinade the tuna for a while.
1. Put the rice, tuna, sesame seeds, green onions, and chopped nori into a rice bowl, sprinkle with sesame oil, and serve.
