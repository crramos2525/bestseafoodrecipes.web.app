##### The ingredients needed to make Bicol Beef Express:

1. Take 100 grams Beef Tenderloin
1. Prepare 1 cup smoky coconut milk
1. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic mince
1. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger jullienne
1. Get 1 small size onion slice
1. Prepare 5 pcs Finger Chillies strips
1. Get 1 tbsp Fermented Shrimp
1. Make ready 1/2 cup pineapple juice
1. Get 6 pcs pineapple chunks(in can)
1. Take  Salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Bicol Beef Express:

1. Heat oil in pan in medium heat.Brown the meat.Put onion,garlic,ginger and Fermented shrimp.Saute
1. Pour coconut milk and pineapple juice.Reduce until sauce thickens and the meat is tender
1. A minute before fire off put chillies and pineapple and stir.Salt and pepper to taste
1. Serve with Steamed Rice
