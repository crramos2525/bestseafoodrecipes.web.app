##### The ingredients needed to make Crispy Shrimp &amp; Vegetable Fritters:

1. Prepare 1 Squid
1. Prepare 10 small Shrimp
1. Make ready 1 bunch Mitsuba
1. Prepare 2 Japanese leek
1. Take 1/2 tbsp ★Baking powder
1. Make ready 200 ml ★Cake flour
1. Take 1/2 ★Egg
1. Get 1 egg + water = 180ml ★Ice cold water
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp ★Sugar
1. Make ready 1 Homemade mentsuyu




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Crispy Shrimp &amp; Vegetable Fritters:

1. Prepare the shrimp and cut in half. Skin the squid and cut into 3 cm pieces. Cut the leeks into 1 cm pieces and the mitsuba into 3 cm pieces.
1. Coat the squid and shrimp with flour. Put the ★ ingredients into a bowl to make the coating. Divide the squid and shrimp into 4 portions, place each portion on a separate plate, and cover with the coating.
1. Deep-fry in 170℃ oil until golden brown.
