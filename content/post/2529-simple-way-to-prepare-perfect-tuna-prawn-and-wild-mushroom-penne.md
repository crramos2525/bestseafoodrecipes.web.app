##### Instructions to make Tuna prawn and wild mushroom penne:

1. Get the water boiling for the pasta with some salt and olive oil in it. In a very hot frying pan or dutch oven large enough to hold all the ingredients sear the prawns till bright orange and then sear the tuna in chunks in the same pot.
1. Pour in about 300ml of white wine and add the garlic, saffron mushrooms and salt.
1. Get the pasta started in the boiling water.
1. Allow to boil for about 3 minutes then turn the heat low and add the soured cream a tablespoon at a time stirring each spoonful in carefully to avoid splitting.
1. Squeeze in half the lemon reserve the rest to serve in slices with the pasta.
1. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce turn off the heat. Stir and serve with white pepper, lemon slices and parmesan.
