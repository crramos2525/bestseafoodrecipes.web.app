##### Steps to make Cucumber and Wasabi Brown Sushi Rolls:

1. Wash the sushi rice. Add the rice and water to the pan and bring to the boil. Cover with a lid and simmer for 35 minutes or until the water has absorbed. Turn off the heat and leave to stand for a further 10 minutes.
1. Using a cutting motion add the mirin and vinegar to the rice.
1. Using the sushi mat, place a sheet of nori and cover with the rice (leaving a 1cm edge at the bottom)
1. Arrange your cucumber strips along the middle of the rice and add a pinch of wasabi. Pick up the end of the sushi mat nearest to you, roll and cut into equal portions with a sharp, wet knife.
1. Serve with pickled ginger or your favourite sushi condiments
