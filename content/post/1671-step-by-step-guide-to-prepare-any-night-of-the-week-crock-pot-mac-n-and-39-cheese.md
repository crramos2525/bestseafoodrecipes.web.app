##### The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Mac n&#39; Cheese:

1. Make ready 16 oz elbow macaroni
1. Prepare 3 cup milk
1. Make ready 8 oz velveeta cheese, cut into cubes
1. Make ready 1/4 cup butter, cut into cubes
1. Take 3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1. Get 1 to 2 cans tuna, if preferred
1. Prepare 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Crock Pot Mac n&#39; Cheese:

1. Cook pasta in boiling water 3 minutes short of al dente.
1. Place the cooked macaroni, milk, velveeta, butter, cheddar cheese, and tuna in your crock pot.
1. Stir and top with parmesan cheese.
1. Cook on low for 2-3 hours. Stir occassionally and check for doneness around the 2 hour mark.
