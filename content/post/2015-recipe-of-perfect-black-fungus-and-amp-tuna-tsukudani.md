##### The ingredients needed to make Black Fungus &amp; Tuna ‘Tsukudani’:

1. Prepare 20 g Dried Black Fungus
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sake (Rice Wine) OR Water
1. Take 1 tablespoon Sugar
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
1. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Chilli Flakes
1. Take 1 canned Tuna *95g, drained
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Black Fungus &amp; Tuna ‘Tsukudani’:

1. Soak Dried Black Fungus in cold/warm water until softened. Wash very well, drain and cut into small pieces.
1. Heat Sesame Oil in a frying pan over medium heat, cook Black Fungus for a few minutes. Add all other ingredients and cook, stirring, until the sauce is almost gone.
