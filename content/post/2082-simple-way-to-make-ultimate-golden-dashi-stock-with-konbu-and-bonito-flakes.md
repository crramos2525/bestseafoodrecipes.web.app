##### The ingredients needed to make Golden Dashi Stock with Konbu and Bonito Flakes:

1. Take 3 grams Kombu
1. Take 15 grams Bonito flakes
1. Prepare 1000 ml Water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Golden Dashi Stock with Konbu and Bonito Flakes:

1. Wipe the white part of kombu seaweed with damp dish cloth or paper towel.
1. Soak the konbu seaweed and water in a pan for 30 minutes.
1. After 30 minutes, heat it over medium and remove the konbu just before it comes to a boil.
1. Soon after the konbu is taken out, put in the bonito flakes. After it comes to a boil (it should come to a boil right after), turn the heat off and leave for 3 minutes.
1. Afterward, strain the broth with a strainer and it&#39;s done.  (If you want make it saltier, add a pinch of salt).
