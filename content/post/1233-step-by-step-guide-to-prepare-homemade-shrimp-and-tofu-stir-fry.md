##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Tofu Stir-Fry:

1. Get  Large bag of medium shrimp, peeled and no tails
1. Take  Sweet onion, diced
1. Take  Zucchini, diced
1. Make ready  green onions, chopped
1. Prepare  Baby portobella mushrooms, roughly chopped
1. Make ready  diced garlic
1. Prepare  salt
1. Take  Sesame seeds
1. Make ready  olive oil, extra virgin for sauteing
1. Prepare  mini corns, drained and rinsed
1. Get  bean sprouts or 1/3 cup fresh bean sprouts
1. Prepare  Super firm Tofu, cubed (bought mine cubed already)
1. Prepare  Bottle of Orange Sauce, any brand will do
1. Get  Teriyaki sauce
1. Prepare  low sodium soy sauce
1. Prepare  water chestnuts, drained and rinsed
1. Prepare  Broccoli slaw




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Shrimp and Tofu Stir-Fry:

1. In a large pot, over medium-high heat, add olive oil and let heat. Then add your onion, garlic, zucchini, and mushrooms. Saute until tender-crisp.
1. Add your broccoli slaw, Sesame seeds, soy sauce, Teriyaki sauce, water chestnuts, bean sprouts, and Tofu. Let the slaw become tender.
1. When the ingredients are nice and soft; add shrimp. Be sure to mix the shrimp together so it can cook evenly! Let cook until almost all the way pink. Then add the Orange sauce!
1. Let simmer for 10 minutes and remove from heat! Serve with your choice of side! We had ours with rice!
1. Enjoy!!
