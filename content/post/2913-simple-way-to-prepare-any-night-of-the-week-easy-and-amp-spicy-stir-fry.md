##### The ingredients needed to make Easy &amp; Spicy Stir Fry:

1. Get 1/2 medium onion, chopped
1. Make ready 2 clove garlic, minced
1. Make ready 250 grams shrimp
1. Make ready 1 cup button mushroom, sliced into 2
1. Prepare 3 medium red chillies, sliced
1. Take 450 grams broccoli florets
1. Get 3 tbsp oyster sauce
1. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Get 1 tbsp fish sauce
1. Prepare 1 salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy &amp; Spicy Stir Fry:

1. Blanch broccoli florets in boiling water and a dash of salt while peel the shrimp.
1. Marinate shrimp with a pinch of salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
1. After broccoli is tender, put in ice cold water and set aside.
1. Preheat non-stick pan in small heat with olive oil.
1. Cook onion, garlic, and chillies until just tender.
1. Add mushroom and shrimp, toss them together with the onion and garlic.
1. Add oyster sauce and fish sauce, turn to medium heat.
1. Add broccoli florets and stir fry for 2-3 minutes. You can add a bit of water if you want.
1. Add salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to your liking.
1. Turn the heat off and put the dish into the plate. Serve with rice.
