##### The ingredients needed to make Bell Peppers and Bonito Flakes Scrambled Eggs:

1. Take 5 small Green bell peppers
1. Take 1 tbsp Sugar
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Mirin
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Miso
1. Prepare 1 small packet Bonito flakes
1. Prepare 1 Egg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Bell Peppers and Bonito Flakes Scrambled Eggs:

1. Remove the seeds from the bell peppers and slice thinly.
1. Heat a pan over medium heat, add the peppers, sugar and mirin and stir-fry.
1. When the peppers have wilted, add the miso.
1. Taste. If it&#39;s OK, add the bonito flakes.
1. Stir-fry to evaporate water from the pan, then add the beaten egg.
1. Stir-fry, scramble, and you&#39;re done!
