##### Instructions to make Squid Fry:

1. Clean and cut the squids into small pieces.
1. Cook the squid with 1teaspoon of chili powder,turmeric powder salt and coconut pieces.Prefer open cook with high flame.No need of adding water.
1. Heat oil(1/4 cup) in a kadai and splutter mustard seeds.Add onion,ginger,garlic,green chillies and curry leaves.Saute it until it becomes light brown color.
