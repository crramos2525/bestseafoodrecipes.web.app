##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Green Prawn Curry:

1. Make ready 150 g cooked king prawn
1. Make ready 1/2 cup coconut milk
1. Make ready 3 tbsp green Thai curry paste
1. Prepare 1 tbsp fish sauce
1. Prepare to taste Salt
1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Get Half cup broccoli
1. Get Half cup green beans
1. Get Half cup spinach
1. Get 3 green fresh chilli
1. Take  Red chilli to garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Thai Green Prawn Curry:

1. Wash all the vegetables. Only cut broccoli in medium pieces. Now in a frying pan pour oil. Fry the broccoli and beans for few minutes. Now add green curry paste. Add prawns and spinach cook them well in medium flame.
1. Then add coconut milk and bring it to boil. Add green chillies and fish sauce. Put the lid on and cook them for 10mins in low flame. If needed can add some water.
1. It can be served with steamed rice or fried rice.
