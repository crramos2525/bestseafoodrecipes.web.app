##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice:

1. Get 3 cups cooked rice
1. Take 1 handful anchovies, crispy fried
1. Make ready 3 garlic cloves and 1 tbsp dried shrimps, pounded in pestle and mortar
1. Get 1 small onion, coarsely sliced
1. Take 3 tbsp That Rendang Lady sambal paste or 3 tbsp of chilli paste or sambal oelek)
1. Take 300 grams chicken thigh, diced
1. Make ready 1 tbsp sweet soya sauce, 1 tsp of salty soya sauce and 1 tsp of caramel soya sauce. Mix them with 2 tbsp of water
1. Make ready 3 eggs
1. Make ready 100 grams canned garden peas
1. Prepare  small knob of butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Fried Rice:

1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok and saute the onion till soft. Add in the pounded garlic and shrimp. Continue to stir till fragrant. Add in the diced chicken and continue to cook till chicken is cooked through.
1. Add in some salt and the sambal paste as well as few tablespoons of the soya sauce mixture. Stir to incorporate the sauce then add the garden peas rice. Let cook for a minute.
1. Finally, sprinkle with the crispy anchovies and chopped spring onions if you have some.
