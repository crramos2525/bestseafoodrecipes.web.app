##### Instructions to make Crab Masala:

1. Marinate the crab with chilli powder,turmeric powder and enough salt for 30 minutes
1. Heat oil in a non stick pan and add grated coconut along with shallot,curry leaves and garlic.Roast the coconut on low flame till it become dark brown color.Add coriander powder,mix well and switch off the flame.Let it cool.Grind it to a smooth paste along with little water.Keep aside.
1. Place crabmeat in a pan with ¾ cup water and cook for 10 minutes. Set aside
1. In a pan heat oil. Splutter the mustard seeds. Add fenugreek seeds and black pepper. ginger,garlic and green chillies.Add shallot and saute for 5 minutes.Add tomato and cook till tomato is done. add cooked crab and coconut paste and mix well. cook for some time. Crab masala is ready.
