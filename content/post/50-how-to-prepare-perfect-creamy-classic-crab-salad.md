##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Classic Crab Salad:

1. Prepare 2 tbs. finely chopped Celery
1. Prepare 1 tbs. finely diced Red Onion
1. Prepare 3 tbs. Light Mayo
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Hot Old Bay Seasoning (or regular)
1. Get 1/2 tsp. Dried Dill Weed
1. Prepare  Salt and Pepper (to taste)

##### Instructions to make Creamy Classic Crab Salad:
