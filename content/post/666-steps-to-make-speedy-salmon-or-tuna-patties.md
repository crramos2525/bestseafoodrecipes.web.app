##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon or Tuna Patties:

1. Prepare 1/2 Cup Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1. Prepare 1 Egg
1. Get 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1. Get  Salt and Pepper to your liking

##### Steps to make Salmon or Tuna Patties:
