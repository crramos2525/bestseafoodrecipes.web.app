##### The ingredients needed to make New Year&#39;s Ozouni (Mochi Soup) from Aichi Prefecture:

1. Make ready 4 Mochi (rice cakes)
1. Prepare 1/2 bunch Mochina greens (or komatsuna greens)
1. Make ready 1 Bonito flakes
1. Prepare 400 ml ★Water
1. Take 2 tsp ★Bonito dashi granules
1. Take 2 tsp ★Soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 pinch Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make New Year&#39;s Ozouni (Mochi Soup) from Aichi Prefecture:

1. Add the ★ ingredients to a pot and bring to a boil.
1. Add the mochi and mochina greens to the pot and simmer until the mochi softens. Add salt to taste.
1. Transfer to bowls and top with bonito flakes.
