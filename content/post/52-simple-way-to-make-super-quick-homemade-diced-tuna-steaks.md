##### The ingredients needed to make Diced Tuna Steaks:

1. Make ready 100 grams Lean tuna (Maguro, sashimi grade)
1. Make ready 1 tsp Yakiniku sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp Sake
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Soy sauce
1. Get 1 Mayonnaise
1. Prepare 1 Green onions or scallions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Diced Tuna Steaks:

1. Dice tuna into 1 to 1.5 cm cubes. Combine yakiniku sauce, sake, and soy sauce to make the marinating sauce.
1. Soak the tuna diced in Step 1 in the marinating sauce and let it rest to absorb the flavors for about 30 minutes. Roll it around for a few times while it&#39;s marinating.
1. Heat some vegetable oil (not listed) in a frying pan and grill tuna prepped in Step 2 over medium heat. Don&#39;t throw away the leftover marinating sauce!
1. Make sure the tuna doesn&#39;t burn. When cooked thoroughly, pour in the leftover marinating sauce if any. Coat with sauce and stir-fry.
1. Transfer the freshly cooked tuna onto a plate and serve with mayonnaise and green onions. Done♪ Sprinkle some shichimi spice if you wish.
1. Just use inexpensive lean sashimi tuna. Blocks are fine; it doesn&#39;t need to be nicely cut! The sinews won&#39;t bother you once it&#39;s cooked.
