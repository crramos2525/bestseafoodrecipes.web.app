##### The ingredients needed to make Seaweed Rice Balls:

1. Prepare 2 cups cooked rice, mashed
1. Prepare 8-10 small roasted seaweed sheets, crumbled
1. Get 1 tsp. soy sauce / salt to taste
1. Get 1/2 tsp. sugar
1. Get 1 tsp. sesame oil
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp. red chilli flakes (opt)
1. Take 1 tsp. roasted sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Seaweed Rice Balls:

1. I have made use of 8-10 of these small roasted seaweed sheets.
1. Mix all the ingredients (except sesame seeds) till everything is well combined.
1. Wet your hands &amp; take a little portion of the rice mix. Shape them into round balls. Make similar balls with the rest of the mix.
1. Sprinkle the roasted sesame seeds over them.
1. Serve with soy sauce, red chilli sauce, ketchup or any chutney a la Indian style.
