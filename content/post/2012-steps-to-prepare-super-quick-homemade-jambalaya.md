##### Instructions to make Jambalaya:

1. In a large pot heat olive oil on medium heat and add in green and red peppers, onion, and celery. Season with salt and pepper. Cook 5 minutes.
1. Stir in chicken and season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Cook 5 minutes.
1. Stir in smoked sausage, garlic, and tomato paste. Cook 1 minute.
1. Add chicken or veggie broth, crushed tomatoes, rice, and old bay seasoning. Reduce heat to medium low, cover with lid, and cook 20 minutes.
1. Add shrimp, cook 3-5 minutes. Stir in green onions and serve.
