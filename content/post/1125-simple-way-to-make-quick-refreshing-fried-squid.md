##### The ingredients needed to make Refreshing Fried Squid:

1. Make ready 1 Squid
1. Make ready 15 grams Butter
1. Take 1 tsp Usukuchi soy sauce
1. Get 1 tbsp Grain vinegar
1. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Refreshing Fried Squid:

1. Prepare the squid (remove the insides) and cut into bite-sized pieces.
1. Put the butter in a heated pan and fry the prepared squid. Season with salt, pepper and light soy sauce. Lastly, pour in the vinegar.
