##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Jalapenos:

1. Get 12 Jalapenos
1. Prepare 16 oz cream cheese
1. Take 1 packages Crab meat
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Cheddar Cheese
1. Get 1/4 cup Light Cream
1. Make ready 2 tbsp old bay
1. Take 1 tbsp garlic powder
1. Take 1 tbsp chipotle powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Stuffed Jalapenos:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
1. Cut your jalapenos in half, making 24
1. Clean out seeds and rinse in cold water. Set to the side.
1. In a bowl, mix all other ingredients together until blends uniform.
1. With a spoon, or fork, stuff your jalapenos and place on a baking sheet.
1. Once you stuff the peppers place in the oven for 6 to 9 min or until heated through. There will be some filling left.
