##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Grilled Salmon with Mirin and Soy Sauce:

1. Get 4 filets Raw salmon
1. Make ready  Sauce for marinating
1. Take 4 tbsp ☆ Mirin
1. Take 4 tbsp ☆ Soy sauce
1. Prepare 2 tbsp ☆ Sake




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Grilled Salmon with Mirin and Soy Sauce:

1. In a baking pan or tray, mix together the sauce ingredients to marinate, and allow the salmon to marinate for about 30 minutes.
1. Grill salmon cuts for 3-5 minutes on both sides over medium heat. Do not dispose the marinating sauce!
1. While the salmon is grilling, pour the leftover sauce into a pot, and simmer on medium heat for about 8 minutes until it thickens.
1. When the salmon is done, transfer to serving plates, drizzle on the stewed sauce, and it&#39;s ready to serve.
