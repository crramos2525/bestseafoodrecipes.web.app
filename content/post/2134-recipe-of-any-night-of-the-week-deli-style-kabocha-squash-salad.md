##### The ingredients needed to make Deli-style Kabocha Squash Salad:

1. Prepare 1/4 Kabocha squash
1. Get 1/4 Onion (large)
1. Take 1 Canned tuna (small)
1. Take 5 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Prepare 1 Salt and black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Deli-style Kabocha Squash Salad:

1. Cut the kabocha squash into large pieces, place in a microwave proof dish and microwave. Refer to. -  - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/143434-steamed-kabocha-in-the-microwave
1. Slice the onions thinly and rub with salt. Soak in water, and squeeze out the water.
1. Mash the cooked kabocha squash with a fork, and combine the onion, canned tuna. Season with mayonnaise, salt and pepper to taste.
