##### Instructions to make Hot Pepper Jelly Glazed Salmon &amp; Cauliflower:

1. Preheat oven to 400°F
1. Marinate salmon filets with a mix of hot pepper jelly, sesame oil, soy sauce, and pepper. Leave it in room temperature for 15 minutes.
1. Butter a baking pan lined with foil and place salmon filets nicely spaced out.
1. Bake filets for 15 minutes, adding leftover marinate every 6 minutes. Cook until desired.
1. Add salt to water and bring to a boil.
1. Add cauliflower and keep boiling until tender.
1. Serve salmon and cauliflower together for a healthy, and protein-filled meal!
