##### Instructions to make Squid &amp; Edamame Patties:

1. Clean Squid and Fish Fillet, cut into small pieces and place them in a bowl of a food processor. Add a pinch of Salt and Pepper, and process until paste-like texture is achieved, but do not over process until too smooth.
1. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl, add Edamame and combine well.
1. Heat small mount Oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Put some Oil on your hands so the mixture won’t stick, make 5cm size patties and cook on the frying pan until golden and cooked though.
