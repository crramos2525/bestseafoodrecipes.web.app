##### Steps to make Paella:

1. Wash and shop thin all vegetables. In a separate pan precook the rice for 10 min, make sure the rice is dry and semi-cooked(great trick to save time with the traditional rice fry)
1. In another pot, put half of your seafood mix, a grated half of onion, garlic and lemon juice and take it to fire for 10 minutes. (conserve the soup)
1. In a different pot, fry your chopped onion, add grated tomate and chilli. Gradually add zucchini, asparagus, half of the shopped red pepper and the remaining seafood mix. Let it steam for few minutes and add rice to the soup
1. Stir until the soup and rice are homogeneous. Let it cook for 10minutes stirring whenever necessary. Add the seafood mix you prepared at the beginning and use the precook seafood to decorate the top of your paella. Let it steam for 10 more minutes open pot and done!
