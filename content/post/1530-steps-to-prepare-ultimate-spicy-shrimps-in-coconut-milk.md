##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimps in Coconut Milk:

1. Take 1/4 cup oil
1. Make ready 1 chopped medium size onion
1. Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
1. Prepare 2 tsp minced ginger
1. Prepare 2 tbsp coriander
1. Get 1/4 tsp cumin
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp cinnamon
1. Prepare 1/8 tsp cayenne
1. Make ready 1/8 tsp tumeric
1. Take 1 cup drained canned tomatoes
1. Get 2 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1. Get 1/2 cup water
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Take 3/4 cup fresh cilantro
1. Make ready 1 1/2 Ibs large shrimps, peeled and cleaned




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spicy Shrimps in Coconut Milk:

1. Heat oil in skillet on med-hi heat
1. Add onions, fry for 3 minutes
1. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 2 minutes
1. Add next 5 spice, cook for 1 minute
1. Add canned tomatoes (or chopped tomatoes) and cook for 3 minutes.
1. Add coconut milk, water, salt and bring to simmer until thicken, about 5-10 minutes
1. Add shrimps, simmer and stirring for 5 minutes
1. Stir in cilantro and serve with lime wedge
