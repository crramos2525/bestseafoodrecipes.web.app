##### The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Pecan Crusted Salmon:

1. Get 1 lb of salmon, cut into 4 pieces, or 4 salmon filets
1. Get 1/2 cup BBQ or mustard sauce/glaze
1. Take 2/3 cup pecans, finely ground in a food processor
1. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
1. Get 2 tbsp olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cinnamon Pecan Crusted Salmon:

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
1. Add ground pecans to a large plate and stir in cinnamon.
1. Brush salmon pieces with BBQ sauce/glaze. Coat with pecan mixture, pressing so the mixture sticks.
1. Heat a skillet on medium high heat and add a tablespoon of olive oil.
1. Brown salmon on each side until pecan crust is golden.
1. Remove and put on a wire rack placed on a baking sheet.
1. Bake for 15 minutes, or until salmon flakes with a fork.
1. Serve with rice or vegetables, or on a salad.
