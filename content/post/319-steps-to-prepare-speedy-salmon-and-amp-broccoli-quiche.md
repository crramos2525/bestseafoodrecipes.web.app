##### Steps to make Salmon &amp; Broccoli Quiche:

1. Preheat oven to 400°
1. Cook frozen pie shell for 8 mins.
1. Reduce oven temp to 325°.
1. Combine milk, mayonnaise, cornstarch, and eggs. Mix thoroughly.
1. Add drained salmon, broccoli, cheese, dill, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
1. Pour mix into pie shell.
1. Cook at 325° for 45 mins.
1. Let stand 5 mins before serving.
