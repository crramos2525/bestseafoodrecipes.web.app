##### The ingredients needed to make Tebasaki Chicken Wings in Oden:

1. Get 10 Chicken wings (middle section and tip)
1. Make ready 800 ml Dashi stock (kombu and bonito based)
1. Prepare 100 ml ●Sake
1. Make ready 3 tbsp ●Soy sauce
1. Get 1 tbsp ●Mirin
1. Prepare 2 tsp ●Sugar
1. Take 1 Grated ginger
1. Make ready 1 Green onions (finely sliced)
1. Take 1 Grated daikon radish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tebasaki Chicken Wings in Oden:

1. Put the frying pan on the heat and add the chicken wings. Brown them on both sides on high heat.
1. Then put the chicken wings from Step 1, the dashi stock, and the seasoning ingredients marked with ● into a saucepan and heat. Once the mixture has boiled, turn the heat to low.
1. Simmer the mixture for around 1 hour on a very low heat. After that, stop the heat and allow the contents of the pan to cool naturally.
1. Warm everything up again just before eating. Serve in a bowl accompanied with some grated daikon radish and grated ginger, then sprinkle some green onion and parsley over the top to finish.
