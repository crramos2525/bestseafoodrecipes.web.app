##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn and Pork Toast:

1. Get 1/4 k Ground Pork
1. Take 1/4 k Prawn, chopped
1. Make ready 8-110 cloves garlic, chopped
1. Prepare 1 Egg white
1. Take 3 Tbsp Fish Sauce
1. Get 2 Tbsp Soy sauce
1. Get  Pepper
1. Take  White bread, cut into four
1. Get  Oil for frying




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Prawn and Pork Toast:

1. In bowl combine 1st 7 ingredients. Mix
1. Toast bread for 1 min.
1. Put enough amount of mixture into bread
1. Heat oil.
1. Fry bread mixture down then turn it over once golden brown
