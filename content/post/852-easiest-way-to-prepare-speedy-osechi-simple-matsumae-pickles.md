##### The ingredients needed to make Osechi Simple Matsumae Pickles:

1. Prepare 1 Surume-ika (dried squid)
1. Make ready 1 Carrot
1. Prepare 1/2 sheet Hidaka kombu
1. Get 1 Herring roe (if you have it)
1. Make ready 50 ml ●Soy sauce
1. Get 50 ml ●Mirin
1. Make ready 150 ml ●Sake
1. Get 1 ●Red chili pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Osechi Simple Matsumae Pickles:

1. Wipe the dried squid and kombu lightly with a cloth and julienne.
1. Julienne the carrots.
1. Add the seasonings marked ● to a pan and boil until the alcohol has evaporated.
1. Once the mixture from Step 3 has cooled, add the dried squid, kombu and carrots.
1. Leave to pickle for at least 1 night for the flavors to meld.
1. [How to Store] Store in an airtight container for up to 1 week in the refrigerator. Always use dry and clean chopsticks when handling to prevent contamination.
