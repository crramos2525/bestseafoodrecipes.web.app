##### The ingredients needed to make Hawaiian Style Garlic Shrimp:

1. Get 250 grams without the shells Shrimp
1. Take  For the marinade:
1. Get 1 to 2 cloves Garlic
1. Make ready 3/4 to 1 teaspoon Salt
1. Get 1 tbsp White wine
1. Get 2 tbsp Olive oil
1. Make ready 1 Coarsely ground black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Hawaiian Style Garlic Shrimp:

1. Chop up the garlic. Peel the shrimp, and devein by making a shallow cut through the back side and washing in salted water to remove the digestive tract. Pat dry.
1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl and mix together. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
1. Put all the ingredients including the marinade into a pan over medium heat. Cook slowly, being careful not to let it burn, until it&#39;s done.
1. Enjoy while piping hot.
1. If you&#39;d prefer it to be less salty, reduce the amount of salt.
