##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon with Honey Mustard Glaze:

1. Prepare 1 lb salmon fillets
1. Make ready 2 tbsp honey
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Bavarin Sweet mustard, or Dijon
1. Take 1/2 tsp thyme
1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Salmon with Honey Mustard Glaze:

1. Heat oil over medium heat in large skillet.
1. Combine honey, mustard,and thyme.
1. Brush on both sides of fish.
1. Cook in oil until fish flakes easily, about 5 minutes per side.
