##### Instructions to make Shrimp Fried Rice:

1. In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Set aside and allow rice to cool.
1. Heat a large skillet or wok for 2 minutes. When the skillet or wok is hot, pour in vegetable oil, bean sprouts and onions. Mix well and cook for 3 minutes.
1. Mix in cooled rice and shrimp and cook for another 3 minutes. Stirring constantly.
1. Mix in green onions, salt, pepper, and soy sauce. Cook for another 4 minutes, stirring continuously, until everything is blended evenly.
1. you can use eggs and sesame oil if you like as well
