1. Over med- heat alfredo sauce over med-low heat and add butter. Once simmering add broccoli.
1. Coat shrimp in a bowl with 1 tsp. Of EVO, lemon juice and caribbean seasoning. Set aside.
1. Once noodles have been drained and rinsed, add them to sauce and broccoli. Cover and simmer for 8 minutes.
1. Take off heat, and add tomatoes and shrimp. The heat from the food will cook them perfectly. Enjoy!
