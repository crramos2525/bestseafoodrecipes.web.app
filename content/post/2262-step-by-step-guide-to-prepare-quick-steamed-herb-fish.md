##### The ingredients needed to make Steamed Herb Fish:

1. Take 2-3 Salmon fish fillets (clean &amp; washed)
1. Get 1 tspn black pepper powder
1. Prepare 1 lemon Juice
1. Get to taste Salt
1. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
1. Take 1 tspn parsley herbs
1. Take 1/2 cup steamed cauliflower florets
1. Prepare 1/2 cup boiled peas
1. Make ready as needed Beetroot slices for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Steamed Herb Fish:

1. In a mixing bowl combine fish fillets with salt, pepper powder, chopped garlic, herbs. Marinate in this mixture for atleast 2-3 hrs.  - Pack the fish fillet in a foil &amp; seal it. Steam on a steamer for 8-10 minutes.
1. Serve hot with steam veggies
