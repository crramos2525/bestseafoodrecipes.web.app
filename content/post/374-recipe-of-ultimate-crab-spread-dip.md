##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Spread/Dip:

1. Prepare 1 large crab
1. Make ready 1 pinch salt
1. Prepare 500 ml water
1. Prepare 3 tbsp mayonnaise
1. Take 1 tbsp prepared mustard
1. Take 30 ml beer
1. Make ready 1 dash piri-piri sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Crab Spread/Dip:

1. Bring the crab to the boil on a high heat and cook for about 10 minutes in the salt and water.
1. Once cooked, take the grab out of the water and place to one side until it&#39;s cool enough to handle.
1. Remove the claws and reserve to be used separately.
1. Crack the crab open and remove the white meat, shred the meat through the tips of your fingers, making sure there is no small bones left.
1. Add the mayonnaise, mustard, beer and piri-piri to the crab meat and mix well.
1. Serve with some warm toast.
