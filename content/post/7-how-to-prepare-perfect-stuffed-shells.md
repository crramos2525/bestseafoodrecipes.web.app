##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Shells:

1. Take 10 jumbo shells cooked al dente
1. Prepare 1 pound hamburger
1. Take Half small onion, diced
1. Make ready 1 cup caesar dressing
1. Prepare 1/2 cup sour cream
1. Take 1/4 cup parmesan
1. Take Tsp black pepper
1. Get Tsp salt
1. Get Tsp garlic powder
1. Take Tsp onion powder
1. Make ready 1 cup spaghetti sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Stuffed Shells:

1. Preheat oven to 375. Brown hamburger with the onion and spices, drain grease
1. In medium bowl, combine dressing, sour cream and parmesan, mix well to combine
1. Add meat, mix well
1. Pour the spaghetti sauce in bottom of dish. Stuff shells with meat mixture and place in baking dish. Sprinkle top with parmesan if desired.
1. Bake 20 minutes until hot and bubbly. I sprinkled top with parsley for presentation.
