##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Prawn and Chicken Batter:

1. Make ready 200 grams prawns, cleaned, cut into several parts
1. Take 100 grams chicken breast, slice thinly then roughly cut
1. Make ready 2 garlics, smashed and chopped
1. Prepare 1 green onion, slice
1. Take as needed Flour
1. Prepare as needed Water
1. Take  Some salt
1. Make ready  Some pepper
1. Prepare  Chicken powder if prefered
1. Get  Oil for frying




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fried Prawn and Chicken Batter:

1. Add in the prawns, chicken, garlics, green onion and seasonings.
1. Add in the flour. Not too much but not too little. Enough so you can still taste the prawn and chicken but also emough to cover everything.
1. Then mix them together.
1. Add some water. Little by little, not too thick but not too runny either.
1. Taste test.
1. Heat the oil in a frying pan.
1. Take 2 tbsp of the batter. Fry them on both sides until golden brown.
1. Done!
