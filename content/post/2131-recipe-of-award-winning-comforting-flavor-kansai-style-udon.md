##### The ingredients needed to make Comforting Flavor Kansai-Style Udon:

1. Get 2 portions Frozen udon noodles
1. Make ready 1 Abura-age
1. Make ready 500 ml Water
1. Get 65 ml Shiro-dashi
1. Take 1 Bonito flakes
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Take 3 Green onions
1. Prepare 1 Grated ginger
1. Take 1 Shichimi spice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Comforting Flavor Kansai-Style Udon:

1. Chop the green onions into small pieces and cut the abura-age into 5mm wide pieces. Boil the water in a saucepan and add the shiro-dashi and salt.
1. Add the frozen udon and abura-age to the pot from Step 1 and bring it to a boil again. Cook the ingredients together for 2-3 minutes, then turn off the heat.
1. Serve it in bowls, and sprinkle with green onions and shichimi spice. Garnish with bonito flakes and grated raw ginger.
