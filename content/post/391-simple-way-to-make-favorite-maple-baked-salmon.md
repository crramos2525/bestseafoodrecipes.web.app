##### Steps to make Maple Baked Salmon:

1. preheat oven to 350°F
1. in small sauce pan, mix water, syrup, mustard, soy sauce, garlic and ginger and bring to a boil on stove
1. let simmer for 5 minutes, or until a half cup of mixture remains
1. place salmon fillets on baking sheet (parchment paper recommended )
1. brush mixture on fillets and bake for 8 to 10 minutes
1. repeat  step 5 two more times
1. Serve with rice and veggies.  Mixture will cover 4 large fillets (up to 6 servings)
