##### The ingredients needed to make Chili Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple kabobs:

1. Get 15 large shrimp
1. Take 1 Green pepper
1. Take 1/2 Fresh Pineapple
1. Make ready tbsp crushed garlic
1. Get dash salt
1. Take 1 cup Chili Teriyaki sauce
1. Make ready 5 small wooden kabobs sticks
1. Get 2 cup water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chili Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple kabobs:

1. Soak kabob sticks in water while preparing the food
1. Turn broiler on high
1. Rinse shrimp and pepper
1. Cut up the pepper and pineapple into small pieces
1. Mix Garlic and Chili Teriyaki sauce together
1. Start to assemble the kabob putting 3 shrimp 1 pineapple piece and 1 pepper piece on each kabob
1. Brush sauce on kabobs and place on second shelf of broiler so it&#39;s not directly under the broiler and cook to your doness
