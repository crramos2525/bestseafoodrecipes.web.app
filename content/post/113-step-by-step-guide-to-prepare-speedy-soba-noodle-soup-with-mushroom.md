##### The ingredients needed to make Soba Noodle Soup
With Mushroom:

1. Get 100 grams shimeji mushroom
1. Take 200 grams soba noodle (buckwheat)
1. Prepare  SOBA SOUP
1. Make ready 100 ml kikomon soup base sauce
1. Prepare 700 ml water
1. Get  GARNISH
1. Make ready 1 tsp otona no furikake ( soy flavour bonito and seaweed topping )

##### Instructions to make Soba Noodle Soup
With Mushroom:

1. SOBA NOODLE
1. bring water to a boil then add soba noodle simmer for 5 minute then cool in runny water then drain set aside
