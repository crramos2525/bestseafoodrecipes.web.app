##### The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Nori Salad:

1. Get 1 bunch (approximately 300 g) spinach
1. Make ready 1 sheet nori (edible seaweed looks like a piece of black paper)
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ground sesame seeds
1. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
1. Prepare 1-1.5 tbsp mentsuyu
1. Take 1 tsp sesame oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spinach and Nori Salad:

1. Mix all the condiments in a bowl.
1. Wash spinach well and boil in water with 2 dashes of salt. Drain the excess water of it.
1. Cut the spinach into 4 cm lengths. Add the spinach in the bowl and mix them well.
1. Tear a sheet of nori into large flakes. Add them in the bowl and mix them again. Now your spinach salad is ready.
