##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolatty Paan Mousse:

1. Get 1-1/2 cup Cream
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Icing sugar
1. Get 4/5 Paan leaves
1. Get 1/4 cup Gulkand
1. Make ready 1 tsp Agar agar or gelatine
1. Get 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chocolatty Paan Mousse:

1. Mix fresh cream 1cup,icing sugar, add crushed paan leaves in a bowl.
1. Add gulkand, gelatine mix (1tsp gelatin soaked with 5tsp water). Mix well.
1. Pour in moulds. Don&#39;t pour full, leave place for chocolate layer. Keep in freezer for about 1 hour.
1. Melt cooking chocolate(dark) by double boiler method or in microwave.
1. When chocolate melts, take out and mix with 1/2cup cream well.
1. Pour this mixture on set paan mousse and keep in freezer for few hours.
1. Unmould carefully and serve chilled.
