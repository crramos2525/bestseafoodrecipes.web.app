##### The ingredients needed to make Useful Braised Burdock &amp; Carrot:

1. Get 1 burdock root (wash well, cut into 3cm length pieces, cut it into 4 equal parts lengthwise)
1. Get 1 carrot (peeled, cut it about the same size as burdock root)
1. Make ready 1 handful dried bonito flakes (about 3~4 g)
1. Make ready 3 tbsp sake
1. Get 2 tbsp mirin
1. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
1. Get  soy sauce (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Useful Braised Burdock &amp; Carrot:

1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat, add burdock root and carrot and saute until they are coated with oil( maybe about 3~4 mins)
1. Add sake, mirin, dried bonito flakes and braise with a lid until become slightly soft and still have the crunchy texture. Be careful not to burn it.
