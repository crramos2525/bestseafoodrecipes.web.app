##### The ingredients needed to make Inari Sushi:

1. Take 5 Aburaage
1. Get 300 ml ◇Dashi stock
1. Prepare 2 tbsp ◇Light brown or brown sugar crystals
1. Get 1 tbsp ◇Mirin
1. Take 2 1/2 tbsp ◇Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 dash ◇Salt
1. Get 360 ml White rice
1. Prepare 5 cm  Kombu
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Sake
1. Take  [Mixed Vinegar]
1. Take 50 ml Rice vinegar
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Sugar
1. Take 1 tsp Salt

##### Steps to make Inari Sushi:

1. Cut the aburaage in half, open them up, and de-grease with a swift pour of hot water. Place them in a pot of the ◇ ingredients brought to a simmer. Simmer over medium heat and cool down as is in the mixture.
1. Wash the rice 30 minutes before cooking. Drain in a colander. Put rice, 360 ml water, sake, and konbu seaweed into the rice cooker and cook. When it&#39;s done, transfer into a handai (sushi rice tub) or a bowl. As you fan over the rice, add vinegar to the rice and cut it in with a spatula.
1. Squeeze the aburaage lightly to remove any liquid. Lightly squeeze a portion of the rice into barrels and stuff in the aburaage.
