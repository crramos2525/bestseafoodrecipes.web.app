##### Steps to make Gluten Free Prawn Pad Thai:

1. Stir fry your veg until soft, add in your prawns and 3/4 cook.
1. Add your sauce (chilli, garlic, ginger, soy, fish sauce, lemon and honey) and reduce, cooking the prawns through.
