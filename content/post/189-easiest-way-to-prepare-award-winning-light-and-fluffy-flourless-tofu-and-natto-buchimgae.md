##### The ingredients needed to make Light and Fluffy Flourless Tofu and Natto Buchimgae:

1. Get 1/2 block Silken tofu
1. Get 1 Egg
1. Prepare 1 pack Natto (with the accompanying sauces)
1. Make ready 1/2 bunch Garlic chives (cut into 2-3 cm pieces)
1. Prepare 1 tsp (Bonito flakes - optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Light and Fluffy Flourless Tofu and Natto Buchimgae:

1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl, and mix while mashing the tofu.
1. Thinly spread some sesame oil on the bottom of a (preferably non-stick) frying pan, and put in the mixture from step 1 a scoop of a ladle at a time. Pan fry until golden brown on one side without the lid, then flip over.
1. If you use an electric griddle, you can make several at once, and they will be ok if you leave them for a bit.
1. Serve with ponzu sauce or mayonnaise or doubanjiang to taste.
