##### The ingredients needed to make Stacked Baked Crab Sticks, Corns and Cheese:

1. Take 2 tbsp or less Frozen corns
1. Prepare 2 Crab sticks
1. Get 1 Sliced cheese
1. Get 1 as required Salt and pepper
1. Get 1 as required Dried parsley

##### Instructions to make Stacked Baked Crab Sticks, Corns and Cheese:

1. Cut the crab sticks into small pieces and quarter the sliced cheese.
1. Fry the crab sticks and corn kernels and season with salt and pepper. Divide into 4 portions.
1. Put 1/4 of Step 2 into a paper cup and lay a sliced cheese on top. Put another 1/4 of Step 2 and lay another sliced cheese on. Repeat.
1. The residual heat from the fried crab sticks and corn will melt the cheese.
