##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon with Sudachi Mayonnaise Sauce:

1. Get 4 slice Raw salmon
1. Get 1 Salt and pepper
1. Make ready 1 Plain flour
1. Take 1 clove Garlic
1. Take 1 tbsp Oil
1. Prepare  Sudachi Mayonnaise Sauce
1. Get 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Prepare 1 squeeze 1 sudachi  Sudachi (if not, lemon or other citrus fruits)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Sugar
1. Take 1 Krazy Salt (if not, salt and pepper)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Salmon with Sudachi Mayonnaise Sauce:

1. Rub salt and pepper gently onto the salmon. Coat with flour lightly. Mix the sudachi mayonnaise sauce ingredients.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add the garlic, and start pan-frying the salmon, skin side first. When the skin becomes crispy, flip, and continue pan-frying until cooked through.
1. Spread the sudachi mayonnaise sauce onto the salmon from Step 2, and it&#39;s done. You can add stir-fried vegetables on top if you want, which adds extra volume.
