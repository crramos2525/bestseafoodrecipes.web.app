##### Steps to make Squid Broccoli:

1. Wash, peel the skin and cut squid. Marinate with salt and sesame oil for 10 mins.
1. Wash and cut broccoli into florets.
1. Saute garlic and ginger until fragrant. Then add in broccoli. Stir fry well then add a little water. Cover to cook it easily.
1. When half done pour squid and mix well. Pour rice wine and salt. Cook it quickly, not to let squid over cook. Or else it will become hard
