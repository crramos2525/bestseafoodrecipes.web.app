1. Get 1 3/4 cup Heavy Cream
1. Get 2 tbsp Butter
1. Make ready 2 Shallots
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
1. Take 2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1. Take 1 clove Garlic
