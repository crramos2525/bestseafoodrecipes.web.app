##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Natto &amp; Bonito Flake Pasta Lunch:

1. Take 1 pack Natto
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Butter
1. Make ready 2 handful Bonito flakes
1. Make ready 1 Your favorite condiments (shredded nori seaweed, green onions etc...)

##### Instructions to make Easy Natto &amp; Bonito Flake Pasta Lunch:

1. Add 2/3 tablespoon of salt per 1 liter of water. Cook the pasta in boiling water.
1. In a bowl, add the natto and the sauce that comes with natto. Melt the butter in the microwave and add it to the natto. Mix until fluffy.
1. Once the pasta is cooked, take it out with a pair of tongs or chopsticks without draining, and add to the bowl in Step 2.
1. Serve the pasta on a serving plate and sprinkle a lot of bonito flakes on top, along with your favorite condiments and you&#39;re done.
