##### The ingredients needed to make Portabello Poppers:

1. Prepare 4 packages Portabello mushroom
1. Get 16 oz artificial Crab meat
1. Get 1/2 Green pepper
1. Prepare 2 bunch Green onions
1. Take 3 Japeleno peppers (or to taste)
1. Prepare 1 cup Mayo
1. Prepare 2 cup Mozzarella cheese
1. Make ready 1 cup Italian bread crumbs
1. Take 1/2 tsp each, garlic powder, salt, pepper
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1. Make ready 1 Olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Portabello Poppers:

1. Clean mushrooms and pull out stem. Set aside.
1. Process onions, peppers and crab to mince.
1. Combine all of ingredients except parm cheese and oil.
1. Stuff caps fully and place in large baking pan. Sprinkle with parm. Drizzle with oil.
1. Bake 400° for 35 mins.
