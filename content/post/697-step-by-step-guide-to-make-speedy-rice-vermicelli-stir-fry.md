##### The ingredients needed to make Rice Vermicelli Stir Fry:

1. Prepare 1 package rice vermicelli, soaked until they expand
1. Take 1/4 of clavaria mushroom, soak then clean, cut into mini cubes
1. Make ready 7 prawns, clean and then cut into several pieces
1. Take 2 eggs
1. Make ready 3 garlics, smash and chop
1. Prepare  Some salt
1. Make ready  Some pepper
1. Make ready  Chicken powder
1. Get  Oil for frying
1. Make ready  Water or broth if needed




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Rice Vermicelli Stir Fry:

1. Heat the oil in a frying pan.
1. Add in the garlics and the stir fry until fragrant.
1. Add in the eggs. Roughly scramble.
1. Add in the mushrooms. Stir everything together.
1. Add in the prawns. Stir quickly.
1. Then add in the vermicelli. Quickly mix everything.
1. Add the seasonings. Taste test.
1. Turn off the heat and serve.
