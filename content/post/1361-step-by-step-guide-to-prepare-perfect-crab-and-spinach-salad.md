##### The ingredients needed to make Crab and Spinach Salad:

1. Get 4 oz yogurt (vanilla adds flavor)
1. Take 5 slice thin of red onion chopped
1. Make ready 1/4 cup chives
1. Make ready 1 lb cooked shrimp (optional)
1. Get 1/2 tsp lemon juice
1. Prepare 3 dollops of light mayo
