##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Butter Mustard Fish:

1. Get 4 pieces Salmon Fish
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
1. Make ready 2 tbsp wholegrain mustard sauce
1. Make ready 1 green chilli
1. Get 2 tbsp lemon juice
1. Prepare 2 tsp fish masala
1. Get 1 tsp black pepper
1. Get 1 tbsp butter
1. Take as needed Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Roasted Butter Mustard Fish:

1. Take pan add butter,when is hot add ginger garlic paste and wholegrain mustard sauce mix well,than add fish masala and 1/4 cup water saute 1 minutes
1. Than put fish in pan add lemon juice,sprinkle black pepper,and salt mix well,cover it for 15 minutes for low ti medium flame,turn it over after 7 minutes
1. Check fish is cook or not,if cooked than flame high and roast with high flame until fish skin is crispy,than turn off the flame,
