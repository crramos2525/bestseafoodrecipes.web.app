##### The ingredients needed to make Avocado and Shrimp Salad:

1. Get 1 Avocado
1. Make ready 50 grams Small shrimp
1. Prepare 2 tsp Lemon juice
1. Get 1 dash Salt
1. Prepare 1 dash Pepper
1. Take 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Sake




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Avocado and Shrimp Salad:

1. Cut the avocado in half. Remove the seed, then cut into 1 cm chunks. Sprinkle half of the lemon juice on top.
1. Sprinkle the sake onto the small shrimp, dry fry in a pan, heat through, then let cool.
1. Add the avocado from Step 1 to the shrimp from Step 2, then add salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Mix lightly. Finally, add the mayonnaise, mix, and it&#39;s done!!
