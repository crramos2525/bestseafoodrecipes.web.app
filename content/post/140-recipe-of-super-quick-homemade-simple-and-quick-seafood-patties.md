##### The ingredients needed to make Simple and Quick Seafood Patties:

1. Get 1 box Stove Top stuffing mix
1. Prepare 2 cup Tuna, crab or lobster  (flaked or chunked)
1. Get 1/3 cup Finely chopped onion
1. Prepare 1/3 cup Finely chopped celery
1. Get 1/3 cup Mayonaise
1. Get 2 eggs ( beaten)
1. Get 3/4 cup water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Simple and Quick Seafood Patties:

1. Mix all ingredients - refrigerate 10-15 minutes
1. form into patties of desired size
1. heat 2 tbsp olive oil and 1 tbsp butter in large skillet
1. Fry patties 4-5 minutes on either side until golden brown
