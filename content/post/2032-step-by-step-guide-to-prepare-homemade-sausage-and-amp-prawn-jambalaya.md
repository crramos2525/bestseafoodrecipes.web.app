##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage &amp; Prawn Jambalaya:

1. Take 1 ring of chorizo or 1 pack of cooking chorizo
1. Get 1 onion
1. Get  Cooking oil (I use sunflower)
1. Make ready 2 sticks celery
1. Prepare 2 green peppers
1. Make ready 1 tsp dried marjoram
1. Get 2 tsp cajun seasoning
1. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
1. Take 400 g long grain rice
1. Take 1 litre chicken stock (made from oxo cubes is fine)
1. Make ready 400 g cooked / ready to eat smoked sausage
1. Make ready 1 (400 g) tin of chopped tomatoes
1. Make ready 200-250 g cooked king prawns
1. Take 6 spring onions
1. Prepare Handful fresh parsley

##### Steps to make Sausage &amp; Prawn Jambalaya:

1. Dice the chorizo and fry without added oil, it should release plenty of oil if you start with a cold pan (you&#39;ll need a big saucepan or stock pot with a lid). Once it&#39;s well browned, take the chorizo out and place on some kitchen roll. Keep the oil for the next step.
1. Chop up the onion, garlic, celery and peppers and fry in the chorizo oil, add extra cooking oil if needed. Add the marjoram and cajun seasoning and continue until softened.
1. Add the rice and stir well while frying for a minute. Pour in the chicken stock and bring to the boil then cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
1. Dice the sausage and add to the pan along with the chorizo and chopped tomatoes. Cook for another 5 to 10 minutes with the lid on until the liquid is absorbed.
1. Mix in the prawns and cover with the heat off for a minute or two. This should be enough to heat the prawns through without overcooking them.
1. Serve with a generous amount of mixed chopped spring onions and parsley.
