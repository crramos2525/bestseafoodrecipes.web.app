##### The ingredients needed to make 5 Ingredients Dinner
Crab&amp;Corn soup:

1. Prepare  Fresh crab meat (or in brine) as desired
1. Make ready 1 cup spring onion, thinly sliced
1. Prepare 300 ml chicken stock
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped

1. The 5 ingredients
