##### The ingredients needed to make Yummy! Leek Okonomiyaki:

1. Make ready 2 to 3 stalks Japanese leek
1. Prepare 2 cm Nagaimo yam
1. Make ready 1 Egg
1. Prepare 1 Dried shrimp (if you have it)
1. Take 1 tsp Dashi stock granules
1. Get 4 tbsp Flour




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Yummy! Leek Okonomiyaki:

1. Chop the leek into largish pieces. Grate the nagaimo yam.
1. Combine the leek, grated yam, egg, dried shrimp, and dashi granules in a bowl, and mix together. Add the flour and mix.
1. Heat oil in a pan.  Spread the batter from step 2 evenly into the pan.  Cover with a lid and cook over low heat for about 5 minutes until golden brown.
1. Turn the pancake over and cook without a lid until golden brown, and you are finished. Garnish the finished okonomiyaki with your choice of ingredients.
1. Of course you can make this with cabbage instead of leeks.
