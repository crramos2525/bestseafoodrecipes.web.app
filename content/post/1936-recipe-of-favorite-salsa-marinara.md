##### Instructions to make Salsa Marinara:

1. Chop onions.
1. Fry chopped onions till they turn to a light brown.
1. Add all the seafood.
1. Add white wine.
1. Leave on medium heat for 10 mins.
1. Add tomato puree, olives, capers and bay leaves.
