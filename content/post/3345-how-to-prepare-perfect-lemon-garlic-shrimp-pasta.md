##### Steps to make Lemon garlic shrimp pasta:

1. Prep: Julienne the onion. Mince garlic. Cut spinach. Boil the corn and take it off the cob. Deshell, clean, devein the prawns.
1. Boil the pasta in 2 tbsps rock salt and water until soft. Drain it in a colander once done.
1. Heat a pan. Add 2 tsps butter and 1 tsps olive oil.
1. Add the onions. Cook until translucent. Add garlic. Cook until onions brown and garlic starts sticking to the bottom of the pan.
1. Add the prawns. Add 1/2 tbsp of salt. Cook until they turn pink. Add chilli flakes and pepper. Cook for another few minutes until most of the water evaporates.
1. Optional step: Add spinach and corn. Cook until the spinach wilts.
1. Add the pasta now. Mix it. Add lemon juice, coriander, 2 tsps of butter and the cheese. Mix thoroughly.
1. Serve with garlic bread.
