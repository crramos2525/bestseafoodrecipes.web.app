##### The ingredients needed to make Western-style Oshi Sushi (Pressed Sushi):

1. Make ready 350 grams ＊Cooked rice
1. Prepare 2 tbsp ＊Sushi vinegar
1. Get 1 tbsp ＊Grainy Dijon mustard
1. Make ready 10 slice Smoked salmon
1. Make ready 1 Cucumber
1. Make ready 1 ● Egg
1. Get 1 tsp ● Sugar
1. Get 1 Mayonnaise

##### Steps to make Western-style Oshi Sushi (Pressed Sushi):

1. Combine all the ＊ ingredients and let cool.
1. Heat some oil in a frying pan and cook the ● ingredients to make scrambled eggs.
1. Slice the cucumber with a vegetable peeler into long, thin shavings.
1. Cut the salmon into roughly the width as the cucumber slices (you could skip this step if you wanted).
1. Arrange on a plastic wrap, like this. Cut the cucumbers as needed before positioning them.
1. Press the mixed ingredients from Step 5 into a mold. Drizzle mayonnaise from one side to the other. Stack in this order: sushi rice from Step 1, scrambled eggs from Step 2, and sushi rice from Step 1.
1. Close the ends of the plastic wrap in Step 6. Put the lid on the mold and press tightly.
