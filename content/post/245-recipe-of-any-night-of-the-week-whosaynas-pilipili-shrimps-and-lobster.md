##### The ingredients needed to make Whosayna’s Pilipili Shrimps and Lobster:

1. Take 1 kg Jumbo Prawns and Lobster (Shelled or Deshelled with tails)
1. Get 2 tsp Kashmiri Chilli powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp Paprika
1. Get 1 and half tsp Salt
1. Take pinch Turmeric powder
1. Get 1-2 tsp Lemon juice
1. Make ready 1 tsp Garlic paste
1. Get 3-4 tbsp Butter
1. Get 1 tsp Cornflour
1. Get 1 tbsp Oil

##### Instructions to make Whosayna’s Pilipili Shrimps and Lobster:
