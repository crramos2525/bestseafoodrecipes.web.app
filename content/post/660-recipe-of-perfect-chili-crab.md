##### The ingredients needed to make Chili Crab:

1. Get 1/2 tsp Chinese 4 spice
1. Take 1 head Garlic
1. Get 1/4 cup Oyster Sauce
1. Take 1/4 cup Banana Catsup
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Soda
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
1. Get 5 stick Green Finger Chili
1. Prepare 2 stick Onion leeks or Spring Onion
1. Take 1 tbsp Veg. Oil

##### Steps to make Chili Crab:
