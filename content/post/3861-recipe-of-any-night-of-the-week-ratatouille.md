##### The ingredients needed to make Ratatouille:

1. Take 1−２ eggplants
1. Take 1 big bell pepper (or 4 small green bell peppers)
1. Make ready 1 zucchini
1. Take 1 can canned tomato (diced)
1. Get  some oregano
1. Prepare  pepper and salt
1. Take  some olive oil
1. Prepare  (optional) an onion, bacon/ham or a canned tuna




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Ratatouille:

1. Cut the eggplants in small cubes. Spread the olive oil in a pot and fry them.
1. Cut the bell pepper(s) and add to the pot to fry together.
1. Cut the zucchini and add to the pot.
1. Add the canned tomato, the pepper, salt and the oregano in the pot. Cook for about ten minutes.
1. I use a pot warmer so that it continues cooking slowly until meal time.
