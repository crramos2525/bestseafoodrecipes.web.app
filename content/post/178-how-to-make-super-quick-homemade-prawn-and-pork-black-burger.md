##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn and Pork Black Burger:

1. Get 400 g minced pork 20% fat content
1. Prepare 200 g raw king prawns roughly chopped
1. Get 4 Spring Onions sliced
1. Get Handful bean sprouts
1. Prepare 4 tbsp oyster sauce
1. Make ready 1 tbsp far soy sauce
1. Get 1 tbsp rice wine
1. Prepare 1 tsp mirin
1. Prepare 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Prawn and Pork Black Burger:

1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl and squeeze the mixture between your fingers until the pork mince fat begins to make strands
1. Take a handful, about the size of a tennis ball and roll into a ball
1. Flatten the balls out onto a piece of parchment paper and shape into burger patties
1. Heat a cast iron pan to maximum heat on the hob
1. Season each pattie with salt and cracked pepper
1. Cook the burgers for 3 minutes each side or until thoroughly cooked through
1. Once cooked rest them for 5 minutes before placing them I toasted brioche buns with all your favourite trimmings. For example crispy fried shallot rings as I have done in the photo
