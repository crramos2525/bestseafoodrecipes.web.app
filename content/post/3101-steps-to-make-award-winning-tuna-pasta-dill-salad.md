##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Pasta Dill Salad:

1. Get 1-16 ounce box pasta (any shape you like)
1. Prepare 1 cup mayonnaise
1. Prepare 1 cup plain yogurt
1. Get 5 teaspoons dried dill weed
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons milk
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1. Take 2-5 ounce can tuna fish in water (drain water)
