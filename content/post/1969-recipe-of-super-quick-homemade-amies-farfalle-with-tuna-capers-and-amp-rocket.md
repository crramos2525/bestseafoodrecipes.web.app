1. Make ready 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
1. Take 1 finely grated zest and juice of 1lemon
1. Make ready 425 grams can good-quality tuna in olive oil, drained and flaked
1. Make ready 80 grams wild rocket
1. Prepare 1 tbsp baby capers, rinsed
1. Prepare 1 salt and freshly ground black pepper
