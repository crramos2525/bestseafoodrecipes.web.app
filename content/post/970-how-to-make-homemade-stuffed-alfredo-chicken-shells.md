##### Instructions to make Stuffed Alfredo Chicken Shells:

1. Make large shells as directed on box
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
1. Mix chicken, broccoli, mushrooms, 1/2 cup of parmesan, and 3/4 of Alfredo sauce
1. Put some of the Alfredo Sauce at the bottom of a large baking dish
1. Fill cooked shells with chicken mixture and place shells into baking dish
1. Top shells with leftover 1/2 cup parmesan
1. Bake in oven at 375 degrees for 30 minutes
