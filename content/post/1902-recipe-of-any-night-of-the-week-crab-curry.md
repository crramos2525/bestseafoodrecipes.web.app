##### Steps to make Crab curry:

1. Add oil in the pan,gingelly oil is recommended. Once the oil got heated add curry leaves and the grinded paste
1. Saute the paste until the raw smell gone and add some water, add chopped tomato and salt as required and fry the tomato until it becomes soft and mushy. Check the taste for salt and spices at this point and correct it.
1. Once the tomatoes are well cooked add crabs into the gravy and cover the lid for 7 to 10 mins approximately.
