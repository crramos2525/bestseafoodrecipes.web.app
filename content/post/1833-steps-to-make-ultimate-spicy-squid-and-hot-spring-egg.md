##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Squid and Hot Spring Egg:

1. Make ready 250 grams Sashimi grade squid
1. Get 1 tbsp ● All-purpose Korean seasoning
1. Make ready 2 Hot spring egg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Spicy Squid and Hot Spring Egg:

1. [&#34;Hanakiri&#34; Squid] Make vertical slits 3/4 of the way through the meat 2 mm apart. Cut each squid in half vertically. Then, make horizontal slits across the squids.
1. Add the ● seasoning ingredients to the squid and mix together. Chill in the refrigerator.
1. After you make the hot spring egg, chill in the refrigerator. Then, transfer it to the plate with the squid. Combine with the squid before serving.
