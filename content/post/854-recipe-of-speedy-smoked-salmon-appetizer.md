##### The ingredients needed to make Smoked Salmon Appetizer:

1. Prepare 3/4 cup finely chopped white onion
1. Make ready 3 tbsp drained capers
1. Get 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
1. Get 4 tsp dijon mustard
1. Take 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1. Take 8 oz cream cheese (room temperature)
1. Prepare 24 thin slices of sourdough baguette
1. Take 6 oz thinly sliced smoked salmon, cut into 24 pieces




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Smoked Salmon Appetizer:

1. stir together first 5 ingredients in a bowl to make relish, season with salt and pepper
1. spread cream cheese onto bread, add 1 piece of salmon, top with relish
1. serve on serving platter and enjoy
