##### The ingredients needed to make Sig&#39;s Salmon and Prawn Pasties:

1. Make ready 1 piece cooked salmon
1. Make ready 125 gr cooked, large prawns
1. Make ready 75 gr uncooked bacon bits
1. Prepare 2 teaspoons mayonaise
1. Make ready 1 sheet best ready puff pastry
1. Prepare 1 egg
1. Make ready 2 pinch each of garlic salt and fresh ground peppet




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sig&#39;s Salmon and Prawn Pasties:

1. Preheat oven to 175C
1. Chop the prawns up a bit, flake the fish gently.
1. Mix all the ingredients and seasonings, except the egg and the pastry
1. Lay out the pastry and cut to dessertplate size circles
1. Top the circles with the mix in middle, gently pull one half of pastry over the mix and create pasty, seal well
1. Whisk the egg, brush this over the pasty.
1. Bake in oven until golden brown, remove.
1. Either eat hot or cold, hope you enjoy
