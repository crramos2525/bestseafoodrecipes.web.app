##### The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese &amp; Tuna Paste:

1. Get 70 grams Cream cheese
1. Take 1 Canned tuna (small - 80 g)
1. Get 1 tsp Basil paste (basil sauce)
1. Get 1 tsp Lemon juice
1. Take 1 tbsp Red or white wine




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cream Cheese &amp; Tuna Paste:

1. Soften the cream cheese in a microwave, drain the oil well from the canned tuna until it weights roughly 50 g.
1. Mix the cream cheese and tuna until smooth.
1. Add the basil pesto, wine, and lemon juice. Mix until everything is blended.
