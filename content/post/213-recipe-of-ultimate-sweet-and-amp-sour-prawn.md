##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet &amp; Sour Prawn:

1. Get 250 g Prawn

1. Make ready 1 tea spoon Cooking wine
1. Get 1 tea spoon Fish sauce
1. Get 2 table spoon Tomato &amp; Chili sauce
1. Get  Garlic chopped to dice

##### Steps to make Sweet &amp; Sour Prawn:

1. Marinated prawn with salt, cooking wine, white pepper powder and fish sauce.
1. Heat cooking oil in pan, put in garlic and big onion, fry until fragance come out.
