##### The ingredients needed to make Adobong Pusit:

1. Take 1 kg baby squid
1. Take tbsp cooking oil
1. Prepare 1 clove garlic
1. Take 1 small onion, chopped
1. Take 1 bunch silantro
1. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
1. Prepare 2 tbsp vinegar
1. Take 1 tsp sugar
1. Prepare 1 pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Adobong Pusit:

1. Saute garlic onion in pan
1. Add squid , vinegar and soy sauce let it simmer for 3 -4 mins 🐙🐙🐙
