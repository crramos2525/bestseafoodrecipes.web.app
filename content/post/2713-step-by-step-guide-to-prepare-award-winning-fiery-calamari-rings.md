##### The ingredients needed to make Fiery Calamari Rings:

1. Make ready 100 gms squid (calamari)
1. Prepare 1 cup bread crumbs
1. Prepare 1 egg beaten well
1. Take 1/2 cup all purpose flour
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
1. Get As required Oil for deep frying




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fiery Calamari Rings:

1. Clean the calamari well. Cut into rings.
1. Mix chilli powder and salt together with the all purpose flour. Dip the calamari rings in the flour once. Then dip in egg mixture.
1. Next dip in bread crumbs. Repeat for all rings. Place on a plate in the fridge for 5 mins.
1. Deep fry each ring until it turns golden brown. Calamari takes less time to cook. So check one ring before frying all.
1. Serve hot calamari rings with ketchup.
