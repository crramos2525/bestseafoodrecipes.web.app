##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Glazed Salmon:

##### Steps to make Sweet Glazed Salmon:

1. Rub salmon with lemon pepper seasoning &amp; set aside.
1. Mix all the other ingredients (except salmon &amp; lemon pepper) in a small bowl.
1. Pour 1/2 a cup of the mixture into a sauce pan and warm over low heat stirring occasionally.
1. Grill salmon for 5 minutes on medium then brush with the remaining mixture.
1. Continue cooking 10-15 minutes with the cover down, basting occasionally.
1. Serve with the warmed sauce.
