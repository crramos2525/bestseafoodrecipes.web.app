##### The ingredients needed to make Cabbage and Imitation Crab Lemon-Mayonnaise Salad:

1. Prepare 5 leaves Cabbage
1. Take 1 packages Imitation Crab (flaked)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Get 2 tsp Bottled lemon juice
1. Get 1 White sesame (to taste)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cabbage and Imitation Crab Lemon-Mayonnaise Salad:

1. Chop the cabbage into small pieces, place into a microwave-safe bowl, and microwave.
1. Mix the shredded imitation crab, bottled lemon juice, and mayonnaise to the ingredients from step 1.
1. Chill, and serve after adding white sesame to taste.
