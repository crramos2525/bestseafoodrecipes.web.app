##### Instructions to make Okonomiyaki:

1. Mix hondashi into water, then combine with flour and egg in a bowl. Mix until smooth.
1. Cut cabbage into thin bite sized pieces. Cut pork into easy to eat pieces.* Add the cabbage, pork*, and tempura balls into the mixture from step one and mix so everything is coated in batter.
1. Add oil to fry pan and pour the mixture in, making a circular shaped patty. Cook on low to medium heat until the bottom is well cooked (dark brown but not burnt), then flip it carefully and continue cooking, reducing the heat.
1. Cook until cooked through (use a toothpick to test that the batter in the centre is cooked)
1. Serve on a plate and top with okonomiyaki sauce, mayonnaise, katsuoboshi, and aonori to taste.
