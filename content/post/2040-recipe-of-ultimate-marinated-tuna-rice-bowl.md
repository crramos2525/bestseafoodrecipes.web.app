##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Tuna Rice Bowl:

1. Prepare 200 grams Fresh tuna (sashimi quality)
1. Take 1 1/2 tbsp ★Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp ★Mirin
1. Take 1 tbsp ★Sake
1. Take 1 Kinshi tamago (finely shredded thin omelet)
1. Take 700 grams Sushi rice
1. Take 1 Sweetened vinegar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Marinated Tuna Rice Bowl:

1. Make the sushi rice. Cut the tuna up into easy to eat pieces, and marinate the ★ ingredients for about 20 minutes.
1. Put the sushi rice in a bowl, top with the marinated tuna, scatter nori seaweed, and put wasabi on the side.
