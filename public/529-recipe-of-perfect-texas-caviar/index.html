The ingredients needed to make Texas Caviar:
  1. Make ready 2 small cans white corn
  2. Take 1-15 oz can black beans
  3. Prepare 1-15 oz can pinto beans
  4. Prepare 1-15 oz can garbonzo beans (chick peas)
  5. Make ready 1 jar chopped/diced pimentos
  6. Make ready 1 cup chopped celery
  7. Prepare 1 green pepper chopped
  8. Make ready 1 red onion chopped
  9. Prepare Sauce
  10. Take 1/2 tsp pepper
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup olive oil
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp water
  13. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  14. Get 1 tsp salt
  15. Get 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
