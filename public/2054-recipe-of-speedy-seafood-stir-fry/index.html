The ingredients needed to make Seafood Stir Fry:
  1. Make ready shrimps
  2. Take calamari
  3. Get mix veggies
  4. Get onion diced
  5. Get salt
  6. Make ready knorr cube
  7. Get fresh pepper diced
  8. Take olive oil
Steps to make Seafood Stir Fry:
  1. Wash shrimps and calamari and marinate with salt, black pepper and any spice of your choice
  2. Remove the mix veggies from the pack and place in the sieve to allow excess water drain out
  3. Heat up frying pan and once its hot, add olive oil and the diced onions and stir
  4. Add the shrimp and calamari and stir fry till they are cooked through, add knorr, salt, fresh pepper and then the mix veggies
  5. Stir fry for max 5minutes and remove from heat and serve with what ever you wish to enjoy it with
