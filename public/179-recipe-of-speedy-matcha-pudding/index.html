The ingredients needed to make Matcha Pudding:
  1. Get Matcha Pudding Ingredients
  2. Make ready 7 gram plain Agar Agar powder (1 sachet)
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Take 2 spoons butter
  5. Take 125 grams sugar
  6. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  7. Make ready 400 ml Water
  8. Take 500 ml Fresh milk
  9. Prepare 2 spoons corn starch
  10. Make ready 5 spoons Matcha powder
  11. Take Vla ingredients:
  12. Make ready 250 ml Fresh milk
  13. Take 60 grams sugar
  14. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons corn starch
  15. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  16. Get Rum (to taste)
Steps to make Matcha Pudding:
  1. For the Matcha Pudding: Wishk the egg then add all the pudding ingredients. Boil it with medium heat (don't stop stirring). Pour into the pudding mold. Then chill it in the fridge.
  2. For the Vla: Wishk the egg yolk first then add all the Vla ingredients except the Rum (add it at the end off the process). Boil it with medium heat (don't stop stirring). Turn off the stove for 1 minutes then add the Rum (to taste).
