The ingredients needed to make Pepper bass & asparagus squid:
  1. Prepare 250 grams bass
  2. Get 5 grams pepper
  3. Prepare 500 grams squid
  4. Prepare 250 grams asparragus
  5. Take 5 garlics
  6. Prepare 1 midsize onion
  7. Get 50 grams walnut
  8. Get 50 grams parmesan cheese
  9. Make ready 20 ml lemon juice

Instructions to make Pepper bass & asparagus squid:
  1. Chop the garlic, onion and asparagus. Use a low oiled pan to cook the mix around 15 minutes.
  2. Chop the squid, add It to the mix and cook till the water from the squid evaporates (personally recomend a wok to do it)
  3. Sprinkle pepper over the bass and cook it using a flat pan with olive oil for 10 minutes aprox. It should be turned to cook both sides, squeeze a lemon before turning It to mix the flavours of both ingredientes.
  4. After serving the bass and the squid smash the walnut and mince the parmesan cheese, mix them and sprikle over the squid with asparagus.
  5. Enjoy!!
