The ingredients needed to make Salmon Potato Foil Packets:
  1. Get 1 salmon filet per foil packet
  2. Prepare 2 red potatoes per foil packet
  3. Get 1/4 yellow onion sliced per foil packet
  4. Get 2 tbsp butter per foil packet
  5. Get 1 tbsp olive oil per foil packet
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp minced garlic per foil packet
  7. Get to taste Salt & pepper
  8. Get 1/4 tsp old bay seasoning
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
Instructions to make Salmon Potato Foil Packets:
  1. Begin by peeling potatoes, slicing them in thin slices. Lay out a large enough piece of tin foil to wrap up everything. Add potatoes onto piece of tin foil. Sprinkle potatoes with olive oil.
  2. Now add salmon filet to the top of potatoes & rub minced garlic onto the top of filet. Now squeeze lemon juice onto filet.
  3. Then take your sliced onion & place ontop of potatoes & filet. Season with salt, pepper & old bay seasoning. Divide butter into pats & place ontop potatoes & filet.
  4. Preheat oven to 400°. Wrap up packet tightly so all seams are closed so the juices do not come out of foil packet. Place on a cookie sheet, put in oven for 30-35 mins or until potatoes are tender & salmon is fully cooked. Enjoy!
