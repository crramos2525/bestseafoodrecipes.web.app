The ingredients needed to make King crab low down:
  1. Get 2 5 ounce bags Louisiana crab boil
  2. Make ready 1 12ounce beer of your choice
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup ground cayenne pepper
  4. Take 4 garlic cloves peeled
  5. Get 3 lemons halved
  6. Take 1 large onion peeled and quartered
  7. Take 5 potatoes cleaned and halved
  8. Make ready 5 ears corn on Cobb cleaned and halved
  9. Take 5 Louisiana hot links halved (or sausage of your choice)
  10. Prepare 8 eggs
  11. Make ready 3 1/2 lb king crab legs
  12. Get 2 lb large deveined shrimp with shell on
  13. Make ready 1 lb mussel meat
