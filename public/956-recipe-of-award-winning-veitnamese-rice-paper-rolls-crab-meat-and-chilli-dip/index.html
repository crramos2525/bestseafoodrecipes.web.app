The ingredients needed to make Veitnamese Rice paper Rolls, crab meat and chilli dip:
  1. Prepare 6 rice papers
  2. Get 50 gm rice noodles
  3. Make ready 6 crab sticks
  4. Prepare 20 gm mint
  5. Prepare 20 gm Corrainder
  6. Prepare 20 gm spring onion
  7. Make ready 1 red chili
  8. Make ready 1 cucumber
  9. Prepare 20 gm leaves
  10. Get 1 tomato
  11. Take 20 peppers
  12. Make ready For chili dip :-
  13. Prepare 3 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  14. Get 1 tsp Soya
  15. Make ready 2 tbsp tamrind
  16. Get 1 tsp fish sauce
  17. Make ready 1 garlic clove
Steps to make Veitnamese Rice paper Rolls, crab meat and chilli dip:
  1. Cut thin juliene of all vegetables.
  2. Dip rice sheets in water for. 10 sec. And use wet cloth.
  3. Wrap all vegetable in the paper.
  4. Make rolls tights. For sauce mix all ingredients togther.
  5. Garnish with sauce and greens.
