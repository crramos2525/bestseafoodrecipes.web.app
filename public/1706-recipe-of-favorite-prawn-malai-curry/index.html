The ingredients needed to make Prawn malai curry:
  1. Get 500 gram prawn
  2. Prepare 2 Onions grinded into smooth paste
  3. Get 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Get 1 cup desiccated coconut
  5. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  8. Take 1 tsp cumin seed
  9. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  10. Take 1 dry red chilli whole
  11. Make ready 1 handful whole garam masala
  12. Take To taste salt and sugar
  13. Get As needed oil
  14. Take As needed coconut milk

Instructions to make Prawn malai curry:
  1. Marinate the prawns with salt and turmeric powder
  2. Heat oil temper Cumin seeds,bay leaf red chilli and whole garam masala
  3. Add prawns and shallow fry
  4. Add onion and ginger garlic paste and saute well adding salt and turmeric powder
  5. Add red chilli Powder and coconut
  6. Saute well adding coconut milk
  7. Cover it and keep the heat low till the prawns get soft
  8. Finally add sugar and garam masala powder
  9. Serve it with plain rice or chapati
