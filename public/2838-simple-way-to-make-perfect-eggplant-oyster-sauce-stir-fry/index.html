The ingredients needed to make Eggplant Oyster Sauce Stir Fry:
  1. Get 2 (around 1 lbs) medium Chinese eggplants, cut into long sticks of about 2 inches (5cm)
  2. Make ready 3 Tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Prepare 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Take 8 cloves shallots, thinly sliced
  5. Prepare 2 inches (5 cm) ginger, finely minced or grated
  6. Prepare 5 bird eye chili peppers, sliced(optional)
  7. Prepare 2 medium tomatoes, cut into small wedges
  8. Make ready 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  9. Take 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  10. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt or to taste
  11. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  12. Take 200 ml water
