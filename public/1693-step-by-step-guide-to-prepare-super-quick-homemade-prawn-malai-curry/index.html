The ingredients needed to make Prawn malai curry:
  1. Take 2 Prawn pieces fried
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Ginger paste
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Onion paste
  4. Take 1 tsp Garlic paste
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  6. Take 2 tbsp Coriander leave chopped
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp Coconut paste
  8. Get 2 tbsp Coconut milk
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  10. Get 1 tsp Sugar
  11. Get 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp Mustard seed paste
  13. Get 1 tbsp Popy paste
  14. Get 1 Green & red chilli
  15. Prepare 1/2 tea spoon Panch foron/ five spices
  16. Prepare 1 Bay leaf
  17. Make ready 5 tbsp Mustard oil
  18. Get 1 cup water
