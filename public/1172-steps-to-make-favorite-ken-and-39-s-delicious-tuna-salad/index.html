The ingredients needed to make Ken's Delicious Tuna Salad:
  1. Take 8-10 oz Tuna (oil-packed preferred, but not required)
  2. Get 1/2 c mayonnaise
  3. Take 1-2 Tbsp celery, finely chopped
  4. Make ready 1/2 Tbsp whole-grain Mustard
  5. Prepare 1/2 Tbsp Honey Mustard
  6. Make ready 1 tsp parsley
  7. Make ready 2 Tbsp Dill Relish (not sweet)
  8. Make ready Pepper (to taste)
