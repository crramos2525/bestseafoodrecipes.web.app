The ingredients needed to make Dark beans tuna salad:
  1. Take 1 can tuna drained
  2. Prepare 2/3 can red dark kidney beans
  3. Take 1/2 cup parsley
  4. Take 2 green pepper
  5. Get 1/2 onion
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp white soy sauce
  8. Take 1 tbsp lemon juice
  9. Take Black pepper for taste
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp mustard
