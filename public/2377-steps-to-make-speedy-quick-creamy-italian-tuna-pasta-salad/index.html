The ingredients needed to make Quick Creamy Italian Tuna Pasta Salad:
  1. Take 1 box (16 ounce) pasta, any shape
  2. Get 1 cup mixed veggies (frozen)
  3. Make ready 1 cup mayonnaise
  4. Take 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoons onion powder
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 envelope Good Seasons Italian Dressing (dry)
  9. Take 1 can tuna fish in water (drained)
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
Instructions to make Quick Creamy Italian Tuna Pasta Salad:
  1. Boil pasta according to package directions……add mixed veggies 4 minutes before pasta is done cooking….
  2. Drain pasta and mix veggies, add cold water to pasta mix and drain well, return to pan….
  3. Next add : Mayonnaise, sweet pickle relish, onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper, envelope of Good Seasons Italian Dressing, can of drained tuna fish and milk, mix all well….
  4. After mixing everything place into refrigerator and chill for about 2 hours…..
