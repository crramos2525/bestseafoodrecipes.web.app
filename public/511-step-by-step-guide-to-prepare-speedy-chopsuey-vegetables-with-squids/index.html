The ingredients needed to make Chopsuey (Vegetables with Squids):
  1. Make ready Salt and Pepper
  2. Prepare 2 tsp sugar
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp soy sauce
  4. Get 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  5. Make ready Garlic and onion diced
  6. Make ready 1 sliced red bell pepper
  7. Make ready 1 canned young corn
  8. Take 1 canned quail eggs
  9. Get 1 cup sliced carrots
  10. Make ready 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  11. Get 1 cup sliced cabbage
  12. Prepare 3 cups squid
  13. Take 1 cup fish balls
  14. Get 1 cup water
  15. Prepare 3 tbsp corn starch
