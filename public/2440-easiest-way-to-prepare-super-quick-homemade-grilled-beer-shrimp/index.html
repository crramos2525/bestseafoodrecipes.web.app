  3. Get 1/4 cup old bay seasoning (more if you prefer)

Instructions to make Grilled beer shrimp:
  1. Pour the cans of beer in large bowl and add old bay.
  2. Add shrimp to the beer and marinate for at least 6 hours. Overnight is better.
  3. Put shrimp on skewers and grill until they are pink.
