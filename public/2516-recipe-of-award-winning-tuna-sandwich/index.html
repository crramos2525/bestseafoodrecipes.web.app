The ingredients needed to make Tuna Sandwich:
  1. Get 2 wheat bread, rolls
  2. Prepare 170 g tuna in oil, equivalent to 1 can
  3. Prepare 4 tablespoons chilli mayonnaise, preferably Mazola
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons corn
  5. Take 4 pickles, sliced lenghtwise
  6. Take 2 lettuce leaves
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps to make Tuna Sandwich:
  1. Cut the bread rolls and fill each one of them with half of the tuna.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of lemon juice on the tuna in each roll.
  3. Press the bread rolls in the sandwich toaster for 2 minutes or until bread rolls are brown (Don't over-toast them, they would be hard to handle).
  4. Re-open each roll, squeeze in mayonnaise, spread sweet corn, arrange pickles and finally put the lettuce on top.
  5. Fold bread roll and serve.
