The ingredients needed to make Mushrooms,broccoli and smoked salmon pastas sauce:
  1. Prepare 150 grams mushrooms
  2. Get 100 grams smoked salmon cut in small pieces
  3. Prepare 150 grams broccoli
  4. Get 250 ml single cream
  5. Make ready Salt
  6. Prepare Fresh ground black pepper
  7. Get 2 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
  8. Make ready White wine
  9. Prepare Fresh parsley

Steps to make Mushrooms,broccoli and smoked salmon pastas sauce:
  1. Take a sauce pan and put the 2 Tbs of olive oil
  2. Add the mushrooms and fry the a bit together with the olive oil until soft. Are going to let a bit of water but no worries. Add a bit of white wine and let the alcohol evaporate.
  3. Add the salmon to the mushrooms and let it fry a bit until pink colour. Add the brocolli and let it simmering for like few minutes. I put frozen broccoli cause is easier to cook and will not take too long to cook it.
  4. Now put the single cream and mix well with the brocolli, mushrooms and salmon. Add salt and fresh ground black pepper. And garnish with fresh parsley.
