The ingredients needed to make Skinny avacado tuna salad:
  1. Prepare 1 can tuna, drained
  2. Prepare 1 avocado
  3. Make ready 1/2 celery stick, chopped
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped almonds
  5. Get 3 tbsp relish
  6. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
