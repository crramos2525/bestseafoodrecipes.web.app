The ingredients needed to make Jambalaya:
  1. Get 1 can (28 ounce) diced tomatoes
  2. Prepare 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  3. Make ready 1 lb andouille sausage (polska kilbasa, or summer sausage work)
  4. Get 1 large white onion, chopped
  5. Get 2 clove garlic, minced
  6. Make ready 2 tsp dried oregano
  7. Take 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
  8. Take 1 cup celery, sliced
  9. Get 1 cup chicken broth
  10. Prepare 2 tsp blackened, or cajun seasoning
  11. Prepare 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  13. Take 1 lb small cooked shrimp, no tails
