The ingredients needed to make My Aunt Edna's Shrimp Potato Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 pound gold potatoes peeled, diced, and boiled
  2. Prepare 1 cup popcorn shrimp cooked and peeled
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced green olives with pimentos
  4. Make ready 1 small onion diced finely
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  6. Prepare To taste salt
  7. Get 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  8. Get 2/3 cup mayonnaise more or less she did very wet
  9. Prepare 2 large boiled eggs chopped
Instructions to make My Aunt Edna's Shrimp Potato Salad:
  1. Cook the potatoes. Let them cool.
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients together. Salt the potatoes to taste then add to the shrimp mixture.
  3. Chill and serve. I hope you enjoy!!!
