The ingredients needed to make Tuna salad:
  1. Take 4 (5 oz) Tuna Cans
  2. Prepare 1/2 Cup Mayonnaise
  3. Prepare Pinch Black Pepper
  4. Take 1 Can Corn
  5. Take 1 Red Onion
  6. Take 2 Tomatoes
  7. Make ready 2 Green Peppers
  8. Take 1 Bag Tostadas

Instructions to make Tuna salad:
  2. Open & empty the tuna into a bowl
  3. Add pinch of black pepper seasoning & mix.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of mayonnaise & mix.
  5. Add one can of whole corn.
  6. Cut & dice red onion & add to bowl & mix.
  7. Cut & dice tomatoes then add to bowl.
  8. Cut & dice green Peppers and add to bowl.
  9. Mix all together & its ready to serve.
  10. Serve on Tostadas.
