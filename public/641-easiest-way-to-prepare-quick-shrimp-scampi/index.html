  2. Prepare 1/2 clove Fresh Pressed Garlic
  3. Make ready 1 stick Butter
  4. Get 1/4 cup Lemon Juice
  5. Get 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  6. Get 1 box Spaghetti Noodles
  7. Take 1 as needed Parmesan Cheese

  4. Bring noodles to a boil, and cook until desired tenderness.
