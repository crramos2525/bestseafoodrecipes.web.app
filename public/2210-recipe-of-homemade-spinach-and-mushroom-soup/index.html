The ingredients needed to make Spinach And Mushroom Soup:
  1. Prepare (VEGAN )
  2. Take 6 cup spinach
  3. Make ready 15 small king oyster
  4. Make ready 1 large thinly siced medaliion eryngii bunch mushroom
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup spring onion / scallion
  6. Take 2 liter vegetable stock or any stock
  7. Get WASABI SAUCE
  8. Get 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  9. Make ready 1 tsp wasabi paste ( optional add on )
  10. Prepare (NON VEGAN )MEAT OPTION ADDITIONAL ADD ON
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup thinly sliced cooked chinese bbq pork
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup sliced cooked chinese roasted pork
  13. Make ready NOODLE OPTION ADDITIONAL ADD ON
  14. Get 2 cup soy noodle
