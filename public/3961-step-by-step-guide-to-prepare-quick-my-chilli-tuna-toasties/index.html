The ingredients needed to make My Chilli Tuna Toasties 😍:
  1. Prepare 1 small Tin Cooked Tuna fish
  2. Make ready 1 tbls diced onion
  3. Get 4 slices Cucumber diced
  4. Prepare 2 small red + green chilli peppers chopped
  5. Get 1/2 Tsp Sriracha sauce
  6. Take 1 tbls Mayonaise or Sour cream
  7. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  8. Take 1 Good pinch of black pepper

Steps to make My Chilli Tuna Toasties 😍:
  1. Take out the tuna from the tin add to a bowl then mix the onion in with the tuna. Next dice the chilli peppers and mix in.
  2. Add the Sriracha and salt + pepper, mayonaise or sour cream. Then butter the toast its better if the toast is crisp. Then add the Tuna mix on the toast and serve.
