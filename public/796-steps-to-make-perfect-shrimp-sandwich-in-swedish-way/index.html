The ingredients needed to make Shrimp sandwich in Swedish way:
  1. Make ready Base :- Shrimps mayonnaise sauce
  2. Take 300 gm mayonnaise
  3. Get 150 gm handpeel shrimps
  4. Get 2 red apple (cut into dice)
  5. Prepare 3 dl lemon juice
  6. Make ready 100 gm dill fresh herb
  7. Get 1 tablespoon sugar
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  9. Prepare 4 fresh salad leaves
  10. Get Topping and decorations :-
  11. Take Fresh dill
  12. Take slices Fresh lemon
  13. Take Caviar
  14. Get 100 gm shrimps
  15. Make ready 4 pcs round bread
