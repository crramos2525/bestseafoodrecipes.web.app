Instructions to make pizza stuffed shells:
  1. prep shells according to box

  3. dice pepperoni into small squares
  4. stuff cooled shells with tbls or two of sauce add cheese and pepperoni
  5. pour remaining sauce over top and cheese
  6. bake until cheese is melted and browned on top
