The ingredients needed to make Mixed Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready 1 cup - Basmati or Boiled Rice
  2. Prepare 1 inch - Cinnamon stick
  3. Take 3 - Green Cardamom
  4. Make ready 3-4 - Cloves
  5. Make ready 1 cup - Sliced Carrots
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup - Chopped Beans
  7. Prepare 1 cup - Sliced Capsicum
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup - chopped spring onions
  9. Take 2 tbsp - Ghee
  10. Get 1 cup - Boiled Soya chunks
  11. Take 10-12 numbers of - prawns medium size
  12. Prepare 2 - Eggs
  13. Get 1 handful - Green peas
  14. Take 2 tsp - Soya sauce
  15. Get to taste Salt
  16. Take 1/2 tsp - Garam Masala powder
  17. Take 1/2 tsp - Black pepper
  18. Get 1/4 tsp - Turmeric Powder
  19. Make ready 1/4 tsp - Garlic Powder
  20. Take 1 tbsp - Vegetable oil

Steps to make Mixed Fried Rice:
  1. First wash the rice and then soak them into the water for 15 minutes.Now Drain the excess water from the rice.Heat a saucepan full of water add soaked rice & cinnamon stick, cardamom,cloves, little salt into it.Boil the rice until 90 % tender.pour the boiled rice in a strainer, rinse it with running cold tap water and keep it aside for drain off excess water.
  2. Boil the soya chunks in another pot till soft.Drain the water from the soya chunks & keep them aside.. In the mean time cut the vegetables length wise or Chopped them & keep them aside.
  3. Clean & Wash prawns properly. Take a bowl add prawns, Garlic powder, salt and turmeric powder mix well.Heat little oil in the fry pan add prawns and fry them until turns into golden brown in color.Keep them aside.
  4. Now in the same pan add oil if needed beat the eggs with salt and fry them as scrambled egg.keep them aside.
  5. Now heat the ghee in the fry pan add carrots, capsicums, beans in the pan and fry them in medium heat for 4-5 minutes or till soften.Now add soya chunks, salt,spring onions, black peppers mix well and saut√© them for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add boiled rice, soya sauce, more spring onions and mix well. Then add fried prawns, scrambled eggs, peas one by one.Add salt to taste mix them properly.Stir fry for 3-4 minutes on low heat.
  7. At last add little ghee, sprinkle little Garam masala powder over the fried rice mix well stir fry for another 2-3 minutes and turn off the heat.
  8. Now enjoy Mixed Fried Rice hot with salads.
