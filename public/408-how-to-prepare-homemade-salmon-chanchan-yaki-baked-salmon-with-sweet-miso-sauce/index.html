The ingredients needed to make Salmon Chanchan-yaki (Baked Salmon with Sweet Miso Sauce):
  1. Take Raw salmon fillets
  2. Get leaves Cabbage
  3. Get Carrot
  4. Make ready Onion
  5. Make ready Green pepper
  6. Prepare packet Enoki mushrooms
  7. Take A. Miso
  8. Prepare A. Sugar
  9. Prepare A. Sake
  10. Prepare A. Mirin
  11. Make ready A. Mayonnaise
  12. Take A. Dashi stock
Steps to make Salmon Chanchan-yaki (Baked Salmon with Sweet Miso Sauce):
  1. Cut the cabbage into bite-sized pieces. Cut the enoki mushrooms in half and shred apart. Julienne the other vegetables. Preheat the oven to 360°F/180°C.
  2. Mix the A. ingredients together very well to make the miso sauce.
  3. Heat up a pan and brown the salmon on both sides.
  4. Put a sheet of kitchen parchment paper on a baking sheet, and put on the salmon. Cover with the cut vegetables and miso sauce.
  5. Wrap the parchment paper around and close it to make a pouch. Bake in a 360°F/180°C oven for 20-25 minutes and it's done.
