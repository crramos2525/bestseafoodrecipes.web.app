Steps to make Mike's Drunken Shrimp Cocktails:
  1. Mince all of your vegetables, fruits and herbs. Add them to a large, nonreactive bowl. Add your seasonings as well. Mix well.
  2. Add your frozen presteamed shrimp to bowl.
  3. Add enough Clamato and V8 to cover the top of your cocktail mixture. Add your tequila as well. Mix together well.
  4. They only brand I use.
  5. Leave on counter for one hour. Don't worry. All vegetables and juices are chilled and your shrimp are fully frozen. You're slightly dethawing your shrimp at this point.
  6. Following that, seal tightly and refrigerate for a few hours, if not longer. Stir occasionally. If serving the following day, place in the fridge straight away.
  7. Serve with the sides and garnishments listed above in chilled cocktail glasses. Or, jazz her up as you'd like! Enjoy!
