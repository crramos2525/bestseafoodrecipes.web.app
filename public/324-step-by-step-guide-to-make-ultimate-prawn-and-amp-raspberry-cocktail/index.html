The ingredients needed to make Prawn & Raspberry Cocktail:
  1. Take aprox 10 king prawns
  2. Prepare 1 little gem lettuce shredded
  3. Get 1 avocado (sml)
  4. Get 1 punnet raspberry
  5. Take 1 tsp horseradish sauce
  6. Take optional (1tbsp mayo)
  7. Take 1/2 lime
  8. Prepare 1 garlic clove
  9. Make ready fresh or dried crushed chilli
  10. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  11. Prepare 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  12. Take paprika - sprinkle
  13. Take salt and pepper
  14. Get tiny bit of mint or coriander or both.. optional
