The ingredients needed to make Tuna with noodles:
  1. Prepare 2 cans tuna drained, 3 if you really want to taste the tuna
  2. Prepare 1 package noodles of your choice, i used gluten free elbow
  3. Take 2 cups frozen peas
  4. Get 1 medium carrot shredded
  5. Get 1/2 cup diced onion, i used sweet
  6. Make ready 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise, you can use less or more
  8. Get 1 tsp white pepper
  9. Take 1 tsp celery seed
  10. Take 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  11. Get 1/2 tsp paprika
  12. Get 1 tbsp lime juice (or lemon)
  13. Prepare Approximately 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
  14. Take 1 cup shredded cheese, i used colby jack
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp yellow mustard
