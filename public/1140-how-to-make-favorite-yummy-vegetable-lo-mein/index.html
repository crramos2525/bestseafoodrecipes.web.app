The ingredients needed to make Yummy Vegetable lo mein:
  1. Take #Sauce#
  2. Take 2 cups chicken flavor soup base mix or chicken broth
  3. Make ready 1/6 cup light soy sauce
  4. Take 1/4 cup mushroom flavored dark soy sauce
  5. Get 1 table spoon Hoisin sauce
  6. Prepare 1 table spoon oyster flavored sauce
  7. Get 2 table spoon sesame oil
  8. Prepare Dash white peppers
  9. Get 1 table spoon brown sugar
  10. Get 1 table spoon or as your taste Sriracha sauce
  11. Take #Vegetable of your choice#
  12. Prepare 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  14. Take 1 pound spaghetti or egg lo mein noodle
  15. Take Sesame seeds (optional)
  16. Take green onion
  17. Get Matchsticks carrot
  18. Prepare Shiitake mushrooms
  19. Prepare Bamboo shoots
  20. Get Beans sprouts
  21. Get Napa cabbage
  22. Take Spinach or chives (Nira)

  3. Add rest of your favorite vegetables except beans sprouts (I like mine crunchy) and cook for 3 min then add cooked noodle and beans sprouts.
