The ingredients needed to make Guacamole shrimp tacos:
  1. Prepare About a 1/3 of a pound of large shrimp
  2. Take whole hass avocado
  3. Get Minced red onion
  4. Make ready lime
  5. Take Salt
  6. Take Pepper
  7. Make ready Taco seasoning for the shrimp
  8. Get olive oil
  9. Prepare canola oil
  10. Make ready white vinegar
  11. Get Corn tortillas
  12. Make ready Lime wedges

Steps to make Guacamole shrimp tacos:
  1. First you season the shrimp with the seasoning. Then you add the vinegar and canola oil,put to the side.
  2. Then you start making the guacamole. Cut the avocado then cut up your onions. Mash the avocado then add onions, olive oil, salt, pepper and some lime juice.
  3. Cook the shrimp in a pan for about 2-3 minutes on each side until opaque. Put to the side.
  4. Now heat up the tortillas then add the guacamole and the shrimp. Then your done enjoy !
