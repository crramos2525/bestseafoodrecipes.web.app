The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Spicy Seafood Pasta:
  1. Make ready 450 g cooked Spaghetti
  2. Make ready 1 cup Shrimp, deveined
  3. Prepare 1 cup Squid, cleaned and cut into rings
  4. Make ready 1 cup Mussels, precooked and removed from shell
  5. Get 1 c water (half from mussel & halfpasta water)
  6. Prepare 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  7. Prepare 1 pc Onion, diced
  8. Make ready 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  9. Make ready 1/2 c Tomato/Spahetti Sauce
  10. Prepare 1/4 c Oyster Sauce
  11. Get 3 Tbsp Sugar
  12. Prepare 3 Tbsp Sriracha, adjust according to preference
  13. Take Pepper
  14. Prepare Spring onion for garnish
  15. Make ready Note 1: precook mussel with 2 Tbsp grated ginger
  16. Get Note 2: I use Filipino style spaghetti sauce
Instructions to make Sweet & Spicy Seafood Pasta:
  1. In a bowl mix together tomato sauce, oyster sauce, sugar and sriracha. Mix until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Heat oil, saute onion & garlic
  3. Add shrimp
  4. Add sauce mixture, let it simmer
  5. Add squid, after 1 minute add mussel. Mix and simmer
  6. Put spaghetti noodles, season with pepper. Mix thoroughly.
