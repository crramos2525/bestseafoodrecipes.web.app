The ingredients needed to make Open faced tuna melt:
  1. Get 2 can (5 oz) chicken of the sea tuna
  2. Prepare 1 medium shallot,minced(approx.2 tbsp)
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp plain non-fat greek yogurt
  4. Take 1 tbsp lemon juice
  5. Take 1 tbsp minced parsley
  6. Get 1/8 tsp sea salt
  7. Make ready 4 slice 100% whole-wheat bread, toasted
  8. Take 2 fresh tomatoes, sliced
  9. Get 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions to make Open faced tuna melt:
  1. Preheat broiler
  2. Combine tuna,shallot,Greek yogurt,lemon juice,parsley, and salt in a medium bowl. If desired you can add some black pepper or hot sauce for additional flavor.
  3. Spread 1/4 cup of the tuna mixture on each slice of toast; top with cheese and tomato.
  4. Place sandwiches on a baking sheet and broil until the cheese is bubbling and golden brown, 3-5 min
