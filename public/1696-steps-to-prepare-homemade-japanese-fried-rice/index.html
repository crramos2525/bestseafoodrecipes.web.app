The ingredients needed to make Japanese Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 1/2 spring onion
  2. Prepare 3 dried Shiitake mushroom
  3. Get 1 pack Kanikamaboko (crab sticks)
  4. Get 3 eggs
  5. Get 2 bowls cooked rice
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Dashi powder (Japanese fish bouillon)
  7. Get 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  8. Get Black pepper
