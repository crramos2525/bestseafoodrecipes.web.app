Instructions to make Mie Ayam Jamur (Chicken Mushroom Noodles):
  1. Cut baby bok choy. Put them into boiling water for 1 minute. Remove from the heat. Set aside.
  2. Stir fry chicken mushroom: - Heat the vegetable oil. SautÃ© the garlic until fragrant.

  4. Add straw mushrooms, oyster sauce, sweet soy sauce, fish sauce, salt, pepper, and chicken stock. - Bring it to boil.

  6. How to serve: - In a small bowl, add 1/2 tsp sesame oil, 1/2 fish sauce, and a dash of white pepper powder.
