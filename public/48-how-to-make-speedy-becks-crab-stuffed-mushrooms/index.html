The ingredients needed to make Becks Crab Stuffed Mushrooms:
  1. Get 12 good sized mushrooms of your choice
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped and sauted prior
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup red peppers, finely chopped and sauted prior
  4. Make ready 1/2 lb (8 ounces) crab meat 'not the imitation kind'
  5. Prepare 2 cup oyster crackers, crushed
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp salt and pepper
  9. Take 1 egg
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  11. Prepare 4 tbsp butter
  12. Take 1/2 cup shredded mozz cheese for topping, or you can get sliced mozz.
