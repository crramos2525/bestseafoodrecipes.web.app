The ingredients needed to make Stuffed peppers with tuna salad:
  1. Prepare 4 round peppers, different colours
  2. Prepare 160 grams tuna can
  3. Prepare 2 boiled potatoes, in cubes
  4. Get 8 black olives, chopped
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp capers
  7. Take 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  8. Prepare 6 tsp olive oil, extra virgin
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
  10. Prepare 1 salt
  11. Make ready 1 ground black pepper
