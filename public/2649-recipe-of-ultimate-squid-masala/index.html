The ingredients needed to make Squid masala:
  1. Prepare 300 gms Squid
  2. Get 1/2 cup Onion paste
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  4. Get 1 dry red chilli
  5. Get 3 tbsp oil
  6. Make ready 1 tsp ginger paste
  7. Take 2 tsp garlic paste
  8. Prepare 2 tsp green chilli paste
  9. Prepare To taste salt
  10. Get 1 tsp turmeric
  11. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  12. Make ready 2 tsp Roasted cumin powder
  13. Prepare 1/3 tsp garam masala powder
