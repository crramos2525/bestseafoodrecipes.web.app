The ingredients needed to make Prawn Tempura:
  1. Prepare 6-7 Pieces Jumbo prawns cleaned and devein
  2. Take 1 pkt Boiled and fried noodles for plating
  3. Prepare For tempura batter:
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp Cornflour
