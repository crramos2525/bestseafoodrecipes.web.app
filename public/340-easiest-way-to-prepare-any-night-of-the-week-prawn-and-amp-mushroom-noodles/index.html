  1. Prepare 200 g ready to eat cooked king prawns
  2. Prepare 100 g button mushrooms

  5. Prepare splash sweet chilli sauce

  7. Get sprinkle of chopped coriander
  8. Take 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  9. Get 1 red chilli
