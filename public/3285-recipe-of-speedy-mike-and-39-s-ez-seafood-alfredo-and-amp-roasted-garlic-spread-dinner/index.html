Instructions to make Mike's EZ Seafood Alfredo & Roasted Garlic Spread Dinner:
  1. ● For The Seafood De-thaw shrimp in sink. Leave clams in freezer until ready to serve. Private Reserves or out a great steamed clam. Available at Costco and Sams Club.
  2. Start by making your roasted garlic by mixing everything together in double lined tinfoil. Mix up well.
  3. Seal garlic up tightly. In a preheated 400° oven - bake for 30 minutes.
  4. At the same time, bake your bread as per manufactures directions [usually 7 to 10 minutes at 400°] then cover bread with a towel until you're ready to slice and serve.
  5. Once roasted - using forks or a potato masher - mash garlic mixture well. Taste test to see if you'd like to add additional herbs or salt. If you feel it's too salty - no worries. Just add additional sun dried tomatoes.
  6. Serve garlic spread warm or room temp in small individual Ramikins for your guests.
  7. Roasted garlic and baguettes presented by one of my 7 year old students.
  8. Alfredo Sauce ingredients. Mushrooms not pictured.
  9. Add 2 tbsp of your roasted garlic mixture and bring all sauce ingredients to a simmer. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  10. At the same time - begin your noodle water boil with salt and a dash of olive oil. Boil as per manufactures directions. Usually 12 minutes.
  11. In the last 3 minutes of your 10 minute Alfredo simmer - put in your shrimp. You're only wanting to warm them since they're already pre -steamed.
  12. Drain pasta and lightly coat noodles with olive oil.
  13. Heat clams in microwave as per manufactures directions. Usually 3 to 5 minutes. Place any residual juices from that steam in your pasta and mix.
