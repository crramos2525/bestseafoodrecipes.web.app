The ingredients needed to make Salmon and zucchini braised in oyster sauce:
  1. Get 2 centre-cut salmon fillets, deboned and skin-on
  2. Take 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  3. Make ready 1 thumb-sized nub ginger, sliced into thin strips
  4. Prepare 2 medium zucchini, chopped into 1 cm cubes
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  7. Get 1 tbsp honey

Instructions to make Salmon and zucchini braised in oyster sauce:
  1. Add a splash of veg oil to a large pan on medium-high heat. Lightly season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper and put them in the pan, skin-side down. Let fry for 3 minutes.
  2. Carefully flip the fillets over and add the garlic and ginger to the pan. Let fry 2 minutes.
  3. Add the zucchini, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, and 1/2 cup water to the pan. Turn the heat down to medium and let simmer for 5 minutes.
