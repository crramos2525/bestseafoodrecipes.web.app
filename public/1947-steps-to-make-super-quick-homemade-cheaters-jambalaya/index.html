  2. Make ready 1 Link smoke sausage (see picture)
  3. Make ready 1/2 Onion sliced
  4. Take 2 Bell peppers, chopped
  5. Make ready 1 packages Imitation crab meat
  6. Prepare 1 Cajun seasoning, to taste
