The ingredients needed to make Easy & Delicious 5 Step Tuna Salad:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Mayonnaise (can use light but not as tasty in my opinion)
  2. Get 5 can Chuck Light Tuna
  3. Make ready 1/2 each White Onion (chopped)
  4. Take 2 each Dill Pickles (cut in 4 lengthwise then chopped into tiny triangles)
  5. Get 1/4 tsp Freash Ground Sea Salt
  6. Make ready 1/4 tbsp Fresh Ground Pepper
  7. Take 2 each Tomatoe (sliced)
  8. Get 3 each Whole Wheat Pita Pockets
Steps to make Easy & Delicious 5 Step Tuna Salad:
  1. Open tuna cans, drain and collect inside tuna fish from all 5 cans in a bowl.
  2. Add mayonnaise, onion, pickles, salt and pepper then mix well with a fork until creamy.
  3. Cut your pitas in half.
  4. Open pitas and line one side with tomatoe and fill with tuna mixture.
  5. OPTIONAl: You can also add in your favorite cheese and some red onions and bake at 350 for 7 min to make delicious tuna melts too.
