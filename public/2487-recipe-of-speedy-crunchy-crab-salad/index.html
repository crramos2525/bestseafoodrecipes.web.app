Instructions to make Crunchy crab salad:
  1. Cut cabbage in small strips.
  2. Cut crab meat into small cubes and add to cabbage.
  3. Add corn, mayo, salt/pepper to taste and mix.
  4. Cover and let chill for about an hour before serving.
