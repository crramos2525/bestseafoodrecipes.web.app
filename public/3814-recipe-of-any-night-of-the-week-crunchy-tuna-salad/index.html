The ingredients needed to make Crunchy Tuna Salad:
  1. Take 1 can tuna fish
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup miracle whip
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon pickle relish
  4. Get 1 teaspoon Mustard
  5. Get 1 small green onion
  6. Prepare 1 small yellow onion
  7. Prepare 6-8 green olives
  8. Get 1 small celery rib
  9. Get 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon celery seed
  11. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  12. Take 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  13. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon pepper
