The ingredients needed to make Oyster Stew:
  1. Make ready 4 Tbs Butter
  2. Prepare 1 medium Celery Stalk, finely diced
  3. Get 1 medium Garlic Clove, minced
  4. Make ready 2 Tbs White Flour
  5. Prepare 2 cups Milk
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp minced Fresh or Freeze Dried Parsley Flakes, plus some for dusting
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  10. Take 2 cup fresh Oysters packed in Oyster liquor
  11. Make ready 1 grind or two of Ground Black Pepper
  12. Make ready Cook
  13. Make ready About an hour before cooking, measure out the milk, cream, and oysters to get to room temperature. Prepare the rest of the ingredients
  14. Get Melt the butter in a soup pot over medium heat. Stir in the celery and garlic and cook until soft
  15. Make ready Add the flour and stir until the roux gets a light tan in color
  16. Take Slowly add the milk and cream to the roux, stirring constantly
  17. Make ready Stir the Onion Powder, Parsley, and Salt into the stew. Reduce heat to medium-low; continue cooking and stirring until the mixture bubbles and thickens, 5 to 7 minutes. Reduce heat to barely simmer
  18. Make ready Add the oysters with the liquid from the container to the mixture; cook until the edges of the oysters curl, and slightly thickened, about 5 minutes more. Season with pepper and serve immediately
  19. Take Ladle into bowls, dust with parsley, and serve along with crusty bread
