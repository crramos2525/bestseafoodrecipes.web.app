The ingredients needed to make octopus spaghetti:
  1. Prepare octopus spaghetti
  2. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  3. Get 1/4 can spaghetti sauce
  4. Make ready 1 packages spaghetti noddle
  5. Get 1 medium sausage
  6. Get 1/2 cup milk
  7. Get 1/2 green pepper
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Get 1 tsp olive oil
