The ingredients needed to make Raw Oysters With Ponzu Sauce:
  1. Get 1/2 lb Oysters in shell on ice
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup Tamari
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Lemon juice
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Rice vinegar
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Fresh grated ginger
  6. Get 1 pinch Thin slices green onion
