The ingredients needed to make King Prawn and Chicken Satay Stir Fry:
  1. Prepare 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  2. Take 1 large onion finely chopped
  3. Get 4 clove garlic finely chopped
  4. Take 2 chilli peppers de-seeded and finely chopped
  5. Take 1 green pepper diced
  6. Take 1 red pepper diced
  7. Prepare 4 carrots peeled and cut into thin strips
  8. Get 240 grams peanut satay sauce
  9. Take 4 cup medium egg noodles
  10. Prepare 2 chicken breasts de-skinned and boned cut into bite size strips
  11. Make ready 10 king prawns de-shelled
Steps to make King Prawn and Chicken Satay Stir Fry:
  1. Heat oil in wok/large pan over a moderate heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic. Cook until soft.
  3. When soft add peppers, carrots, chilies and cook for about 5 mins.
  4. Add chicken and stir in.
  5. Add Satay sauce and cook for about 2 mins.
  6. Add king prawns.
  7. Add noodles and cook for about 2 mins.
  8. Season to taste and enjoy.
