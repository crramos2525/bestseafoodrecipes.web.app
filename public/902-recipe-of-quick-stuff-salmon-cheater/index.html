The ingredients needed to make Stuff Salmon Cheater:
  1. Take 1 box Crab cake
  2. Prepare 1 Pink Salmon
  3. Take 1 box Couscous
  4. Make ready 1 All purpose seasoning / seafood seasoning of your choice
  5. Make ready 1 Optional sliced lemon
  6. Take 4 1/4 stick of butter
Steps to make Stuff Salmon Cheater:
  1. Base pan with butter, minced garlic, lemon, and seasonings ( you will drizzle this over your couscous and fish once you finish)
  2. Put fish and crab cake in the pan season with seafood seasoning and put 2 to 3 sliced lemons on/ in between the fish.
  3. you can split salmon and stuff after cooking or before cooking during prep.
  4. I cooked mine for about 35 minuets until my fish was all the way cooked and the inside of crab cake was done about the same time
  5. Follow the instructions for couscous from the box, you want to cook it like you would rice.
  6. Once finished drizzle butter seasoning over couscous and you can split salmon and stuff after cooking or before cooking during prep.
