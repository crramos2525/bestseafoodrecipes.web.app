The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Breakfast Tacos:
  1. Take 2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  2. Get 1 small onion, chopped
  3. Make ready 2 bell peppers, chopped
  4. Get 8 oz mushrooms, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 lb shrimp meat
  6. Make ready salt, pepper, & garlic
  7. Prepare 4 eggs
  8. Take 1/4 cup dairy
  9. Make ready cilantro or parsley
  10. Make ready 1 packages tortillas

Steps to make Shrimp Breakfast Tacos:
  1. Heat oil to medium high heat.
  2. Add onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Add peppers and mushrooms. Cook half way.
  4. Add shrimp and seasoning. Cook until heated.
  5. Mix eggs and daily. Pour over the shrimp and vegetables.
  6. Optional - add cilantro/parsley.
  7. When eggs are done, place in a tortilla and enjoy. Also, you may want to top with cheese.
