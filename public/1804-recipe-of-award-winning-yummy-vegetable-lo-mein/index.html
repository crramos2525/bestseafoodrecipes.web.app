The ingredients needed to make Yummy Vegetable lo mein:
  1. Make ready #Sauce#
  2. Take 2 cups chicken flavor soup base mix or chicken broth
  3. Prepare 1/6 cup light soy sauce
  4. Take 1/4 cup mushroom flavored dark soy sauce
  5. Prepare 1 table spoon Hoisin sauce
  6. Take 1 table spoon oyster flavored sauce
  7. Prepare 2 table spoon sesame oil
  8. Get Dash white peppers
  9. Take 1 table spoon brown sugar
  10. Make ready 1 table spoon or as your taste Sriracha sauce
  11. Get #Vegetable of your choice#
  12. Get 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  14. Make ready 1 pound spaghetti or egg lo mein noodle
  15. Prepare Sesame seeds (optional)
  16. Take green onion
  17. Take Matchsticks carrot
  18. Get Shiitake mushrooms
  19. Prepare Bamboo shoots
  20. Get Beans sprouts
  21. Get Napa cabbage
  22. Take Spinach or chives (Nira)
