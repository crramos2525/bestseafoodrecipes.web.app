The ingredients needed to make KaShawna's Crab Cakes With Shrimp:
  1. Get 1 lb Lump Crab meat
  2. Get 1/3 cup Crushed Cracker (I use Ritz)
  3. Take 3 Green Onions Cut (White part too)
  4. Take 1 Egg
  5. Prepare 1/2 Shrimp Cut into small pieces

  12. Take 1 tsp Salt
  13. Get 1 tsp Pepper

Instructions to make KaShawna's Crab Cakes With Shrimp:

  2. Shape into patties and dust with flour.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
