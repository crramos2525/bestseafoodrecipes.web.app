The ingredients needed to make Moms Ceviche:
  1. Make ready 2 lbs crab, shredded
  2. Get 2.5 lbs salad shrimp
  3. Make ready 4 large tomatoes, diced
  4. Make ready lime juice (about 10)
  5. Take 1 extra large sweet onion
  6. Make ready 2 large bunches of cilantro
  7. Prepare 1/2 can orange soda
  8. Make ready to taste tapatio
  9. Take 1/2 can beer
  10. Prepare to taste ketchup
  11. Get salt
  12. Prepare granulated garlic (powder)
  13. Make ready white wine vinegar, ~3tbsp
  14. Make ready to taste oregano
  15. Take 4 cucumbers diced
Steps to make Moms Ceviche:
  1. Toss vegetables, shrimp, spices together, add ketchup, beer, orange soda, lime juice, white wine vinegar and tapatio
