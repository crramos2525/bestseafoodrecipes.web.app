The ingredients needed to make Sig's Leek and Crab (or Croutons) Soup:
  1. Take 250 to 300 grams fresh leeks
  2. Take 2 to 3 brown onions
  3. Get 4 clove smoked garlic
  4. Take 50 grams unsalted butter
  5. Prepare 40 grams plain flour
  6. Take 3/4 liter vegetable stock (use more if the leeks were large and the soup is to thick)
  7. Make ready 150 ml heavy cream ( double cream)
  8. Get 1 pinch each salt or salt substitute, dried coriander or flat parsley leaf and fresh ground black pepper
  9. Make ready 2 to 3 drops or to taste, worcestershire sauce (optional, but recommended )
  10. Make ready 1 tsp or to taste sherry or brandy (optional, but recommended )
  11. Prepare 150 grams precooked crab ( or use croutons of your choice instead)

Steps to make Sig's Leek and Crab (or Croutons) Soup:
  1. Wash the leeks well and chop into small rings , reserve some of the rings
  2. Peel the onions and the garlic and chop roughly
  3. Add the leeks,onions, garlic, butter and saute, do not fry or brown them , dust with the flour until all is used and has slightly thickened the mixture .Add the vegetable stock slowly stirring all the time . Gently cook for about 15 minutes
  4. Cool slightly .Puree all with a blender or food processor . Add half the cream , all brandy or sherry if using and season with pepper , salt parsley or coriander and Worcestershire sauce
  5. Add the precooked crab meat just before serving with the rest of the cream and Leek cutting
