The ingredients needed to make Roasted Salmon with Dill & Maple Syrup:
  1. Prepare 1.5 lbs salmon fillet, skin on
  2. Take 1.25 tsp salt
  3. Get 1/2 tsp pepper
  4. Prepare 6 limes, the juice of (plus more for serving)
  5. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp maple syrup
  8. Make ready 3-4 tbsp chopped fresh dill (or 1 tbsp dried dill, but I really recommend fresh)
  9. Get 1 garlic clove, minced
  10. Take 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
Instructions to make Roasted Salmon with Dill & Maple Syrup:
  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F. Line a baking dish with wax paper and place the salmon, skin side down, in it. Be sure to use a baking dish where the salmon fits snugly, otherwise the sauce will get lost in the corners, and you definitely don't want that!
  2. Whisk all the other ingredients in a bowl, bathe the salmon with the resulting sauce.
  3. Place in the oven and roast for 12 minutes. Serve with your favorite starch side (we used a simple white rice with veggies). Garnish with lime wedges. Enjoy!
