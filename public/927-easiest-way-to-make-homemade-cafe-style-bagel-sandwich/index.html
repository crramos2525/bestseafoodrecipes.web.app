The ingredients needed to make Cafe-style Bagel Sandwich:
  1. Take 1 any kind Bagel
  2. Get 1 tbsp ●Salmon flakes (bottled)
  3. Prepare 1 piece ●Smoked cheese
  4. Prepare 1 small ●Pickled cucumber
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ●Mayonnaise
  6. Take 1 Grainy mustard
  7. Take 1 ・Black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 ・Basil
Instructions to make Cafe-style Bagel Sandwich:
  1. Cut the bagel in half and evenly spread the mustard on the surface.
  2. Cut the smoked cheese into small chunks, then mix together with the ● ingredients. Spread onto one slice of the bagel and then toast both slices. Sprinkle to taste.
