  2. Make ready 1 12ounce beer of your choice
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup ground cayenne pepper
  4. Get 4 garlic cloves peeled
  5. Get 3 lemons halved
  6. Get 1 large onion peeled and quartered
  7. Make ready 5 potatoes cleaned and halved
  8. Make ready 5 ears corn on Cobb cleaned and halved
  9. Get 5 Louisiana hot links halved (or sausage of your choice)
  10. Make ready 8 eggs
  11. Take 3 1/2 lb king crab legs
  12. Make ready 2 lb large deveined shrimp with shell on
  13. Take 1 lb mussel meat
