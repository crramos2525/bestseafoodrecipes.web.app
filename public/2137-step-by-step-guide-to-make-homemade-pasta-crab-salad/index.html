The ingredients needed to make Pasta Crab Salad:
  1. Make ready 3 celery stalks, chopped
  2. Prepare 1/2 green bell pepper, diced
  3. Make ready 6 baby dill pickles, chopped
  4. Take 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  6. Make ready 1 cup sour cream
  7. Get 1 tbs. pickle juice
  8. Get 2 tsp. lemon juice
  9. Get 1/4 tsp. salt
  10. Get dash black pepper
  11. Prepare 1 tsp. Nature's Seasons seasoning blend
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp. dill weed
  13. Take 1/4 tsp. onion powder
  14. Prepare 1/8 tsp. garlic powder
  15. Prepare 1 package imitation crab meat shredded
  16. Make ready 10 oz. rotini pasta, cooked according to package directions

Instructions to make Pasta Crab Salad:
  1. Pasta, cook according to package directions, and let it cool.
  2. Meanwhile prepare vegetables, and crab meat place in a large bowl
  3. For sauce, in small bowl combine mayonnaise, sour cream, pickle juice, lemon juice, and seasonings
  4. Place pasta with vegetables, and crab meat add sauce mixture stir all together
  5. Chill for a few hours or over night. Serve and enjoy!
