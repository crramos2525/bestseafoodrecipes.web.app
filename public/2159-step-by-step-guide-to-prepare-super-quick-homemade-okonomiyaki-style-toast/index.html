The ingredients needed to make Okonomiyaki-style Toast:
  1. Get 1 slice Sliced bread (thick)
  2. Make ready 1 leaf Cabbage
  3. Take 1 piece Bacon
  4. Take 1 Egg
  5. Take 1 to tatse Salt and pepper
  6. Take 1 tsp Tempura crumbs (optional)
  7. Take 1 as required Vegetable oil
  8. Make ready 1 as required ☆ Okonomiyaki sauce
  9. Make ready 1 as required ☆ Mayonnaise
  10. Make ready 1 as required ☆ Dried Bonito flakes
  11. Prepare 1 as required ☆ Dried green nori flakes
Steps to make Okonomiyaki-style Toast:
  1. Cut out the white part of the bread (with your knife cut along the crust).
  2. Slice the bacon and cabbage.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and lay the crust in the pan. Fill the cavity with cabbage and bacon. (Fry over a medium to low heat.)
  4. Salt and pepper. Stir in the whisked egg and tempura crumbs.
  5. Place the cut-out white part of bread over the ingredients inside the crust. Press gently.
  6. Flip and fry further.
  7. Put the ☆ toppings on the toast and serve.
