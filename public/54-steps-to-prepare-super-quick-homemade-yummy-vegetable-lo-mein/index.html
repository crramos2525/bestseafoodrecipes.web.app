The ingredients needed to make Yummy Vegetable lo mein:
  1. Prepare #Sauce#
  2. Take 2 cups chicken flavor soup base mix or chicken broth
  3. Get 1/6 cup light soy sauce
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup mushroom flavored dark soy sauce
  5. Take 1 table spoon Hoisin sauce
  6. Make ready 1 table spoon oyster flavored sauce
  7. Take 2 table spoon sesame oil
  8. Prepare Dash white peppers
  9. Prepare 1 table spoon brown sugar
  10. Prepare 1 table spoon or as your taste Sriracha sauce
  11. Take #Vegetable of your choice#
  12. Prepare 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  14. Take 1 pound spaghetti or egg lo mein noodle
  15. Get Sesame seeds (optional)
  16. Get green onion
  17. Take Matchsticks carrot
  18. Prepare Shiitake mushrooms
  19. Take Bamboo shoots
  20. Make ready Beans sprouts
  21. Get Napa cabbage
  22. Get Spinach or chives (Nira)
