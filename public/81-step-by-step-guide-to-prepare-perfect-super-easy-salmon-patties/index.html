The ingredients needed to make Super Easy Salmon Patties:
  1. Prepare 3 packages fresh salmon packs
  2. Make ready 2 each eggs
  3. Make ready 1 packages glutino crackers (one sleeve) or 3/4 sleeve Ritz
  4. Take 3/4 cup sliced green onion
  5. Get 1 tbsp minced fresh dill
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp shredded parmesan
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp minced italian parsely
  8. Take 1/2 tsp tumeric
  9. Make ready to taste Kosher salt black pepper
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup plain Panko bread crumbs for coating
  11. Get 2 tbsp butter 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions to make Super Easy Salmon Patties:
  1. Crush crackers finely
  2. Mix all ingredients well, form into patties
  3. Refrigerate one hour
  4. Melt some butter in non stick pan and heat with oil to med high
  5. Coat chilled patties in Panko crumbs
  6. Cook patties about 3 minutes each side until browned flip tenderly
