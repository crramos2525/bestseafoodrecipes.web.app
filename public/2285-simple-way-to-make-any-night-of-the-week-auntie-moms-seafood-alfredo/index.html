The ingredients needed to make Auntie moms seafood alfredo:
  1. Take 1 packages noodles (I use penne)
  2. Make ready 1 packages mushrooms
  3. Get 1/2 onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 1/2 lb shrimp
  5. Take 1 can clams
  6. Take 1 can bay scallops or white crab meat
  7. Make ready 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  8. Make ready 1 pints heavy cream
  9. Take 1 packages shredded parmesan cheese
  10. Take 1 tbsp butter
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp garlic
  12. Make ready 4 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions to make Auntie moms seafood alfredo:
  1. In oil saute mushrooms, onions, and garlic until tender.
  2. Drain cans of seafood. Add all the seafood and cook until shrimp is pink.
  3. Stir in heavy cream, cream of mushroom soup, 1 TBS butter, and parmesan cheese. Cook down and let simmer.
  4. Cook and drain noodles. Mix together or pour sauce over noodles as desired.
