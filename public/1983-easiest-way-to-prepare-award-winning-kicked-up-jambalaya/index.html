The ingredients needed to make Kicked up Jambalaya.:
  1. Take 1 box of Zantran brand jambalaya rice mix
  2. Get 1 14 ounce can of fire roasted tomatoes
  3. Get 1 Three andouille sausages cut into chunks
  4. Take 10 to 15 peeled deveined raw shrimp
  5. Prepare 4/5 sprigs of fresh flat-leaf parsley
  6. Get 1 Half of Cup chopped white onion
  7. Make ready 1 cup of chopped bell pepper any color
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil
  9. Prepare 1 One and one half cups of water

Steps to make Kicked up Jambalaya.:
  1. In a large saucepan combine olive oil onions and bell pepper saute for 3 to 5 minutes until vegetables are soft.
  2. Add the box of jambalaya rice mix the canned tomatoes and the water to the vegetables mix well bring to a boil reduce heat to medium low simmer with lid on for 15 minutes
  3. Next you will add the raw shrimp and the sausage for an additional 10 minutes or until shrimp starts to turn pink. Remove from heat let sit for 5 minutes and garnish with parsley.
