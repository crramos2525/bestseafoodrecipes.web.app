The ingredients needed to make quick and easy pasta salad:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp oregano
  2. Prepare 3 cup pasta
  3. Get 2/3 cup mayonaise
  4. Make ready 1 small tomato diced (optional)
  5. Take 1 small chicken breast or can of tuna chopped
  6. Prepare 2 cup italian dressing or to taste

Instructions to make quick and easy pasta salad:
  1. Boil chicken until done. In a separatre pot boil pasta until done.
  2. Mix italian dressing, mayo, and oregano in a medium sized bowl.
  3. Mix everything together in the bowl with the sauce.
  4. Refrigerate 30 mins (optional). Or serve as is and enjoy.
