The ingredients needed to make Curry Udon! Japanese style leftover curry recipe!:
  1. Take 1 leftover curry
  2. Prepare 200 hot watter
  3. Make ready 1 udon
  4. Take 2 tbs watery potato starch
  5. Prepare If you have "bonito fish soup stock"(Hondashi)
  6. Take 1 tsp bonito fish soup stock
Instructions to make Curry Udon! Japanese style leftover curry recipe!:
  1. Boil the udon in boiling water
  2. At the same time as ①, warm up curry and then put hot watter in curry
  3. After ②, you can put 1 tsp of bonito fish soup stock if you have!
  4. 1-serving udon into curry soup and then mix it up
  5. Ready to eat:)
