The ingredients needed to make Avocado and Crab Stacked Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 FOR CRAB SALAD
  2. Make ready 8 oz jumbo lump crabmeat
  3. Get 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  4. Make ready 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp cajun seasoning
  6. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper and salt to taste
  7. Get 1 tbsp chopped chives
