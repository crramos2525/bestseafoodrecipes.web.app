The ingredients needed to make Mike's Chinese Hot And Sour Soup:

  2. Get 5 Cups Chicken Or Beef Stock
  3. Get 1 1/2 Cups Precooked Shreaded Chicken, Beef Or Pork [packed]
  4. Take 1/4 Cup Bamboo Shoots [drained - thin sliced]
  5. Make ready 1/4 Cup Water Chestnuts [drained - minced]
  6. Make ready 1/4 Cup Shiitake Mushrooms [small chop - or, white & brown]
  7. Get 6 oz Firm Tofu [1/2" chop]
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Fine Minced Fresh Ginger
  9. Get 1 [or 2 - your call] LG Eggs [beaten]

  13. Make ready 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
