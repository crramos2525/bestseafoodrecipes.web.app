The ingredients needed to make Tuna Mousse with Avocados:
  1. Prepare 1 Tin Tuna in Spring Water (Chunks best)
  2. Prepare 1 Large Tbls Mayonnaise
  3. Get 2 Anchovy Fittles
  4. Take 1 Tsp Capers
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp Lemon or Lime Juice
  6. Make ready Salt and Pepper
  7. Take Avocados
  8. Prepare Paprika to Garnish

Steps to make Tuna Mousse with Avocados:
  1. Put everything into a blender or food processor with Blade - pulse until smooth but no puree - little texture is good.
  2. Pour into a bowl and put in the Fridge for 55 min or over night. it will thicken.
  3. Serve over half an avocado with some salad and hot buttered Toast. Sprinkle with a little paprika to garnish.
