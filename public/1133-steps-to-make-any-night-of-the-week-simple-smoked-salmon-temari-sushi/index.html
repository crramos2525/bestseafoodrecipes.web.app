The ingredients needed to make Simple Smoked Salmon Temari Sushi:
  1. Prepare Smoked salmon
  2. Make ready Cooked white rice
  3. Make ready ●Sushi vinegar (grain vinegar)
  4. Make ready ●Sugar
  5. Get ●Salt
Steps to make Simple Smoked Salmon Temari Sushi:
  1. Add the ingredients marked with ● into a bowl of firm cooked rice. Mix well whilst allowing the rice to cool.
  2. Lay a piece of smoked salmon on top of a sheet of cling film.
  3. Add about 1 tablespoon of sushi rice on top of the smoked salmon.
  4. Wrap everything with the cling film and then form into a ball. It's best not to squeeze too tightly. Lightly squeeze the sushi just enough for everything to hold together..
