The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Get 1 lb large devained & cleaned shrimp
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup butter
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp minced garlic
  4. Take 1 tbsp parsley
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup dry white wine
  6. Take 1 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1 tsp pepper
  8. Take 1 tsp red jalapeno in jar
  9. Take 1 tsp dried onion flakes
  10. Get 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
  11. Take 1 lemons for side
  12. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  13. Make ready 1 pinch red pepper flaked
