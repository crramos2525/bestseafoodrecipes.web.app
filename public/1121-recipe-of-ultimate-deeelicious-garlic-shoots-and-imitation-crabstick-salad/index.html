The ingredients needed to make Deeelicious! Garlic Shoots and Imitation Crabstick Salad:
  1. Prepare 2 packs Garlic shoots
  2. Get 1 Imitation crab sticks
  3. Take 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  4. Get 1 Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Deeelicious! Garlic Shoots and Imitation Crabstick Salad:
  1. Chop the garlic shoots into 2 cm pieces, put in a plastic bag and microwave for 1.5 minutes. Gently turn over the bag, and microwave for 1 more minute.
  2. Combine the garlic shoots, shredded crab sticks, mayonnaise, salt and pepper in a bowl, and serve.
