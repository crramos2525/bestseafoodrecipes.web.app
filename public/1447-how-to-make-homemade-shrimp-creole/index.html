The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Creole:
  1. Get 2 lb medium shrimp
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chopped onions (3 medium onions)
  4. Take 2 cup chopped green bell peppers (2 medium)
  5. Prepare 1 cup chopped celery (2 stalks)
  6. Make ready 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
  7. Get 1 cup water
  8. Make ready 2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  9. Take 1 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  11. Prepare 2 dried bay leaves
  12. Get 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  13. Prepare 6 cup hot cooked rice
  14. Get 1 packages chorizo

This southern Louisiana shrimp creole dish is cooked with tomatoes, onions, peppers and celery. Adjust the spice to your preference, but don't be scared of a little heat! Instructions to make Shrimp Creole:
  1. Peel and devein shrimp. Cover and refrigerate.
  2. In 3 quart saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Cook onions, bell peppers, celery and garlic in butter about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until onions are tender. Cook chorizo separately and remove grease.
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients EXCEPT rice and shrimp. Heat to boiling; reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in shrimp. Heat to boiling; reduce heat to medium. Cover and cook 4 to 6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until shrimp are pink and firm. Remove bay leaves. Serve shrimp mixture over rice.
