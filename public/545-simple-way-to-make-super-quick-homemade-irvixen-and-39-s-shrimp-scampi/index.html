The ingredients needed to make Irvixen's Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 lb Frozen, cooked, shelled shrimp
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Butter, cubed
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup Olive oil
  4. Take 6 Garlic cloves, minced
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp Dried parsley
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Lemon zest
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Each, salt and pepper
  8. Get 1 Parmesan cheese, optional
