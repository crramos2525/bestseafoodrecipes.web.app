  2. Take 100 g squid
  3. Prepare 200 g or so of mussels (about 3-4 per person) (optional)

  8. Get Glug of white wine
  9. Get Knob butter
  10. Make ready Olive oil
  11. Take to taste Salt
  12. Make ready Fresh parsley
