The ingredients needed to make Savory Tuna Casserole:
  1. Take 6 oz packet or can of White Albacore Tuna in water
  2. Take 8 oz wide egg noodles
  3. Get 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  4. Prepare 2 cups sharp cheddar, shredded (separated)
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup frozen peas and carrots
  6. Make ready 1/8 cup onion, diced
  7. Take 2 tsp minced garlic
  8. Get 6-7 Ritz crackers, crushed
  9. Take Salt and Pepper (to taste)
Instructions to make Savory Tuna Casserole:
  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse.
  2. Combine tuna, noodles, onion, garlic, peas and carrots, soup, and 1 cup cheddar. Spoon into a small-medium casserole dish.
  3. Crush Ritz crackers and mix with remaining cheddar and spread over top of casserole.
  4. Bake uncovered at 425° for 20 mins. Enjoy!!
