The ingredients needed to make Avocado and Tomato Chirashi Sushi:
  1. Take 2 sevings cooked rice
  2. Take 2 Tbsp vinegar
  3. Prepare 1 Tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 avocado
  6. Make ready 1 tomatoes
  7. Take 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  8. Get 1 Tbsp olive oil
  9. Get 1 pinch salt
  10. Make ready seaweed and sesami for decoration

Instructions to make Avocado and Tomato Chirashi Sushi:
  1. Cut the avocado and tomatoes into small pieces, and put them in to a bowl and pour the lemon juice, olive oil and a pinch of salt over.
  2. Prepare another bowl and put in the cooked rice along with the vinegar, sugar and 1 tsp salt. Mix together gently.
  3. Put the cooked rice into a rice bowl, and put the vegetables on top the rice. You can decorate with sliced seaweed and sesame seeds on top.
