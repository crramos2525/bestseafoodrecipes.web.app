The ingredients needed to make Seafood Chowder:
  1. Make ready 1 Haddock filet, cut into cubes
  2. Get 400 grams Large peeled prawns
  3. Prepare 200 grams Muscle meat ( I used frozen)
  4. Take 400 grams Calamari
  5. Make ready 1 can Condensed Chicken or chicken and mushroom soup
  6. Take 1 can Whole corn
  7. Take 2 tsp Fresh thyme. Finely chopped
  8. Make ready 2 tsp Fresh parsley finely chopped
  9. Get 1 tsp Crushed garlic
  10. Prepare 250 ml Fresh cream
  11. Take 1 Salt
  12. Prepare 1 Black pepper
  13. Take 1/2 Red pepper chopped roughly
  14. Take 1/2 Yellow pepper chopped
  15. Prepare 2 large Carrots. Peeled and chopped
  16. Get 2 medium Potatoes. Peeled and chopped
  17. Take 1/2 packages Bacon chopped
  18. Take 1/2 tsp Dried sage
  19. Get 1 Chili finely chopped
  20. Get 1/2 packages Fresh button mushrooms
