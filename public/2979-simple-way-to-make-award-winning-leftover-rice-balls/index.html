The ingredients needed to make Leftover rice balls:
  1. Prepare 1 cup leftover rice
  2. Make ready 1 cup rice flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 tsp mustard seeds
  5. Take 2 tsp curry leaves
  6. Get 4 green chillies
  7. Take 1 pinch asafoetida
  8. Get 1 tsp lemon juice
  9. Make ready 2 tsp oil
  10. Make ready 2 cups water

Steps to make Leftover rice balls:
  1. In a mixer jar take leftover rice and add salt and two green chillies.add little water and grind it to make a smooth paste.transfer in a plate
  2. Add rice flour in batches to this mixture.knead it and make a semi soft dough.now prepare lemon size balls from the dough.
  3. Take a wok or kadhai add two cups water when water heated then add rice balls boil it on high flame untill they float.now cover the lid and cooked for 3 minutes when they float turn off the flame.transfer the balls in a plate
  4. Take a pan add oil heat it then add asafoetida,mustard seeds and curry leaves saute then add rice balls and toss them for few seconds on low flame.transfer into serving plate squeeze lemon juice and serve it.
