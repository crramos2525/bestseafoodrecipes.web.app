The ingredients needed to make Hot Crab and Swiss Bites:
  1. Prepare 8 oz crab meat
  2. Get 1 cup shredded swiss cheese
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  4. Take 1/4 tsp curry powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
  7. Take 1 baguette, sliced

Instructions to make Hot Crab and Swiss Bites:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly oil a baking sheet.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the crab meat, swiss cheese, mayonnaise, curry powder, salt and lemon juice. Mix well.
  3. Spoon crab meat mixture onto each bread slice. Bake at 400 degrees F for approx. 12 minutes. Serve hot.
