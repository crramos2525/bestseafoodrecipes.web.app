The ingredients needed to make Brad's steak oscar:
  1. Take 2 12 oz rib eye steaks
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  3. Make ready 1/4 lb lump crab meat
  4. Take 1/2 cup prepared hollandaise sauce
  5. Take 1 salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste
  6. Make ready marinade
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup each, soy sauce, worchestershire sauce, balsamic vinegar, water, and red wine vinegar

Steps to make Brad's steak oscar:
  1. Mix all marinade ingredients. Marinade steaks for 30-45 minutes
  2. Place steaks on a broiler pan. Sprinkle with seasonings. Bake at 350 until steak is at desired doneness. I prefer rare to med rare
  3. 5 Minutes before done top steaks with crab meat and cheese. Remove when cheese is melted
  4. Top steaks with hollandaise sauce and serve immediately.
