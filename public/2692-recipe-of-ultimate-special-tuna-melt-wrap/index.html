The ingredients needed to make Special Tuna Melt Wrap:
  1. Make ready Tin flaked tuna
  2. Make ready 1 red Thai chilli
  3. Prepare 3 pickled Green chilli
  4. Make ready 3 cherry tomatoes
  5. Get 1/2 a chopped onion
  6. Take 1 clove garlic
  7. Make ready Vinegar, salt and pepper
  8. Make ready Worcestershire sauce
  9. Make ready 1 x teaspoon of white sugar
  10. Get Mayonnaise
  11. Take 2 x wraps
  12. Get Grated strong cheese
