The ingredients needed to make Shrimp in the oven:
  1. Prepare 2 table spoon soya sauce
  2. Prepare 500 gm large shrimp
  3. Get 3 gloves of garlic or garlic powder
  4. Prepare pinch Italian spices
  5. Make ready pinch coriander powder
  6. Get pinch Cumin powder
  7. Take 2 table spoon lemon juice
  8. Prepare 2 table spoon olive oil
  9. Make ready 3 dried red pepper
  10. Take 2 stick cinnamon
  11. Take pinch crashsed black pepper
  12. Get As you like green onion
  13. Get slice add some lemon

Steps to make Shrimp in the oven:
  1. Clean the shrimp open the back with scissors and devein the shrimp.
  2. Marinate shrimp in the soya sauce, oil,lemon juice and spices.
  3. Add lemon slice, red pepper and put in the oven for 200 degree until it colour change to red
  4. Serve with salad and rice if want.
