The ingredients needed to make Brad's easy tuna salad:
  1. Get 3 cans tuna fish
  2. Get 1/4 of a LG red onion, fine chopped
  3. Make ready 2 celery stalks, fine chopped
  4. Prepare 1 tsp minced garlic
  5. Take 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  6. Take 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  7. Prepare to taste Black pepper
