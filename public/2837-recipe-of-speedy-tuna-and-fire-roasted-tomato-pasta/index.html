The ingredients needed to make Tuna and Fire Roasted Tomato Pasta:
  1. Take 1-16 ounce box Bow Tie Pasta
  2. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons parsley flakes
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Adobo
  5. Take 1/3 cup olive oil
  6. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  7. Take 1-14.5 ounce can fire roasted diced tomatoes
  8. Take 1-5 ounce can tuna fish in water (drained)
  9. Make ready 4 cloves minced garlic
  10. Make ready Parmesan cheese for the topping
