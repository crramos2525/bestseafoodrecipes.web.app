The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Ceviche:
  1. Get 1/2 cup chopped onion
  2. Make ready 6 cups water
  3. Get 3/4 cup fresh lime juice, divided
  4. Make ready 1 pound medium shrimp
  5. Make ready 1 cup chopped peeled cucumber
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup ketchup
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  8. Take 2 Tbsp Mexican hot sauce (such as Tamazula)
  9. Make ready 1 Tbsp olive oil
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Make ready 1 Avocado

Instructions to make Shrimp Ceviche:
  1. Place chopped onion in a colander, and rinse with cold water. Drain
  2. Bring 6 cups water and 1/2 cup juice to a boil in a Dutch oven. Add shrimp; cook 3 minutes or until done. Drain and rinse with cold water; peel shrimp.
  3. Combine shrimp and 1/2 cup juice in a large bowl; cover and chill 1 hour. Stir in onion, cucumber, and remaining ingredients.
  4. Dice avocado into squares using the skin as a cup to dice
  5. Add diced Avocado and Serve immediately or chilled.
