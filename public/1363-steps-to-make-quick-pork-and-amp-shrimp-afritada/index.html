The ingredients needed to make Pork & Shrimp Afritada:
  1. Make ready 2 lbs boneless shoulder pork meat
  2. Take 1 bg frozen peas
  3. Take 1 pkg little smokies
  4. Take 1 bg deveined, tail off uncooked shrimp
  5. Take 3 red potatoes (quartered)
  6. Prepare 1 medium yellow onion
  7. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  8. Take 2 tsp pepper
  9. Get 4 cloves minced garlic
  10. Take 2 tbsp cooking oil
  11. Prepare 1 (26 oz) can tomato sauce
  12. Prepare 3 cups beef broth

Steps to make Pork & Shrimp Afritada:
  1. Start by heating your cooking oil in the bottom of a stew pot to medium heat. Add chopped onion & minced garlic til translucent.
  2. Add cubed pork & uncooked shrimp to pot. Cook til pork is lightly browned & shrimp is lightly pink. So for about 8mins
  3. Once done add the beef broth & tomato sauce. Let boil for 45-60mins.
  4. Once done boiling add potatoes, peas & smokies. Cook for another 10-15 mins. Serve & enjoy!
