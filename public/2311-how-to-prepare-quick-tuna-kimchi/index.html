The ingredients needed to make Tuna kimchi:
  1. Make ready 300 g tuna
  2. Prepare 1 avocado
  3. Prepare 30 g kimchi
  4. Prepare 3 tabls spoons soy sauce
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoons Japanese sweet sake
  6. Get Lemon liquid
