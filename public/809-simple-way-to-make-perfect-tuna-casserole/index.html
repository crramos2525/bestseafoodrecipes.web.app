Steps to make Tuna casserole:
  1. Remove the cheese powder packets from the macaroni and set aside.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the noodles and stir. Cook the noodles for 7-9 minutes. Add the peas during the last 2 minutes that the pasta cooks. Drain.
  3. Return the pasta and peas to the pan and add the butter, milk, cheese powder, Cheddar and tuna. Stir well.
  4. Serve and enjoy. I add salt and pepper and hot sauce to my bowl when making my plate.
