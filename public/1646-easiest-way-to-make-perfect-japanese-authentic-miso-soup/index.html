The ingredients needed to make Japanese Authentic Miso Soup:
  1. Take 500 ml Water
  2. Take 1 handful Katsuobushi(bonito flakes)
  3. Take 1 pack Tofu
  4. Prepare Some Wakame seaweed
  5. Take 2 tablespoons Miso

Steps to make Japanese Authentic Miso Soup:
