The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti Tacos:
  1. Get Spaghetti Noodles
  2. Make ready Spaghetti sauce
  3. Prepare Hamburger meat
  4. Get Taco shells
  5. Make ready Toppings
  6. Prepare Lettuce
  7. Get Tomato
  8. Get Cheese
  9. Get Hot sauce
  10. Get Taco sauce
Instructions to make Spaghetti Tacos:
  1. Brown hamburger meat, and start the spaghetti noodles. Or skip this step if using leftover spaghetti.
  2. Make the spaghetti sauce, and spaghetti, add them together in one big bowl or pot when they are done.
  3. Cook taco shells
  4. Add spaghetti in shell add the toppings or leave plain with no toppings
