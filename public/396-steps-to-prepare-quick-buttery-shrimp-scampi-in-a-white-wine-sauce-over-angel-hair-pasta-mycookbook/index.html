The ingredients needed to make Buttery shrimp scampi in a white wine sauce over angel hair pasta

#mycookbook:

  1. Prepare 1 and a half lbs angel hair pasta (will need two boxes, use one and a half boxes)
  2. Get 16 oz bag of spinach
  3. Make ready 12 oz small shrimp, (bag) raw peeled,deveined,tail off, frozen
  4. Make ready 2 containers sliced mushrooms
  5. Make ready 2 medium white sweet onions
  6. Prepare 1 bottle white wine (use half the bottle) any white wine will work
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoon butter
  8. Make ready 2 tablespoon Olive oil
  9. Take 2 tablespoon or more minced garlic (I used 4 -5 tablespoon)
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon sweet basil
  11. Make ready 2 tablespoon oregano
  12. Get 2 tablespoon kosher salt
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  14. Make ready Parmesan cheese
  15. Take Garlic bread optional
