The ingredients needed to make Seafood pasta salad:
  1. Prepare 1 cup mayonnaise
  2. Prepare 1/2 buttermilk
  3. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp sweet basil
  6. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp spicy mustard
  8. Take 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Tabasco
  10. Make ready 2 cup large pasta shells cooked according to package
  11. Prepare 1 half of small red onion finely diced
  12. Prepare 3 sweet mini peppers finely diced
  13. Take 1 stalks celery finely diced
  14. Prepare 2 stalks green onion finely diced
  15. Make ready 1 tsp old bay seasoning (or more to suit your taste)
  16. Prepare 2 cup shredded imitation crab
  17. Get 2 cup salad shrimp
  18. Make ready 4 boiled eggs chopped
