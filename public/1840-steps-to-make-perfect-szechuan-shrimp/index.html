The ingredients needed to make Szechuan Shrimp:
  1. Get 2 tbsp soy sauce
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp dry sherry
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp cayenne
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 1 lb shelled and deveined shrimp
  8. Make ready 4 cloves chopped garlic
  9. Take 1/2 c sliced green onions
Steps to make Szechuan Shrimp:
  1. Combine soy sauce, sherry, sugar, ginger, and cayenne in a small bowl.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over high heat.
  3. Stir fry the shrimp until pink and firm, about 2 minutes.
  4. Remove shrimp and set aside covered to stay warm.
  5. Add the garlic to the skillet and stir fry for 1 minute.
  6. Add the soy sauce mixture and cook stirring continuously until bubbly.
  7. Return the shrimp to the pan and heat 1 minute more.
  8. Serve with hot cooked fried or plain rice.
