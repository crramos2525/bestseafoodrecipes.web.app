Instructions to make Beef stir fry:
  1. Slice steak
  2. chop garlic
  3. Heat pan spray with non stick spray
  4. Add steak and garlic cook low till meat is brown
  5. Add oyster sauce and sugar let simmer (adjust to your liking )
  6. Add veggies (depending on how you like your veggies that's how long you cook)
  7. Serve with white rice.
