The ingredients needed to make Mediterranian Monkfish:
  1. Get 500 Grams Monkfish
  2. Take 500 Grams Cherry Tomatoes
  3. Make ready 1 White Onion
  4. Prepare 100 Grams Basil
  5. Make ready 1/2 Red Pepper
  6. Make ready 1/2 Green Pepper
  7. Make ready 1 Large Lemon
  8. Get 100 ml Olive Oil
  9. Make ready 1 Small Ciabatta

Steps to make Mediterranian Monkfish:
