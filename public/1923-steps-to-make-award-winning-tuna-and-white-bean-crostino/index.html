The ingredients needed to make Tuna and White Bean Crostino:
  1. Prepare 5 oz Canned Tuna
  2. Get 15 oz Canned White Beans
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  4. Get 1 tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley
  5. Get 1/4 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp Pepper
  7. Take 2 oz Fontina Cheese
