The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Take 1/2 Pound Whole Wheat Linguine
  2. Take 14 Shrimps , deveined and tails on
  3. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Butter
  4. Get 1 1/2 Tablespoons Garlic , minced
  5. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Cilantro Parsley or , chopped
  6. Make ready 2 Pinchs Chilli Flakes
  7. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Lemon Zest
  8. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Lemon Juice
  9. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper , freshly ground
  10. Make ready 1/3 Cup White wine Chicken Stock or
  11. Take To Taste Salt

Instructions to make Shrimp Scampi:
  1. In a pot bring water (enough to cook pasta) to boil. Season the water generously with salt. Throw in the linguine and cook as per package instruction. I always cook for 1-2 minutes less than instructed as the pasta continues to cook once the sauce is added. Drain and keep aside.
  2. In a separate pan, melt the butter on medium heat and fry the garlic till fragrant. Add the shrimps and cook till they turn pink. Be careful not to overcook.
  3. Now carefully take the shrimp out and set aside. To the same pan - there should be plenty butter left - add the lemon zest, lemon juice, chili flakes and chopped cilantro and stir well to incorporate.
  4. Add the wine (or chicken stock) and cook till it reduces a bit. Now add the shrimps back. Give it a quick stir. Pour the sauce over the linguine and toss. Serve warm.
