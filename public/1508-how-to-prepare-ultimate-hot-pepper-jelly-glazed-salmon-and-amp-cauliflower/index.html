The ingredients needed to make Hot Pepper Jelly Glazed Salmon & Cauliflower:
  1. Get 2 salmon filet
  2. Get 3 tbsp hot pepper jelly
  3. Make ready 1 coconut oil
  4. Make ready 1 cauliflower
  5. Make ready 1 salt & pepper
  6. Get 1 dash butter
  7. Make ready tbsp soy sauce
  8. Get tbsp sesame oil
Steps to make Hot Pepper Jelly Glazed Salmon & Cauliflower:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Marinate salmon filets with a mix of hot pepper jelly, sesame oil, soy sauce, and pepper. Leave it in room temperature for 15 minutes.
  3. Butter a baking pan lined with foil and place salmon filets nicely spaced out.
  4. Bake filets for 15 minutes, adding leftover marinate every 6 minutes. Cook until desired.
  5. Add salt to water and bring to a boil.
  6. Add cauliflower and keep boiling until tender.
  7. Serve salmon and cauliflower together for a healthy, and protein-filled meal!
