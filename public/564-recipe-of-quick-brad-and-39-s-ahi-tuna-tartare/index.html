  2. Get 1 tbs soy sauce
  3. Prepare 1 tsp rice wine vinegar
  4. Take 1 tsp olive oil
  5. Take 1/2 tsp white and black sesame seeds
  6. Take 1/2 tsp ground mustard and ginger
  7. Take to taste Sea salt
  8. Take Garnishment
  9. Make ready Crystallized ginger
