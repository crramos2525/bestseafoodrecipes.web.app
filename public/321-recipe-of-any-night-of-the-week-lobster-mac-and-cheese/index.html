The ingredients needed to make lobster mac and cheese:
  1. Make ready 40 oz lobster meat
  2. Prepare 1 lb macaroni elbows cook to instructions on box
  3. Take 1 1/4 cup extra sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  4. Prepare 1 1/4 cup colby jack cheese shredded
  5. Take 2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup margarine
  7. Get 2 tsp granulated garlic powder
  8. Prepare 1 3/4 cup milk
  9. Get 1/4 cup cornstarch
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
