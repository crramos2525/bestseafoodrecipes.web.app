The ingredients needed to make Fried Fresh Salmon w/kale & dandelion greens:
  1. Get Fresh Salmon
  2. Get Fresh dill
  3. Take Bread crumbs
  4. Prepare Old bay seasoning
  5. Prepare Garlic powder
  6. Make ready Bell peppers
  7. Get Green onions
  8. Make ready Bella mushrooms
  9. Take Sea Salt
  10. Prepare Grapeseed oil
  11. Take Dill dipping sauce (healthy version)
  12. Take Coconut milk yogurt unsweetened
  13. Get Fresh dill chopped small
  14. Make ready Small cucumbers chopped small
  15. Get to taste Lime zest
Steps to make Fried Fresh Salmon w/kale & dandelion greens:
  1. Bell peppers, green onions, Bella mushrooms sauteed
  2. Add number 1 to fresh cut up salmon, old bay seasoning, garlic powder, sea salt, panko bread crumbs, and blend together
  3. Form into patties and refrigerate for a hour. Fry on both sides in grapeseed oil
  4. Fresh dill, coconut milk yogurt, dairy free, lime, dipping sauce
