The ingredients needed to make Beef & Potatoes in Oyster Sauce:
  1. Make ready Marinade
  2. Make ready Roughly 300g rump steak beef (or any cut you prefer)
  3. Get 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  6. Take 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  7. Prepare Ingredients
  8. Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  9. Take 1 tbsp sesame oil
  10. Prepare 2 cloves garlic - diced
  11. Make ready 2 large slice of ginger - diced
  12. Get 1/2 yellow onion - wedged
  13. Take 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  14. Prepare Cornstarch slurry
  15. Take Scallions
  16. Make ready 2 large potatoes wedged and pre-cooked

Instructions to make Beef & Potatoes in Oyster Sauce:
  1. Combine beef and marinade ingredients in a bowl, cover and let it sit in the fridge for at least 1 hour
  2. Sautee garlic + ginger until fragrant, add beef.
  3. Cook until beef is brown and you can see caramelized crust round the edges, add potatoes and onion wedges. Add oyster sauce and toss to coat.
  4. Add cornstarch slurry and scallions, toss until scallions slightly wilted and take off heat.
