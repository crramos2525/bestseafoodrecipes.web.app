  2. Meanwhile the mushrooms simmer on low heat. Heat a pot or deepfryer and add the Aubergine üçÜ slice in hot oil until it's beautiful light gold. Remove and dab oil off with paper towel.

  4. Assemble the Burger by starting with the Aubergine, then Rocket, tomatoes, glazed mushrooms then top with peeled half an Avocado and drizzle with seasame seeds.
