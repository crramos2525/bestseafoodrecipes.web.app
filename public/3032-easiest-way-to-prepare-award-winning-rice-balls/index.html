The ingredients needed to make Rice balls:
  1. Get 1 bowl cooked rice
  2. Get 1 onion chopped
  3. Prepare 1 tsp lemon jiuce
  4. Prepare 1 tsp dry mango powder
  5. Take according to taste Salt
  6. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Make ready 1 coriander powder
  8. Take 2-3 green chilli chopped
  9. Get 1 tsp garlic paste
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup gram flour
  11. Get 2 spoon semolina
  12. Prepare 4 boiled potatoes
  13. Take 3 tsp cornflour
  14. Prepare as needed Coriander leaves

Instructions to make Rice balls:
  1. Take boiled rice and mash it with masher, now add potatoes and gram flour and mix well
  2. Onions, green chilli, lemon juice, salt,red chilli, garlic paste,and dry mango powder and mix it well and make a soft dough no need of water.now make a liquid solution if cornflour.
  3. Make a small balls and roll over in cornflour, then in semolina.and leave it for 5 to 7 minutes. Heat a pan with oil for frying the balls.
  4. It becomes golden brown then take out and serve hot with sauce and mayonnaise.
