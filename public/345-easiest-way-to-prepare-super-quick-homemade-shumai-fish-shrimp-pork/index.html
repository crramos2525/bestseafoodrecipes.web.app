The ingredients needed to make Shumai (fish shrimp pork):
  1. Get 250 gr fish meat (tenggiri)
  2. Take 250 gr shrimp (skinless)
  3. Get 50 gr pork minced
  4. Make ready 1 pc egg
  5. Prepare 3 garlic (mince and fry)
  6. Prepare 2 tbs tapioca flour
  7. Take Paper
