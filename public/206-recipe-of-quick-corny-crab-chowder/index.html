The ingredients needed to make Corny Crab Chowder:
  1. Take fresh crab meat or refrigerated canned crab meat
  2. Make ready olive oil
  3. Make ready russet potatoes, peeled and chopped (about 13 oz)
  4. Take small onion, chopped
  5. Make ready low-sodium chicken broth
  6. Take frozen corn
  7. Prepare crushed red pepper
  8. Take dried basil
  9. Get half and half
  10. Prepare salt
  11. Take pepper
  12. Prepare chopped fresh parsley or dried oregano to garnish
Steps to make Corny Crab Chowder:
  1. In large pot, heat oil. Add potatoes and onions. Cook until onion is tender. Add broth and simmer 20 min.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and cook on low 5 min. Serve with garnish if using.
