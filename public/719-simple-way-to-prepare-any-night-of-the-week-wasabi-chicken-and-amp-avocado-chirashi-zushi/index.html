The ingredients needed to make Wasabi Chicken & Avocado Chirashi-zushi:
  1. Make ready <Sushi Rice>
  2. Take (*180ml cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  3. Take Rice Vinegar *5 teaspoons
  4. Prepare Sugar *about 4 teaspoons
  5. Get Salt *about 1/2 teaspoon
  6. Prepare <Toppings>
  7. Get Chicken *This time I used tenderloin fillets
  8. Take Salt & Pepper
  9. Make ready Avocado
  10. Get Soy Sauce
  11. Make ready grated Wasabi *OR Wasabi paste as required
  12. Prepare Cucumber
  13. Take Lettuce Leaves
  14. Make ready Toasted Nori (Seaweed Sheet) *cut into thin strips

Instructions to make Wasabi Chicken & Avocado Chirashi-zushi:
