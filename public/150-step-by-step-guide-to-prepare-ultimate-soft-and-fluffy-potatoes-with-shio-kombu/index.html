The ingredients needed to make Soft and Fluffy Potatoes with Shio-Kombu:
  1. Make ready 2 Potatoes
  2. Prepare 2 pinch Shio-kombu
  3. Take 1 Butter (not necessary)
Instructions to make Soft and Fluffy Potatoes with Shio-Kombu:
  1. Microwave the potatoes until they're soft and fluffy. Peel and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Just mix together the potatoes from Step 1 with the shio-kombu and it's complete. Add butter if you like, for a richer flavor.
