Instructions to make Delicious Celery, Cheese, and Tuna pasta salad:
  1. chop Celery and Cheese into tiny cubes. toss into a mixing bowl. in the mean time, add about half a box of salad macaroni into a pot of boiling water to cook.
  2. open, and drain two cans of Tuna. add into mixing bowl with Celery and Cheese
  3. sprinkle Garlic Salt, Pepper, and dried onion flakes into bowl with the rest of the ingredients.
  4. to the bowl, add 5 big spoon fulls of Mayo and mix ingredients.
  5. take cooked salad macaroni and rinse with cold water and drain.
  6. mix salad macaroni into the bowl if ingredients. lightly toss together till filly mixed.
  7. refrigerate over night or eat on the spot!
  8. Enjoy!
