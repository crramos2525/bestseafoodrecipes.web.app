The ingredients needed to make Cheap & Easy Salmon Tagliatelle:
  1. Prepare 1 X Can of Pink Salmon
  2. Take 1 x Half a Lemon
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon Dried Chilli Flakes
  4. Prepare 1 Bunch Cherry Tomatoes
  5. Get Olive Oil
  6. Take About 35g of Tagliatelle
  7. Take Half a Zucchini Chopped
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Take Pinch Parsley

Instructions to make Cheap & Easy Salmon Tagliatelle:
  1. Boil a Pot of Hot Water, Add Salt, then the Tagliatelle. Cook for 11 mins.
  2. Pre hit a pan with olive oil…. Cut up a Zucchini into Quarters, and then add the Zucchini and Tomatoes to grill
  3. Once Golden add Crushed Garlic, A table spoon of chilli flakes and the full can of Salmon. Cook for about 5 mins.
  4. Once cooked, drain the water and add the Tagliatelle to the pan. Add lemon and a pinch of Parsley. Mix it up. Then Eat it…..! ;)
