The ingredients needed to make My simple easy tuna casserole:
  1. Get 2 box macaroni
  2. Prepare 2 can tuna
  3. Make ready 1 can evaporated milk
  4. Take 1 butter
  5. Take e.v.o.o. olive oil
  6. Get 1 dash salt
  7. Prepare 1 packages Philadelphia cream cheese
