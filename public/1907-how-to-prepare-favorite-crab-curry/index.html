The ingredients needed to make Crab Curry:
  1. Prepare I used about 3.5kg of crab (cleaned and chopped)
  2. Get 2 kg tomatoes (liquidised)
  3. Prepare 1 onion (sliced)
  4. Get 10 green chillies
  5. Get leaf Curry
  6. Make ready Methi seeds (pinch)
  7. Take Jeera seeds
  8. Get Mustard seeds
  9. Prepare Garlic
  10. Prepare 5 Tablespoons Chilli powder (change according to taste)
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon jeera powder
  12. Get 1/2 teaspoon garum masala
  13. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  14. Prepare to taste Salt
  15. Make ready 1 Tablespoon ground ginger and garlic
  16. Take 1/2 teaspoon tamarind (I used the liquid one)

Steps to make Crab Curry:
  1. Fry onions, ginger and garlic chillies, garlic, curry leaf, methi seeds, jeera seeds, mustard seeds in oil.
  2. Once fried, add chilli powder, garum masala, jeera powder, turmeric powder and braise for a few seconds.
  3. Add cleaned crab, mix, lower heat and allow water to come out of crab. Once the water has dried out a bit, add in tamarind and mix.
  4. Then add tomatoes and salt. Mix and allow to cook on medium heat. Once chutney thickens, remove from stove. - - Enjoy
