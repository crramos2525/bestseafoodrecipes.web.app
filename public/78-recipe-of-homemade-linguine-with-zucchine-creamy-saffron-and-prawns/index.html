  2. Take 1 small courgette
  3. Make ready Small chopped onion
  4. Get 150 of cooked prawns fresh or frozen
  5. Take 4 large prawns shell on - optional
  6. Get Saffron
  7. Prepare Glug of white wine
  8. Get 2 Tablespoons single cream
  9. Take to taste Salt
  10. Get Olive oil
  11. Take Fresh parsley
