The ingredients needed to make Simple tuna salad:
  1. Get salad
  2. Get 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  3. Take 1 tbsp sweet relish salad cubes
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp yellow prepared mustard
  5. Get 1/3 cup pimento stuffed olives
  6. Take 10 oz tuna
  7. Get 1/4 tsp chopped parsley
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp finely chopped onion
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp onion powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  11. Prepare topping
  12. Get 1 ground black pepper, fresh
  13. Take 1 cilantro
  14. Make ready 1/4 tsp paprika
