The ingredients needed to make Okonomiyaki:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Flour
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup Water or Chicken Broth
  3. Take 2 Eggs
  4. Get 4 cups Cabbage
  5. Take 2 stalks Green Onion
  6. Take 4 slices Bacon (optional)
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Shrimp (optional)
  8. Prepare Mayo/Okonomiyaki sauce to garnish

Instructions to make Okonomiyaki:
  1. Whisk together flour and water (or broth) until smooth.
  2. Add eggs, cabbage, onions and optional ingredients
  3. Pour half the batter into a hot oiled pan. Cook for approx. 3 minutes then flip when bottom begins to brown and cook other side until brown.
  4. Garnish with mayonnaise and okonomiyaki sauce.
