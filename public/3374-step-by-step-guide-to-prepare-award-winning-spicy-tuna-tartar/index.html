The ingredients needed to make Spicy Tuna Tartar:
  1. Make ready 8 oz tuna
  2. Prepare 2 roasted red peppers (or 1/2 of a can roasted red peppers)
  3. Make ready 1 oz Sriracha
  4. Get to taste salt and pepper
  5. Make ready Juice of 1 lime
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Sesame oil (optional)
  7. Get Tbsp chopped cilantro
