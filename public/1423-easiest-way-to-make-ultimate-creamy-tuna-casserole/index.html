The ingredients needed to make Creamy Tuna casserole:
  1. Make ready 3 (6 oz) cans of tuna fish, halfway drained
  2. Make ready 1 package bowtie pasta
  3. Get 3 small red onions
  4. Get 1 can peas, drained
  5. Take 1 can corn, drained
  6. Get 2 (10.75 oz) cans cream of mushroom soup
  7. Take 1 (10.75 oz) can cream of chicken soup
  8. Take 1/2 cup milk
  9. Make ready 2 cups shredded black jack cheddar cheese
  10. Take 2 cups crushed, regular potato chips

Steps to make Creamy Tuna casserole:
  1. Cook the pasta as suggested on the package, drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  3. In a large bowl, mix together tuna (with remaining liquid), soups, corn, peas, onion, cheese, milk, and cooked pasta. Mix well.
  4. Place mixture, evenly, in a 9x13 inch baking dish
  5. Top with potato chip crumbs
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Enjoy…
