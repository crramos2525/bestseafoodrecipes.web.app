The ingredients needed to make Crawfish soup:
  1. Take 1 pk large jumbo shrimp shell on or off(devein)
  2. Make ready 1 pk crawfish (1lb)
  3. Get 1 pk coconut milk or can cream optional
  4. Take 1 pk flavored soup mix (I used grace cock soup)
  5. Prepare 1 small pumpkin or calabaza
  6. Take 3 large white potatoes diced
  7. Get 3 cups chicken broth
  8. Make ready 2 cups water
  9. Prepare 1 large carrot diced
  10. Get 1 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  12. Get 1 tsp oregano
