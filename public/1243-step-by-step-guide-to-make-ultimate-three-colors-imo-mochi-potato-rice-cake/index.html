Steps to make Three colors Imo-mochi (Potato rice cake):
  1. Boil potatoes until softened.
  2. While still hot, mash them up.
  3. Put in potato starch and mash with potatoes.
  4. Divide potatoes into 3 portions.
  5. white): Form into small, round dumplings as is.
  6. purple): Mix in cheese. I also mixed in some sweet potato powder to throw in something extra and give color. Form into rounds like a dumplings.
  7. green): Mix in green laver (ao-nori) and form into dumplings.
  8. Heat a little vegetable oil in a frying pan and fry surface of the dumplings
