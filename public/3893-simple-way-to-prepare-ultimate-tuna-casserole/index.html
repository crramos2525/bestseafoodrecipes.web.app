The ingredients needed to make Tuna Casserole:
  1. Prepare 2 can Tuna fish
  2. Get 1 can cream mushroom soup
  3. Take 1 box noodles (any kind really)
  4. Prepare 3/4 can milk (refill soup can to measure)
  5. Make ready 1 garlic salt
  6. Take 1 seasoning salt
  7. Prepare 1 scoop of chopped garlic
  8. Get 1 dash of real bacon bits
  9. Make ready 2 slice pepperjack cheese
  10. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheese
  11. Prepare 1 small bag of potato chips for topping
  12. Make ready 3 small green onions

Steps to make Tuna Casserole:
  1. Boil noodles until tender (8 to 12 min)
  2. While noodles are boiling, mix together in microwaveable bowl : drained tuna, soup, milk, seasoning, torn up pepperjack cheese, & seasonings. cover, then microwave for roughly 2min or until warm throughout.
  3. Drain noodles, place in 13x9 pan. Pour tuna mixture in & mix together. Sprinkle with shredded cheese & green onions.
  4. Crunch up potato chips, and sprinkle a layer on top of whole casserole. Bake on 350 for about 8 to 10min.
  5. I serve with homeade garlic bread!
