The ingredients needed to make Chicken Pot Pie:
  1. Prepare Chicken
  2. Prepare 2 can cream of chicken soup
  3. Make ready 1 can mushrooms drained
  4. Take 1 can green peas drained
  5. Take 1 can sliced carrots drained
  6. Prepare 1 can sliced potatoes drained
  7. Prepare 1/2 chopped onion
  8. Prepare 2 can chicken breast drained
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  10. Prepare 1 salt
  11. Get 1 pepper
  12. Get 1 celery or celery flakes
  13. Get 1 packages of 2 pie shells in pie pan
  14. Get 1 packages of 2 flat pie crusts toppers
