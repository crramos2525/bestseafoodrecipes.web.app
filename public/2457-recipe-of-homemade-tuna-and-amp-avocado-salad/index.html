The ingredients needed to make Tuna & Avocado Salad:
  1. Get 1 large Avocado cut in half
  2. Get 1 1/3 cup chopped carrots
  3. Take 1 1/3 cup cucumber chopped in small pieces
  4. Take 1 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes chopped in small pieces
  5. Make ready 1 1/4 cup red onion chopped finely
  6. Prepare 1 cup shredded can tuna in oil
  7. Prepare 1 salt & pepper to taste
  8. Prepare 1 full lemon
