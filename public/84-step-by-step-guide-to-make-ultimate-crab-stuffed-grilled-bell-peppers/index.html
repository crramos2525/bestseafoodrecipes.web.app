The ingredients needed to make Crab-Stuffed Grilled Bell Peppers:
  1. Take 12 mini sweet bell peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
  2. Prepare 1/4 c finely chopped celery
  3. Prepare 1-1/2 tsp grated lemon rind
  4. Make ready 2 T fresh lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Take 8 oz lump crab meat, shell pieces removed
  8. Take 3 oz whipped cream cheese, softened
  9. Get 1 T minced fresh chives

Steps to make Crab-Stuffed Grilled Bell Peppers:
  1. Preheat grill to high heat.
  2. Coat grill rack with cooking spray. Arrange peppers cut sides up on grill rack, cover and cook 4 min. or until well marked.
  3. In a bowl, combine all ingredients except chives; mix well. Spoon mixture into pepper halves. Sprinkle with chives.
