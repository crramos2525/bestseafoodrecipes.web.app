The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Pasta Pomodoro - Level III:
  1. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Get 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  3. Take 2 tbsp fat-free chicken broth, fat free
  4. Get 1/2 cup tomato, peeled and diced
  5. Make ready 2 oz whole wheat pasta
  6. Take 12 oz shrimp, uncooked
  7. Take 2 tbsp basil leaves, chopped
Instructions to make Shrimp Pasta Pomodoro - Level III:
  1. Bring large pot of water to boil for pasta.
  2. Meanwhile, place olive oil and garlic in a large sauté pan and sauté for several minutes until lightly browned.
  3. Add chicken broth and tomatoes to pan; simmer for 5 to 10 minutes or until tender.
  4. Cook the pasta according to the difference on the package. Drain.
  5. Add shrimp to pan and cook for several more minutes, until shrimp are opaque. Add basil and toss shrimp mixture with cooked pasta.
