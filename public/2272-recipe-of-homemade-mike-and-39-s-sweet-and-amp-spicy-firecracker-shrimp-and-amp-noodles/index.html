Instructions to make Mike's Sweet & Spicy Firecracker Shrimp & Noodles:
  1. Separate your hard vegetables from your soft.
  2. Add all hard vegetables to pan and sauce first. Fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Then add your soft vegetables and fry for 1 minute longer.
  4. Add shrimp and fry only until it's heated.
  5. Boil your Udon Noodles and add some sweet chilli sauce. Or, prepare your rice as per manufacturer's directions.
  6. Enjoy!
