The ingredients needed to make Crabs:
  1. Take 2 kg crabs
  2. Prepare 3 coconut milk
  3. Prepare 1 onion
  4. Make ready 2 tomato
  5. Take 1 tbs oil
  6. Get 2 tablespoons ground chicken stock
