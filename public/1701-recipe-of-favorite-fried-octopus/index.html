  1. Make ready 0.5 lb boiled octopus
  2. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Get 1 tbsp sake
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp potato starch
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp all purpose flour
  6. Make ready Appropriate amount canola oil

Instructions to make Fried octopus:
  1. Mix soy sauce and sake in a plastic bag. Cut octopus in a bite size and add into the plastic bag. Marinate for 10 mins ~ over night.
  2. Take marinated octopus out of the plastic bag and remove as much as liquid. Mix potato starch and flour in a separate plastic bag. Add octopus and coat them evenly.
  3. Add canola oil in a pod as required and fry them until outside is crispy.
