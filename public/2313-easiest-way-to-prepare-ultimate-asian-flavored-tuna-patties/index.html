The ingredients needed to make Asian Flavored Tuna Patties:
  1. Take 1 can tuna (6oz), drained and fluffed
  2. Take 1/3 cup chopped green onion
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Korean BBQ Sauce
  4. Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  5. Get 1 eggs beaten
  6. Take 1/2 cup Japanese Panko
  7. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper or to taste
  8. Take 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Sriracha hot sauce optional
