The ingredients needed to make Full flavor Low carb tuna patties:
  1. Get 10 ounces canned tuna fish, packed in spring water drained
  2. Take 1 large egg
  3. Prepare 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
  4. Prepare 1/3 teaspoon paprika
  5. Take 1/3 teaspoon dill weed
  6. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil to pan fry
  8. Make ready to taste hot sauce, optional
  9. Prepare to taste London Pub malt vinegar, optional
  10. Make ready to taste red wine vinegar, optional

Instructions to make Full flavor Low carb tuna patties:
  1. In a pan heat oil
  2. Put the tuna, dill weed, paprika, pepper, egg, and onion.
  3. Mix them together
  4. Form into patties and fry. Get a very good crust on bottom before your first flip.
  5. Fry till crunchy
  6. Takes about 15 minutes total to fry. Let sit 2 minutes and serve. I like the vinegars, especially the malt vinegar. However you try them I hope you enjoy!
