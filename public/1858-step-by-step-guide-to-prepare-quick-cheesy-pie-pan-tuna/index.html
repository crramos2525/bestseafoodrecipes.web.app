The ingredients needed to make Cheesy pie pan tuna:
  1. Prepare crust
  2. Make ready 20 oz canned tuna
  3. Prepare 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  4. Take 1/4 cup arrowroot powder
  5. Get 2 large eggs
  6. Get filling
  7. Make ready 2 cup shredded extra sharp cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready 3/4 cup grape tomatoes
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cup black olives ripened
  10. Take coating of the pan
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp margarine

Steps to make Cheesy pie pan tuna:
  1. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit. Coat the pie pan with margarine.
  2. Mix the crust ingredients. Tuna, parmesan cheese,arrowroot powder, and eggs. Add to pie pan and press into bottom and sides.
  3. Slice the olives and tomatoes in half and add to top of crust. Shred the cheddar cheese add on top. Put into oven 15-20 minutes. Leave in for 25-30 minutes for a crunchy crust.
