Steps to make Coffee Jelly:
  1. Combine agar-agar, water, coffee, and sugar in a pot.
  2. Use medium heat and stir until the sugar and agar-agar powder dissolve.
  3. Turn off the heat when it's boiling. Pour into mold.
  4. When it has cooled, cut into cubes and serve with condensed milk.
