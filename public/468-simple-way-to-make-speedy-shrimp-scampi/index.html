The ingredients needed to make shrimp scampi:
  1. Make ready 1/2 clove garlic
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup onion
  3. Get 1/2 bunch cooked shrimp
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup parsley
  5. Make ready 1/3 butter
  6. Prepare 1/4 dash Basel
