  2. Prepare 1 lemon, rind and juice
  3. Get 1 can Tuna, drained
  4. Make ready 12 grams Fresh parsley, finely chopped
  5. Prepare 1 clove garlic, crushed
  6. Make ready 1 dash Olive oil
