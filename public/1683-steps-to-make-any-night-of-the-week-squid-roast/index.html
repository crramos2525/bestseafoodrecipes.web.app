The ingredients needed to make Squid Roast:
  1. Take 250 gm squid
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Make ready 5-6 shallots
  4. Get 1 tsp ginger
  5. Take 3-4 green chillies
  6. Take 1 tomato
  7. Prepare 2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam masala
  10. Make ready As needed Coconut
  11. Take to taste Salt
  12. Prepare 2 tsp Curry leaves
  13. Take As needed Coconut oil

Instructions to make Squid Roast:
  1. Clean the squid and cut them into small square shapes or cut them circularly.
  2. In a pan saute ginger and green chillies,add onions and shallots saute till they r translucent.
  3. To this add chilli powder and turmeric.saute till it's raw smell goes.to this add coconut pieces and tomatoes and cook them well.
  4. Now add squid, enough salt,garam masala and mix everything well.cook the mixture either in a pressure cooker for 3 to 4 whistles or cover the pan and cook till the squid is done.
  5. Add curry leaves and serve hot with rice or chapathis
