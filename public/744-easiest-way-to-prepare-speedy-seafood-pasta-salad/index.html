The ingredients needed to make Seafood pasta salad:
  1. Get 1 cup mayonnaise
  2. Make ready 1/2 buttermilk
  3. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Get 1/2 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp sweet basil
  6. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp spicy mustard
  8. Get 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  9. Take 1 tbsp Tabasco
  10. Make ready 2 cup large pasta shells cooked according to package
  11. Take 1 half of small red onion finely diced
  12. Take 3 sweet mini peppers finely diced
  13. Take 1 stalks celery finely diced
  14. Take 2 stalks green onion finely diced
  15. Prepare 1 tsp old bay seasoning (or more to suit your taste)
  16. Make ready 2 cup shredded imitation crab
  17. Take 2 cup salad shrimp
  18. Take 4 boiled eggs chopped
