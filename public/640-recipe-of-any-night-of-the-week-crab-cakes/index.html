The ingredients needed to make crab cakes:
  1. Make ready 2 cup crab meat
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  3. Get 1 small egg, beaten
  4. Prepare 1 cup bread crumbs, divided
  5. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Get 1 dash pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. Take 1 tbsp butter

Instructions to make crab cakes:
  1. mix crab meat, mayo, 1/4 c. bread crumbs, salt, pepper, and about half of the beaten egg with fork.
  2. spread remaining bread crumbs on a plate. heat butter and olive oil in a saute pan over medium.
  3. form crab mixture into 6-8 patties and dredge in remaining 3/4 c. bread crumbs.
  4. when pan is hot, place crab cakes in pan and cook until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side.
