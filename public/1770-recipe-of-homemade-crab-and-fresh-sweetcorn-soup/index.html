The ingredients needed to make Crab and Fresh Sweetcorn Soup:
  1. Take 300 Gr Fresh Sweetcorn - removed from Cob - if you have to can use Tinned
  2. Prepare 1 Tbls Olive Oil
  3. Take 3 Spring Onions - really finely chopped
  4. Get 2.5 cm fresh Ginger - chopped into Matchsticks
  5. Make ready 250 Gr Crab meat - as lumpy and whole as possible
  6. Make ready 1 Ltr Chicken Stock
  7. Get 1 Tsp Corn Flour (mix with 4 tbsp of the chicken stock to form a paste)
  8. Take 1 Large Egg
  9. Make ready 1 Tbls Sesame Seeds
  10. Make ready 1 Red Chilli very finely chopped
