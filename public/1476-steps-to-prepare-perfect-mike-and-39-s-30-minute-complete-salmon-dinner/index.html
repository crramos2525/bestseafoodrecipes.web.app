The ingredients needed to make Mike's 30 Minute Complete Salmon Dinner:
  1. Get ● For The Seafood
  2. Take 1.5 Pounds Fresh Salmon
  3. Make ready as needed Garlic Olive Oil [to coat salmon]
  4. Take 1 Fresh Lemon Juiced [divided + reserve slices for serving]
  5. Get 1 tbsp Fresh Chopped Dill Weed [+ reserves for garish]
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Granulated Garlic Powder
  7. Take 1/2 Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  8. Get to taste Sea Salt
  9. Make ready ● For The Asparagus
  10. Prepare 1 LG Bunch Fresh Asparagus [2" of bottoms cut & discarded]
  11. Get as needed Garlic Olive Oil [to lightly coat asparagus]
  12. Make ready 1 Fresh Lemon Juiced
  13. Make ready 1/4 Cup Fresh Grated Parmesan
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  15. Prepare to taste Coarse Sea Salt
  16. Make ready ● For The Side Salads
  17. Make ready 1 Bag Mixed Lettuce Leaves
  18. Take as needed Crumbled Feta Cheese
  19. Take as needed Chopped Cucumbers
  20. Make ready as needed Chopped Radishes
  21. Make ready to taste Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  22. Prepare as needed Homemade Ranch Dressing [mayo & while milk needed]
  23. Get ● For The Breads [as needed]
  24. Get Fresh Warm French Bread Or Dinner Rolls
  25. Get Soft Honey Or Salted Creamed Butter
  26. Prepare ● For The Sides
  27. Take Lemon Wedges
  28. Take Hollandaise Sauce [one package]
Steps to make Mike's 30 Minute Complete Salmon Dinner:
  1. Make your side salads.
  2. Use only a quality Parmesan to shred for your asparagus if possible.
  3. Season your disposable pans with garlic olive oil. Add seasonings listed above.
  4. Add rinsed and pat dry salmon to pan. Coat with garlic olive oil. Then, season again.
  5. Do the same with your asparagus. But with Parmesan Cheese.
  6. Baked at 375° for 30 minutes. Internal temp of fish must be at least 145°. Turn your asparagus once while baking.
  7. Quick mix your side salad.
  8. Serve with warm crispy bread and honey butter. Enjoy!
