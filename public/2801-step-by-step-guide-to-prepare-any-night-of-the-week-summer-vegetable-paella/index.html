The ingredients needed to make Summer Vegetable Paella:
  1. Prepare 1 packet paella rice
  2. Take 1 onion and 2 shallots
  3. Get 1 teaspoon paprika
  4. Prepare Mixed tomatoes (2 large)
  5. Take Summer veg of your choice - peas, beans, asparagus
  6. Prepare Mushrooms - prefer chestnut in this but I used oyster
  7. Prepare Saffron or turmeric
  8. Get 1 Lemon
  9. Make ready 1 bunch parsley
  10. Make ready Tabasco (red or green)
