The ingredients needed to make Seafood Boil:
  1. Prepare 12 Blue crabs
  2. Make ready 6 snow crab clusters
  3. Get 2 lbs jumbo shrimp
  4. Take 2 lbs green lip mussels
  5. Make ready 2 sticks butter- melted separate
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup old bay seasoning + extra for topping
  7. Prepare 16 oz can beer
  8. Get 8 potatoes- quartered
  9. Get 5 whole corn on cob - halved
  10. Get 4 links smoked sausage- Rogerwood is the best!
  11. Take 1 clove garlic-minced

Instructions to make Seafood Boil:
  1. Add large stock pot and fill 3/4 of the pot with water. Allow to come to a rolling boil and add 1/2 old bay and stir. Next add blue crab first followed by remainder of seafood. Add more Old Bay Seasoning with every other layer of seafood
  2. Add corn and sausage on top. Pour in beer and allow to boil til potatoes are soft.
  3. Pull items from pot and place in a dish or pot large enough to hold everything. Next take minced garlic and place into melted butter. Pour over all the seafood, corn potatoes and sausage. Sprinkle on a bit more old bay over top!
  4. Enjoy with a bowl of butter to dip seafood
