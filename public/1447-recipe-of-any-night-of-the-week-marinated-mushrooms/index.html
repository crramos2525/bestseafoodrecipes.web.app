Steps to make Marinated Mushrooms:
  1. Cut the stems off of the mushrooms. Divide the mushrooms into bite-size pieces (shred your hands or cut).
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add Step 1 and quickly blanch. Drain.
  3. When mushrooms from (2) cool off, transfer to a container. Add the vegetable oil, salt and pepper and mix to make the marinade.
  4. Store in the refrigerator.
